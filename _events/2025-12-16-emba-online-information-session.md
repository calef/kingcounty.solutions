Join us for the EMBA Online Information Session on Dec. 16, 2025, at 12 p.m. PST. This engaging webinar offers prospective students a chance to learn about the Executive MBA program, including curriculum details, admission requirements, and career outcomes. Attendees will have the opportunity to ask questions and gain insights from faculty and current students. Whether you're considering furthering your education or looking to advance your career, this session will provide valuable information to help you make informed decisions. Don't miss this chance to explore the possibilities of an EMBA!

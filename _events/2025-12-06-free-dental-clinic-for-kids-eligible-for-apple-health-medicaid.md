Join us for a Free Dental Clinic for Kids eligible for Apple Health/Medicaid on December 6, 2025, from 8:00 AM to 2:00 PM at the Skyway Resource Center, located at 12610 76th Ave S, Seattle, WA. This event offers children free dental checkups, cleanings, fluoride treatments, and referrals for any necessary follow-up care. It aims to provide families with accessible preventive dental care in a welcoming community setting. Appointments are encouraged, so please call 1-800-756-5437 to schedule or learn more about the services available. If your child is eligible for Apple Health/Medicaid and lacks a dental provider, our Access to Baby and Child Dentistry program can assist you in finding one. Don’t miss this opportunity to ensure your child's dental health!

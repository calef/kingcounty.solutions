This weekly gathering invites creative individuals of all ages to work on personal projects while connecting with fellow art enthusiasts. Bring your own materials and unleash your creativity in a supportive environment. Whether you're a seasoned artist or just starting out, you'll find inspiration and encouragement among like-minded participants.

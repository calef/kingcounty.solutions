Join NAMI Eastside for "Creative Minds: Art for Mental Wellness" on December 9 from 6:00 PM to 8:30 PM at the Together Conference Center in Redmond. This monthly event invites participants to explore mental health topics through hands-on art activities and guided discussions. The first session will focus on anxiety, providing a supportive environment for self-expression and connection. No prior art experience is required—just come as you are and engage in creative activities designed to promote emotional regulation and relaxation. Attendees will also have access to valuable mental health resources. Registration is required, and a consent form is necessary for participants under 18. Don't miss this opportunity to express yourself and connect with others in a welcoming space!

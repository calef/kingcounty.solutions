Join us for "Strategies for Success 2: Health & Well-Being" at WorkSource Seattle-King County in Rainier, starting on December 8, 2025, from 9:00 AM to 12:00 PM. This engaging workshop series focuses on achieving work-life balance and personal well-being. Attendees will explore essential topics, including self-care strategies, boundary setting, stress management, and accessing community resources. Participants will also enhance their networking skills and problem-solving abilities, all while examining their core values and life choices. This flexible program allows for a personalized experience, with modules that can stand alone or be combined. Don't miss this opportunity to invest in your health and well-being!

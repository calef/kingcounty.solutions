Join us for The Chef's Holiday Table on December 5th from 5:00 PM to 6:00 PM at the South Park Neighborhood Center, located at 8201 10th Ave S, Seattle, WA. Seating is limited, so be sure to make a reservation by contacting Andrew at 206-909-0346 or via email. Don’t miss this opportunity to celebrate the season with delicious food and community spirit!

Join us for the Sight Tech Global Virtual Conference, taking place from December 9 to 10, 2025. This free, fully virtual event is dedicated to exploring how advancements in AI and technology can enhance accessibility for individuals with vision loss. Attendees can expect engaging panels, live demos, and one-on-one discussions featuring leading technologists, researchers, and advocates in the field of assistive technology. Each session will include insights from people who are blind or visually impaired, ensuring a comprehensive perspective on the challenges and innovations in accessibility. The conference will also feature expert moderators from prominent organizations. Don't miss this opportunity to be part of a global dialogue shaping a more accessible future!

Join us at Johnny Rockets in Kent from Dec. 15 to Dec. 21 for a special event where 20% of sales will support the Domestic Abuse Women’s Network. This is a great opportunity to enjoy delicious food while contributing to a worthy cause. Whether you're dining in or taking out, your purchase will help raise awareness and funds for advocacy and support for those affected by domestic abuse. Located at 418 Ramsay Way, Johnny Rockets invites all self-identifying DAWN supporters to participate and make a difference in the community. Don't miss out on this chance to eat well and support a vital cause!

Join us for a delightful dinner featuring sticky Asian chicken on Dec. 19 from 5 to 6 p.m. at the South Park Neighborhood Center, located at 8201 10th Ave. S, Seattle, WA. Enjoy a delicious meal that includes chicken with wheat noodles, sautéed vegetables, a mixed green salad, and fresh fruit. This event is a great opportunity to socialize and savor a tasty dinner with friends and neighbors. Please note that seating is limited, and reservations are required. Contact Andrew at andrew@spseniors.org or call 206-909-0346 to secure your spot. Don't miss out on this flavorful evening!

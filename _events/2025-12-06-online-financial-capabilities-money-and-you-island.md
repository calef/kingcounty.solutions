Join the online workshop "Financial Capabilities: Money and You" on Dec. 6, 2025, from 9 to 11 a.m. This engaging two-day series, hosted by WorkSource Seattle-King County, focuses on empowering participants to explore their relationship with money, including understanding sources and timing of income. The workshop is designed to help attendees make informed financial decisions and is flexible, allowing for a personalized experience. Each unit can be taken independently or combined with others. Participants must register in advance, and the Zoom meeting link will be shared via email before the event. Don't miss this opportunity to enhance your financial literacy and confidence!

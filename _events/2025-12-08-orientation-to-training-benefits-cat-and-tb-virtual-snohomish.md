Join WorkSource Seattle-King County for a virtual workshop, "Orientation to Training Benefits: CAT and TB," on Dec. 8, 2025, from 2 p.m. to 4 p.m. This session is designed for individuals receiving Unemployment Insurance (UI) benefits who plan to enroll in a full-time training program. Attendees will gain essential insights into maintaining UI eligibility while pursuing education and will learn how to complete the necessary applications for Commissioner Approved Training and Training Benefits.

Participants will receive a Zoom link and meeting details via email one day before the event. Registration is required, and attendees are encouraged to create an account at WorkSourceWA.com prior to the workshop. This informative session is crucial for anyone looking to navigate the intersection of education and unemployment benefits effectively.

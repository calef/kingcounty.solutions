Join the Rainier Symphony for a delightful holiday concert on December 14, 2025, from 3:00 PM to 5:00 PM at Foster High’s Performing Arts Center in Tukwila. Attendees can expect a festive program featuring beloved seasonal classics, including 'Twas the Night Before Christmas, Engelbert Humperdinck's Prelude to Hansel and Gretel, and Leroy Anderson's Sleigh Ride. The event will also showcase performances by talented students from the Foster High School Concert Choir, adding a special community touch to the celebration. Don't miss this opportunity to enjoy an afternoon of beautiful music and holiday cheer!

Join us for the MSIM International Q&A Information Session on December 15, 2025, at 8:30 AM PST via Zoom. This event offers a unique opportunity for prospective students to engage directly with Nishit Bhasin, a recent graduate of the Master of Science in Information Management program. Attendees can expect insightful discussions about the challenges and rewards of entrepreneurship, as well as guidance on developing a startup from the ground up. Whether you're considering the program or simply curious about the entrepreneurial journey, this session is designed to provide valuable information and answer your questions. Don't miss this chance to gain insights from someone who has successfully navigated the path you're interested in!

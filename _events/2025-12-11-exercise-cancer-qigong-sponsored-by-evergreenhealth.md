Join us online on December 11 from 11:30 AM to 1:00 PM PST for "Exercise & Cancer: Qigong," sponsored by EvergreenHealth. This session offers a gentle, flowing practice that combines movement, breath, and mindfulness to enhance overall well-being. Participants can expect to increase their energy levels, improve mental clarity, and promote emotional balance through Qigong, making it suitable for all ability levels—whether you're a beginner or experienced practitioner. Come as you are and experience the healing benefits of this ancient practice in a supportive environment.

Join us for Drop-in Digital Navigation on Dec. 11, 2025, at The Center for Active Living, located at 4217 SW Oregon St., Seattle. From 11:30 a.m. to 3 p.m., attendees can receive personalized assistance with their cell phones, tablets, or personal computers in our dedicated computer lab. Each session lasts 25 minutes, and no appointment is necessary—just come by with your device and your questions. A suggested donation of $5 is appreciated. To confirm the computer lab's availability for the day, please call 206-932-4044, ext. 114, before your visit. Whether you're a tech novice or looking to sharpen your skills, this event is a great opportunity to enhance your digital literacy.

Join us for "Get Moving!" on Dec. 16, 2025, from 9:30 to 10:30 a.m. at The Center for Active Living, located at 4217 SW Oregon St., Seattle, WA. This engaging workout class, led by physical therapist Sherryl DeVries, is designed for all fitness levels, particularly those with a Parkinson’s diagnosis. Attendees can expect a dynamic mix of exercises, including big movements, cardio, weight training, dance, and boxing. Each activity can be modified for those who prefer to use a chair. Come ready to have fun and enhance your physical well-being in a supportive environment!

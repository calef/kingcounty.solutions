Join us at Westfield Southcenter Mall on December 5 from 6 PM to 8 PM for the Holiday Sing-Along Piano Show. Kick off the festive season with a lively Dueling Piano Caroling Sing-Along, where you can enjoy live performances of your favorite holiday songs. Sip on hot cocoa as you celebrate the spirit of community through music and cheer. Bring your family, friends, and your best singing voice for an evening filled with joy and merriment. Don’t miss this chance to create lasting holiday memories!

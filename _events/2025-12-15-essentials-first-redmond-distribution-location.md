Join us on Dec. 15 from 10 a.m. to 2 p.m. at the Essentials First Redmond Distribution Location in Washington for a community event focused on providing essential hygiene access. Attendees can walk in to receive hygiene kits, snack packs, and ORCA cards, ensuring vital support for those in need. This event is open to everyone, particularly low-income families, unhoused individuals, immigrants, and others facing hardships. No strict eligibility requirements are in place, but please bring an ID and be ready to complete a brief intake form with the assistance of Essentials First staff. Your personal information will be kept confidential. Come and help us make a difference in our community!

Join the Low Vision Group on December 9, 2025, from 2:00 PM to 3:00 PM at The Center for Active Living, located at 4217 SW Oregon St., Seattle, WA. This supportive gathering invites individuals facing low vision challenges to share their experiences and access valuable resources. Attendees can expect a welcoming environment where they can discuss their struggles and receive guidance from peers and professionals. Please note that pre-registration is required to attend; contact social worker Josh Maduell at 206-932-4044, ext. 2, or via email at joshm@wscenter.org to secure your spot. Don’t miss this opportunity to connect and find support in your journey.

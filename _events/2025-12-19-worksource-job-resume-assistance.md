Join us at the Tukwila Library on December 19 from 10 a.m. to noon for the Worksource Job & Resume Assistance event. Attendees can receive help with resume writing, interview preparation, and job search strategies, including information about upcoming job fairs and career options. This program is designed for adults and teens, ages 13 to 17. Registration is not required, and reasonable accommodations for individuals with disabilities can be requested by emailing access@kcls.org at least seven days in advance. Automated closed captioning will be available for online participants. Don't miss this opportunity to invest in your future!

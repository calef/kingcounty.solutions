Join us online on Dec. 9, 2025, for an informative session on "Oregon Psilocybin Services: New Health and Safety Data." Attendees will gain insights into the latest research and developments surrounding psilocybin, focusing on health and safety implications. This event is an excellent opportunity for individuals interested in the evolving landscape of psilocybin services in Oregon, as well as for health care professionals seeking to understand the impact of these services on public health. Engage with experts and participate in discussions that aim to enhance knowledge and awareness in this emerging field. Don't miss this chance to stay informed and connect with others in the community.

Join the Normandy Park Arts Commission for the 3rd annual Solstice Soiree on Saturday, December 13th, from 6 PM to 9 PM at The Cove, located at 1500 SW Shorebrook Dr, Normandy Park. This festive evening will transport you back to the fifties, featuring live music from Robbie Dee’s Tribute to Elvis and DJ Calico spinning holiday classics. Enjoy delicious food and beverages, with beer and wine provided by The Friends of Normandy Park Foundation. Attendees are encouraged to contribute by submitting family photos from past Normandy Park holidays, especially retro ones, to be displayed at the event. Don your semi-formal sparkly solstice attire and celebrate the season with your community!

Join Antioch University for a Graduate Management Info Session on December 9, 2025, from 12:00 PM to 12:30 PM EST, held virtually via Zoom. This session is designed for prospective students interested in exploring the university's graduate management and leadership programs. Attendees will gain insights into core courses and the unique opportunities available within the programs, including the MA in Nonprofit Management, MHSA in Human Services Administration, and an MBA with various concentrations such as Nonprofit Leadership and Healthcare Leadership. Additionally, learn about the option to create a self-designed MBA. Don't miss this chance to discover how Antioch can help you achieve your academic and professional goals!

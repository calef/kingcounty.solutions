Join us for the Holiday Tree Lighting on Dec. 6, 2025, from 4 to 5 p.m. at Town Square Park, located at 31600 Pete von Reichbauer Way S. in Federal Way, Washington. This festive, free event welcomes everyone to celebrate the holiday season with a variety of activities. Enjoy live music, a special visit from Santa, and delicious free cocoa and cookies. The highlight of the evening will be the tree lighting ceremony led by Mayor Jim Ferrell. Attendees can also look forward to performances from local youth groups, including the Federal Way Parks Youth Jazz Classes and the Sherwood Forest Elementary Choir. Don’t miss this wonderful opportunity to kick off the holiday season with family and friends!

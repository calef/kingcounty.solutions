Join WorkSource Seattle-King County for a workshop designed specifically for Creole speakers on job search strategies. Participants will learn effective methods to find employment, including how to identify companies hiring and utilize networking opportunities. The event will take place on Dec. 8, 2025, from 3 p.m. to 4 p.m. via Zoom. 

This workshop is ideal for those who may not speak English fluently and are seeking guidance in their job search. Registration is required, and attendees are encouraged to ensure they have a reliable internet connection. For more information or to register, please contact Maryse Brock at Maryse.Brock@esd.wa.gov. Don't miss this opportunity to enhance your job search skills in a supportive environment!

Join the Community Services and Safety Committee meeting on December 8 from 5:30 to 6:45 p.m. This hybrid event will take place both onsite in the Hazelnut Conference Room at City Hall, located at 6200 Southcenter Blvd., and virtually via MS Teams. Attendees can expect to engage in discussions on policy recommendations related to public safety, community engagement, and various municipal services. The meeting is open to the public, providing a platform for community input on important legislative matters. Access details for the virtual meeting will be included in the agenda. Don't miss this opportunity to contribute to your community's safety and services!

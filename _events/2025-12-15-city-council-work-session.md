Join the City Council for a work session on December 15, 2025, from 5:30 PM to 6:45 PM at City Hall in the Council Chamber, located at 6200 Southcenter Boulevard, Tukwila, WA. This informal gathering allows community members to engage with council members as they discuss topics of general interest and receive presentations. While no formal decisions will be made during this session, attendees can expect an open environment for dialogue and insights into local governance. The meeting will be held in a hybrid format, allowing participation in person or via Microsoft Teams. Don’t miss this opportunity to connect with your local leaders and stay informed about community matters.

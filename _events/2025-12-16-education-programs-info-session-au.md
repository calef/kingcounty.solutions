Join us for the Education Programs Info Session at Antioch University on Dec. 16, 2025, from 3:30 to 4:30 p.m. PST. This virtual event offers a unique opportunity to explore Antioch's diverse graduate and post-graduate education programs, including the Master of Arts in Education and various certificates in specialized fields like Dyslexia Studies, Mindfulness for Educators, and Climate Change Education. Attendees will engage in an interactive session designed to highlight Antioch's commitment to progressive education and how it empowers educators to create impactful learning environments. Whether you're looking to advance your career or deepen your expertise, this session is the perfect step toward achieving your educational goals. Don't miss this chance to connect and learn more about transforming your educational journey!

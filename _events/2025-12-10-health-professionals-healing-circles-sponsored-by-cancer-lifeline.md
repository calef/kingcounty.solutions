Join the Health Professionals Healing Circles, sponsored by Cancer Lifeline, on Dec. 10 from 8:30 to 9:30 a.m. PST. This online event offers healthcare professionals a supportive space for sharing experiences and reflecting on their journeys. Participants will engage in guided discussions, fostering connection and understanding among peers in the field. The Healing Circles occur on the second and fourth Wednesdays of each month, providing ongoing opportunities for support and community engagement. Don't miss this chance to connect with others who understand the unique challenges of working in healthcare.

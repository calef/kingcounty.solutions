Join EvergreenHealth for the online event "Cancer Survivorship: Fear of Recurrence" on December 9 from 6:30 PM to 7:30 PM PST. This session is designed for cancer survivors who struggle with anxiety about recurrence. Attendees can expect to learn effective strategies to manage fear and uncertainty, empowering them to live life to the fullest after a cancer diagnosis. This supportive environment encourages open discussion and offers valuable insights to help navigate post-treatment challenges. Don't miss this opportunity to gain tools for emotional resilience and connect with others who share similar experiences.

Join us for Family Night @ the Museum on December 9, 2025, from 6 PM at the Hands On Children's Museum, located at 414 Jefferson Street NE, Olympia. This engaging evening invites families to explore interactive exhibits and participate in hands-on activities designed for children of all ages. Attendees can enjoy special programs, crafts, and demonstrations that inspire creativity and learning. It's a perfect opportunity for families to bond while discovering the wonders of science, art, and culture in a fun and welcoming environment. Don't miss this chance to create lasting memories together!

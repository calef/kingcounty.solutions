Join us for the Museology Information Session featuring a student panel on December 11, 2025, at 10:00 AM PST via Zoom. This engaging event offers attendees the opportunity to learn about the field of museology from current students. Participants will gain insights into academic programs, career paths, and real-world experiences in museums and cultural institutions. The panel will allow for an interactive Q&A, providing a platform for prospective students to ask questions and gather valuable information. Don't miss this chance to explore the fascinating world of museology!

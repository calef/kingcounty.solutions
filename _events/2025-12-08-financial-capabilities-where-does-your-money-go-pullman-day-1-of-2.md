Join WorkSource Seattle-King County for "Financial Capabilities: Where Does Your Money Go?" on Dec. 8 from 2:30 to 4:30 p.m. in Pullman. This interactive workshop is designed to help participants explore their spending habits and develop a personalized monthly budget. 

As part of a two-day workshop series, attendees will gain valuable insights into making informed financial decisions. The sessions are flexible and can be customized to fit individual needs, allowing participants to take units in any order. 

Registration is required, and early sign-up is encouraged due to limited space. This event will take place online via Zoom, making it accessible from anywhere. Don't miss this opportunity to enhance your financial literacy and take control of your finances.

Join us for "Cultural Dancing—Shining Night Dancing" on December 8 from 6:00 PM to 8:00 PM at the South Park Neighborhood Center in Seattle. This vibrant event showcases traditional dances from various cultures, including Cambodia, Latin America, and Vietnam. Attendees can enjoy an evening filled with music and movement, celebrating the rich diversity of our community. Whether you're a seasoned dancer or just looking to have fun, this event promises to be a lively and engaging experience. Don't miss out on this opportunity to connect with others and immerse yourself in the joy of cultural expression!

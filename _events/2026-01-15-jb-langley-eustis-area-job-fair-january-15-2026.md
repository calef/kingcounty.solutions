Join us for the JB Langley-Eustis Area Job Fair on Jan. 15, 2026. This event is a fantastic opportunity for job seekers to connect with local employers and explore a variety of career options. Attendees can expect to meet recruiters, submit applications, and participate in on-site interviews. Whether you're looking for a new job, a career change, or just want to network, this job fair is designed to help you take the next step in your professional journey. Mark your calendar for Jan. 15 and come ready to engage with potential employers in a supportive environment.

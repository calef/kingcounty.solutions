Join us for the Islamic Finance Workshop: Wealth, Wills & Wisdom on December 6, 2025, from 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM. This informative workshop will cover essential topics related to Islamic finance, including wealth management and the importance of wills in accordance with Islamic principles. Attendees will gain valuable insights and practical knowledge to help navigate financial decisions in a manner that aligns with their values. Whether you're looking to enhance your financial literacy or seeking guidance on estate planning, this workshop offers a supportive and engaging environment. Don't miss this opportunity to learn from experts and connect with others in the community. The event will take place at the designated location.

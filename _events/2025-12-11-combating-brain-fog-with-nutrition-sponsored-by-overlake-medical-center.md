Join us online on Dec. 11 from 9:30 to 10:30 a.m. PST for "Combating Brain Fog with Nutrition," a presentation sponsored by Overlake Medical Center. This informative session will explore how nutrition can play a vital role in enhancing cognitive functions and alleviating memory issues commonly experienced during and after cancer treatment. Attendees will gain valuable insights and practical tips to improve their mental clarity through dietary choices. Don't miss this opportunity to learn from expert Suzanne McMurry, ND, FABNO, and take a positive step toward better brain health.

Join us for the Free or Low-Cost Mobile Pet Clinic on December 14 from 11:00 AM to 1:00 PM at the Renton Library, located at 100 Mill Ave S, Renton. This event, hosted by WA Health Outreach, offers essential veterinary services for pets belonging to individuals who are unhoused or have low incomes. Services are provided on a first come, first served basis, so it's advisable to arrive early and be prepared for a wait. Please bring any items you may need for comfort while you wait. The clinic aims to assist as many pets as possible, but capacity may be limited. We appreciate your understanding and patience as we strive to provide quality care to our community's furry friends.

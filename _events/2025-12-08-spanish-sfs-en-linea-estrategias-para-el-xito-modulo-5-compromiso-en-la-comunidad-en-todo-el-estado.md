Join us for the online event "(Spanish SFS) EN LINEA Estrategias para el Éxito: Modulo 5 Compromiso en la Comunidad," starting on December 8, 2025, at 9:00 AM and running through December 11, 2025. This workshop, hosted by WorkSource Seattle-King County, will explore the importance of community engagement and how small actions can create significant change. Participants will learn about individual culture, social rights, and civic responsibility. The sessions will be conducted in Spanish via Zoom, and attendees are encouraged to register in advance. Each module in this series is designed to be flexible, allowing participants to join any module in any order. Don't miss this opportunity to enhance your skills and commitment to your community!

Join us for an exciting evening of karaoke and dance at the South Park Neighborhood Center on Dec. 12 from 6 to 8 p.m. Doors open at 5:30 p.m., and everyone is welcome to participate in this fun-filled community event. Whether you want to showcase your singing talent or simply enjoy the performances, it's a great opportunity to connect with neighbors and have a good time. If you're interested in being added to our ongoing guest list, please reach out to Andrew Pardini or Thuy Numrich. Don't miss out on this lively night of music and camaraderie!

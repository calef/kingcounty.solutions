Join us for the food bank event on Dec. 22, 2025, from 9 a.m. to noon. This community gathering provides an opportunity for attendees to receive essential food supplies and support those in need. Whether you are looking to donate or require assistance, all are welcome to participate. The event will take place at the designated location, where volunteers will be on hand to help distribute food items. Come together with your neighbors to make a positive impact in our community.

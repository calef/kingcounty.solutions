Join us for EARLY START Kent on December 16, 2025, from 12:00 PM to 1:00 PM. This engaging event offers attendees the opportunity to connect with local resources and support systems aimed at fostering early childhood development. Participants can expect informative discussions, networking opportunities, and valuable insights into early education initiatives. Whether you're a parent, educator, or community member, this event is designed to enhance understanding and collaboration in early childhood care. Don't miss this chance to be part of a supportive community focused on the future of our children.

Join us for a captivating performance of "They Don't Pay! We Won't Pay!" at the Floyd and Delores Jones Playhouse Theater on Dec. 6, 2025, at 7:30 p.m. This lively comedy by Dario Fo explores themes of social justice and the absurdity of economic struggles through humor and wit. Attendees can expect an engaging evening filled with laughter and thought-provoking commentary as the characters navigate their challenges. Don't miss this opportunity to experience a unique theatrical production that promises to entertain and inspire.

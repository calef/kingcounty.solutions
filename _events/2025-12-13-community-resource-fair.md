Join us for the Community Resource Fair on December 13 from 11:30 AM to 2:30 PM at the Tukwila Library, located at 14380 Tukwila International Boulevard. This event is designed to support community members in need, featuring various social service providers who can connect you with essential resources such as healthcare, education, and legal assistance. Whether you're seeking help or want to learn about available services, this fair is an excellent opportunity to gather information and support. Reasonable accommodations for individuals with disabilities are available by request, and automated closed captioning will be provided for online events. Don’t miss this chance to engage with local resources and strengthen community connections!

Join us on Dec. 7, 2025, from 8 a.m. to 5 p.m. at the Bloodworks Donation Center, located at 660 SW 39th St. #100 in Renton, for the "Give Blood to Save 3 Lives" event. This is a vital opportunity to donate blood and help save lives, as every two seconds someone in the U.S. needs blood. Attendees can fill out an entry form for a chance to win a big prize while contributing to this essential cause. Your donation is crucial, and you can also inquire about donating umbilical cord blood after childbirth. To schedule an appointment, please call 1-800-398-7888. Come be a hero and make a difference in your community!

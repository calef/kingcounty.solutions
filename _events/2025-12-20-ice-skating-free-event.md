Join us for a fun-filled afternoon of ice skating at the Sno King Ice Arenas in Snoqualmie on December 20 from 3:00 to 4:00 p.m. This free event, organized by Empower Youth Network, invites middle and high school students to enjoy skating during winter break. Skates will be provided, and attendees are encouraged to wear their favorite winter attire to enhance the festive spirit. Please note that a waiver form must be signed by a parent or guardian for participants to skate. Parents are advised to drop off their youth and return by 4:00 p.m. for pick-up. Don't miss this opportunity for a great time on the ice with friends!

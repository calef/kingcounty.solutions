Join us on December 12 from 1:00 PM to 2:00 PM at Sunshine Garden, located at 611 S Lane St, Seattle, WA, for the Sunshine Garden: Tech Help event. This weekly drop-in session is designed to assist attendees in enhancing their skills with smartphones and tablets. Participants can learn how to stay connected with family and friends, utilize translation apps, and discover other useful technology tips. Whether you're a beginner or looking to improve your tech knowledge, this event offers a friendly environment to get your questions answered. Don't miss this opportunity to boost your digital literacy!

The Planning Commission meeting scheduled for December 11, 2025, at 6:30 PM has been cancelled. Typically, the Planning Commission serves as an advisory body to the City Council, reviewing and making recommendations on critical land use matters such as the Comprehensive Plan, Shoreline Plan, and Zoning Codes. While this meeting will not take place, residents are encouraged to stay informed about future discussions and decisions impacting the community. For any inquiries, please reach out to Wynetta Bivens at 206-431-3670. The event was set to be held at Tukwila City Hall in the Council Chambers, located at 6200 Southcenter Blvd, Tukwila, WA 98188.

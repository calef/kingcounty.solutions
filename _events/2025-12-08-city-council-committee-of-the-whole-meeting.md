Join the City Council Committee of the Whole Meeting on December 8, 2025, from 7:00 PM to 10:00 PM at Tukwila City Hall, located at 6200 Southcenter Blvd. This meeting will be held both in-person and virtually, providing an opportunity for community members to engage with local government. Attendees can expect discussions on various agenda items that will be referred to regular meetings, as well as the chance to provide public comments for up to five minutes. Comments can be made on-site or via phone and Microsoft Teams. To participate virtually, please email in advance for instructions. This meeting serves as a platform for residents to stay informed and involved in local governance.

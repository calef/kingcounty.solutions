Join the City Council regular meeting on December 15, 2025, from 7 to 10 p.m. at Tukwila City Hall, located at 6200 Southcenter Blvd. This event will be held both on-site and virtually, allowing community members to participate from anywhere. Attendees can expect to hear discussions on general and routine business, as well as the adoption of ordinances and resolutions. The mayor will preside over the meeting but will only vote in the case of a tie. Public comments are welcome, with the option to speak for up to five minutes either in person or through phone and Microsoft Teams. To provide comments virtually, please email your name and topic by 5 p.m. on the meeting date. This is a great opportunity to engage with local government and share your thoughts on community issues.

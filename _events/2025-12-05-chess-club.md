Join us for the Chess Club on December 5, 2025, from 1:00 PM to 3:30 PM in the Chamberlain Room. This event is perfect for both seasoned players and newcomers looking to learn the game. Attendees can enjoy friendly matches with fellow chess enthusiasts or receive guidance on the basics of chess. Whether you're looking to sharpen your skills or simply have fun, come and be part of this engaging community gathering!

Join us for the MLIS Law Librarianship Information Session on February 13, 2026, at 4:00 PM PST via Zoom. This informative session will provide attendees with insights into the field of law librarianship, including career opportunities, essential skills, and the impact of law libraries in legal education and practice. Participants will have the chance to engage with experts, ask questions, and learn about the Master of Library and Information Science (MLIS) program tailored for aspiring law librarians. Whether you're considering a career in this niche or simply curious about the profession, this session will equip you with valuable knowledge and resources. Don't miss this opportunity to explore the dynamic world of law librarianship!

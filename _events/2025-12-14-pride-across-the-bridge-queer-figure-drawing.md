Join us for the Pride Across the Bridge | Queer Figure Drawing event on December 14 from 2:00 PM to 4:00 PM at the Together Center in Redmond, WA. This free, relaxed figure drawing session invites artists to take turns posing for each other. Attendees are encouraged to wear their favorite outfits, whether fancy, casual, or even costumes. While no drawing instruction will be provided, participants should bring their own drawing supplies. The event is facilitated by Jack from Wolfberry Studio LLC, with assistance from PAB volunteers. To ensure a safe and enjoyable environment, masks are encouraged, and food is not allowed in the gallery. However, limited consumption of packaged dry snacks and non-alcoholic beverages in closed containers is permitted. Come express your creativity and connect with the community!

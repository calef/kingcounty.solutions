Join the UW Traumatic Brain Injury - Behavioral Health ECHO on Dec. 19, 2025, from 12 p.m. to 1:30 p.m. PST. This monthly online clinic connects front-line health care providers with specialists to discuss evidence-based treatments and case consultations for managing behavioral health issues in patients with traumatic brain injury (TBI). Attendees can expect to engage in interactive discussions, enhancing their understanding of the complexities of TBI-related behavioral health conditions. This program is designed for any provider involved in the care of patients dealing with post-TBI challenges. By participating, you can improve patient outcomes through better access to behavioral health treatment resources. The sessions are conducted via Zoom, making it easy to join from anywhere in Washington state. Don’t miss this opportunity to expand your knowledge and improve your practice.

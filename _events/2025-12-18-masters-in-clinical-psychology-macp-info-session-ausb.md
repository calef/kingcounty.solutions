Join us for the Masters in Clinical Psychology (MACP) Info Session on Dec. 18, 2025, from 4 to 5 p.m. PST, hosted virtually by Antioch University Santa Barbara. This informative session will provide an overview of the MA in Clinical Psychology program, including degree requirements and potential career outcomes. Attendees will have the opportunity to engage with faculty and explore specializations such as Latinx mental health and somatic psychotherapy. The program focuses on fostering authentic relationships and understanding diverse identities, emphasizing social justice and community support. Don't miss this chance to learn how you can make a meaningful impact in the field of psychology!

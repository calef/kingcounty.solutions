Join us for the MLIS Prospective Student Information Session on Dec. 9, 2025, at 12 p.m. PST via Zoom. This event is designed for anyone interested in pursuing a Master of Library and Information Science. Attendees can expect to learn about the program's curriculum, admission requirements, and career opportunities in the field. You'll have the chance to ask questions and gain insights from faculty and current students. Whether you're considering a career shift or looking to enhance your skills, this session will provide valuable information to help you make an informed decision about your educational journey. Don't miss this opportunity to explore your future in library and information science!

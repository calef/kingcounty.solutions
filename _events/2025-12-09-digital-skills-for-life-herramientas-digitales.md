Join us for the free Spanish-language digital literacy training, Digital Skills for Life, on Dec. 9, 2025, from 5:30 to 7:30 p.m. in Washington. This event is designed for individuals looking to enhance their digital skills and build confidence in using technology. Participants will learn the basics of computer parts, how to navigate Office programs like Word, Excel, and PowerPoint, and best practices for safe internet browsing. Additionally, the training will provide tools and tips to help protect children from inappropriate digital content. Space is limited, so those interested should contact Luis Jaimez at 206-833-2986 to secure a spot. Don't miss this opportunity to support your digital learning journey!

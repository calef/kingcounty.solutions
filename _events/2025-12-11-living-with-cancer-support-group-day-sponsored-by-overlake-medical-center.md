Join the Living with Cancer Support Group on Dec. 11 from 9:30 to 11:30 a.m. PST, hosted online by Overlake Medical Center. This supportive gathering is designed for patients navigating the challenges of a cancer diagnosis, treatment, or post-treatment. Attendees can expect educational presentations and open discussions, providing a safe space to share experiences and learn from others facing similar journeys. Whether you are newly diagnosed or have been through treatment, this group offers valuable insights and community support. Don't miss this opportunity to connect with others and find comfort in shared experiences.

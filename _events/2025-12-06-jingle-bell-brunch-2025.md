Join us for the Jingle Bell Brunch on December 6, 2025, from 10:00 AM to 11:30 AM at the Federal Way Community Center, located at 876 South 333rd Street, Federal Way, WA. This festive event promises a delightful morning filled with holiday cheer, featuring a delicious breakfast, fun holiday crafts, and a special visit from Santa Claus. Children ages 2-10 will enjoy engaging activities, while everyone can revel in the jolly music and festive atmosphere. Don't miss the chance to capture memories at the Hot Cocoa Station with Santa! Limited spots are available, so be sure to pre-register for this joyful celebration at just $18 per person. Come and experience the magic of the season with family and friends!

Join us online on December 10 from 2:00 PM to 3:30 PM PST for "Fermented Foods for Gut Health," sponsored by Valley Medical Center. This informative class will explore the benefits of fermented foods in supporting gut health, especially for those undergoing cancer treatment. Attendees will learn how these foods can provide essential probiotics, ease digestive discomfort, and enhance nutrient absorption. Discover practical tips on incorporating fermented foods into your daily routine during and after treatment. Don't miss this opportunity to improve your gut health with simple and effective dietary changes.

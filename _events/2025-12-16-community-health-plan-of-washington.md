Join the Community Health Plan of Washington on Dec. 16 from 11 a.m. to 1 p.m. at the Advocate Office located in the Together Center, 16305 NE 87th St., Suite 111, Redmond, WA. This free event is designed to help you navigate your health care options and ensure you are adequately covered. Attendees can expect personalized support in scheduling doctor visits, obtaining medications, and connecting with community services. With a mission to empower families in making informed health care decisions, the Community Health Plan of Washington is committed to being more than just a health care provider; it aims to be a supportive community resource. Join us to learn more about how we can assist you and your family in achieving better health outcomes.

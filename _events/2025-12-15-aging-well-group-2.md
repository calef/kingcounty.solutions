Join the Aging Well Group #2 on Dec. 15, 2025, from 11 a.m. to noon at The Center for Active Living, located at 4217 SW Oregon St., Seattle, WA. This weekly in-person support group offers a safe space for participants to engage in discussions and share experiences related to aging. All conversations are confidential, fostering a supportive environment for everyone involved. Please note that pre-registration is required to attend, and those not registered will not be admitted. For more information about this group, contact Michele Fawcett-Long at 206-928-1730.

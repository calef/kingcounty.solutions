Join the Normandy Park Park Commission for its regular meeting on Dec. 17, 2025, from 7 to 9 p.m. at City Hall, located at 801 SW 174th St., Normandy Park, WA. This hybrid event allows attendees to participate in person or via Zoom. Expect discussions on local park initiatives, community feedback, and upcoming projects that impact the parks in our area. Whether you're a resident interested in local governance or looking to share your thoughts on park facilities, this meeting is a great opportunity to engage with the commission. Can't make it? Recorded sessions will be available for later viewing. Stay informed by signing up for the city newsletter for updates on future meetings.

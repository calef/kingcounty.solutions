Join the Living with Meaning Support Group sponsored by Cancer Lifeline on Dec. 8 from 1 to 3:30 p.m. PST. This online event provides a nurturing environment for cancer patients, survivors, and caregivers to connect and share experiences. Participants can expect engaging group discussions, journaling, and creative expression to enhance personal resilience and well-being. Each session is designed to foster support and healing through various themes, including mindfulness, self-care, and emotional growth. The group meets monthly, allowing participants to join at any time. Experience the power of community and gain valuable tools to navigate the journey of cancer together.

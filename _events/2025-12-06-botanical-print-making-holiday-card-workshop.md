Join us for the Botanical Print-Making Holiday Card Workshop on Dec. 6, 2025, at the Douglas Research Conservatory. This hands-on event invites participants to create unique holiday cards using natural materials and print-making techniques. Attendees will learn how to press leaves and flowers to design beautiful, eco-friendly cards perfect for the season. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced artist, this workshop offers a creative way to celebrate the holidays while connecting with nature. Supplies will be provided, so just bring your holiday spirit! Don't miss this opportunity to craft something special to share with loved ones.

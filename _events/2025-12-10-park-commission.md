Join the Park Commission meeting on December 10 from 5:30 PM to 6:30 PM at the Tukwila Community Center, located at 12424 42nd Avenue South, Tukwila, WA. This meeting offers an opportunity for community members to engage in discussions about the acquisition, development, and operation of parks and recreational facilities. Attendees can provide input on local programs and initiatives, ensuring that the community's voice is heard in shaping Tukwila's park system. You can attend in person in the Executive Conference Room or participate virtually via Microsoft Teams. For more information, contact Kris Kelly at Kris.Kelly@TukwilaWA.gov.

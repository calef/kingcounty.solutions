Join us on Dec. 23 from 8:30 a.m. to 4:30 p.m. at the South Park Neighborhood Center for the Social Services Community Connection. This event offers a valuable opportunity to meet with Janette, who will guide attendees through the various social services and resources available in the community. Whether you need assistance or simply want to learn more about local support options, this is the perfect chance to connect. Spanish-speaking assistance is also available. Feel free to drop by or contact Janette at 206-948-7378 to secure an appointment. Don't miss this chance to enhance your understanding of community resources!

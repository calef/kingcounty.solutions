Join us for the EMBA Online Application Workshop on Dec. 10, 2025, at 5:30 p.m. PST. This informative webinar is designed for prospective students interested in pursuing an Executive MBA. Attendees will gain valuable insights into the application process, including tips on preparing a strong application, understanding admission requirements, and navigating deadlines. Whether you're just starting to consider an EMBA or are ready to apply, this workshop will provide the guidance you need to enhance your application. Don't miss this opportunity to ask questions and learn from admissions experts in a supportive online environment.

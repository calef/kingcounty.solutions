Join us for the New Member Concierge event on Dec. 8, 2025, from 11 a.m. to 1:30 p.m. at The Center for Active Living, located at 4217 SW Oregon St., Seattle, WA. This welcoming session, hosted by our dedicated volunteer Cynthia, is designed to assist new and newer members in navigating the center's offerings. Attendees can expect to have their questions answered, meet other members, and receive recommendations for classes and programs. Cynthia will also share information about upcoming events to help you get involved. The Member Concierge will be available every Monday, Wednesday, and Thursday during these hours, so come by to make the most of your membership!

Join us for the Informatics Program Overview for Transfer Students on December 15, 2025, at 4:00 PM PST via Zoom. This informative session will provide potential transfer students with insights into the Informatics program, including curriculum highlights, career opportunities, and the support available for students transitioning into the program. Attendees will have the chance to ask questions and engage with faculty and current students, making it a valuable opportunity for those considering a future in Informatics. Don't miss this chance to learn more about how you can thrive in this dynamic field!

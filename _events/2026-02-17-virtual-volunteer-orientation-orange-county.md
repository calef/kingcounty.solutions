Join Olive Crest for a Virtual Volunteer Orientation on February 17, 2026, from 6:30 PM to 7:30 PM. This event is perfect for those looking to make a difference in the lives of children in need of safety and support. Attendees will learn about various volunteer opportunities available in Orange County and how their contributions can provide loving homes and encouragement to kids. Whether you're new to volunteering or looking to expand your involvement, this orientation will equip you with the information you need to get started. Don't miss this chance to become part of a caring community dedicated to helping others.

Join us for Open Shop on Jan. 10, 2026, from 1 to 5 p.m. at the Bike Works Warehouse, located at 3715 S. Hudson St., Seattle, WA. This self-directed event offers adults the chance to use tools and repair stands, with professional mechanics available for guidance if needed. It's a welcoming environment designed to empower you to learn bike mechanics, tackle repairs like fixing flat tires, or even changing handlebars for a more comfortable ride. Open Shop is not just about fixing bikes; it's about building community and sharing knowledge in a friendly, nonjudgmental space. Whether you're a seasoned cyclist or a beginner, come demystify your bike and gain confidence in your mechanical skills!

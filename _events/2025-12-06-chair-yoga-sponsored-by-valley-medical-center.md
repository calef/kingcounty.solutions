Join us for Chair Yoga sponsored by Valley Medical Center on December 6 from 9:00 AM to 10:00 AM PST, held online. This session is perfect for those curious about yoga but unsure where to start. Attendees will learn the fundamentals of chair yoga and discover the numerous health benefits of regular practice. The class is designed for all ability levels, making it accessible for patients and caregivers alike. Whether you're looking to enhance flexibility, reduce stress, or simply try something new, this event is a great opportunity to improve your well-being from the comfort of your home. Don't miss out on this chance to connect with others and experience the calming effects of yoga.

Join us for the Chilly Yeti Challenge from January 1 to January 31, 2026, as we kick off the new year with an active lifestyle! This community event encourages participants to run, walk, or hike one mile each day throughout January. It's a fantastic opportunity to get outdoors and stay motivated while supporting a great cause. Registration is open until January 5, 2026, with merchandise orders available until January 20. Proceeds from the challenge will benefit KidVantage, providing essential items for local children and families. Don’t miss out on this chance to make a difference while enjoying the winter scenery!

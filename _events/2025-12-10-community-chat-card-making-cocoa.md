Join us for the Community Chat: Card Making & Cocoa event on Dec. 10, 2025, from 4:30 to 6:30 p.m. at Chocolati, located at 8319 Greenwood Ave. N in Seattle. This festive gathering invites attendees to create beautiful holiday cards while enjoying delicious chocolate treats. All card-making supplies will be provided, making it a perfect opportunity to unleash your creativity and spread some holiday cheer. Whether you're a seasoned crafter or trying it for the first time, come connect with neighbors and enjoy a warm, welcoming atmosphere. For any questions, feel free to reach out via email. Don’t miss out on this delightful community event!

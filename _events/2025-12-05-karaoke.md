Join us for an exciting Karaoke event on December 5th from 1:00 PM to 2:00 PM in the Chamberlain Room. This weekly gathering is perfect for those looking to showcase their singing talent or simply enjoy the atmosphere. Attendees can expect a lively environment filled with good food, great company, and the joy of music. Whether you're a seasoned performer or a first-time singer, everyone is welcome to participate in this fun-filled session. Don't miss out on the chance to connect with fellow community members and enjoy an afternoon of entertainment!

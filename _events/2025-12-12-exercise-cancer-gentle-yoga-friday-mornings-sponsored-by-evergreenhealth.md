Join us for "Exercise & Cancer: Gentle Yoga" every Friday morning, starting December 12, from 10:00 AM to 11:15 AM PST, hosted online by EvergreenHealth. This therapeutic yoga class is designed for individuals affected by cancer, offering easy stretches, postures, guided meditation, and breathing exercises to promote relaxation and well-being. Participants can expect a supportive environment led by Sharon St. John, a certified cancer exercise specialist. Whether you're looking to enhance your physical health or find a peaceful moment in your week, this class is a wonderful opportunity for self-care.

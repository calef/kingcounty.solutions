Join the Community Oriented Policing Citizens Advisory Board (COPCAB) meeting on Dec. 11 from 5:30 to 7 p.m. at the Tukwila Justice Center, located at 15005 Tukwila International Blvd., Tukwila, WA. This event offers community members the opportunity to engage with local police leadership and discuss public safety initiatives. The board, composed of residents, business owners, a school representative, and a local student, collaborates to enhance community policing efforts. Attendees can expect to hear updates, share their perspectives, and contribute to fostering a safer community. For further inquiries, contact COPCAB Liaison Phi Huynh.

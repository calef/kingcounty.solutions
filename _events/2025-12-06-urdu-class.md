Join us for an engaging Urdu Class on December 6, 2025, from 10:30 AM to 12:30 PM. This weekly class, held every Saturday, is perfect for both beginners and advanced students looking to enhance their language skills. Attendees can expect a supportive environment to learn Urdu language arts, explore its rich culture, and connect with fellow learners. Classes will continue throughout the year, making it a great opportunity to immerse yourself in this beautiful language. Don't miss out on this chance to expand your linguistic horizons!

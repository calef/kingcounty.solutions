Join us on Dec. 15, 2025, from 3 to 4 p.m. at The Center for Active Living, located at 4217 SW Oregon St., Seattle, for "Traveling the Digital World." This engaging event offers attendees the opportunity to explore various technologies that impact daily life. Participants can expect discussions on topics such as scam awareness, email management, cell phone settings, and podcasts. It's a great chance to enhance your digital skills and stay informed about the latest tech trends. Don't forget to visit the computer lab for a printed schedule of upcoming topics. All aboard for a journey into the digital age!

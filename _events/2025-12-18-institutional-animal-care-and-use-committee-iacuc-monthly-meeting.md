Join the Institutional Animal Care and Use Committee (IACUC) for their monthly meeting on December 18, 2025, from 2:30 PM to 5:00 PM at the UW Police Department Building’s Public Meeting Room, located at 3939 15th Ave NE, Seattle, WA. This open meeting invites community members to engage in discussions regarding animal care and use policies. Attendees can expect to hear reports from the attending veterinarian and the OAW director, and have the opportunity to provide public comments during a designated period. Each speaker will have up to two minutes, with preference given to those who sign up in advance. Public comment sign-up opens seven days before the meeting. The IACUC is committed to accessibility; for accommodations, please contact the Disability Services Office at least ten days prior to the event.

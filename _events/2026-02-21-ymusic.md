Join us for an enchanting evening with yMusic on February 21, 2026, at 7:30 PM at Meany Hall. This innovative ensemble blends classical and contemporary music, offering a unique experience for both classical music enthusiasts and newcomers alike. Attendees can expect a captivating performance featuring original compositions and arrangements that showcase the versatility of the group’s musicians. Don’t miss this opportunity to immerse yourself in a night of inspiring music that pushes the boundaries of genre.

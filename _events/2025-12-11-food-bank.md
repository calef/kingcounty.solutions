Join us for the Food Bank event on December 11, 2025, from 9:00 AM to 12:00 PM at the Federal Way Senior Center, located at 4016 South 352nd Street, Federal Way, WA. Attendees can expect to receive essential food supplies, connect with community resources, and enjoy a welcoming atmosphere. This event aims to support those in need and foster community spirit. Don't miss this opportunity to contribute to a vital cause and help make a difference in the lives of local families.

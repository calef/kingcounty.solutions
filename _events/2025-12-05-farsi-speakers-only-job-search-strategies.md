Join us for an online workshop focused on job search strategies exclusively for Farsi speakers. This event will take place on December 5, 2025, from 2:00 PM to 3:00 PM Pacific Time. Participants can expect to learn effective techniques for navigating the job market, enhancing their job search skills, and gaining insights into available resources.

The workshop will be conducted via Zoom, and registration is required to attend. Upon registration, participants will receive a link to join the session. This event is organized by WorkSource Seattle-King County, aiming to support Farsi-speaking individuals in their employment journey. Don't miss this opportunity to improve your job search approach and connect with others in the community.

Join us for the Digital Literacy Learning Lab on December 8, 2025, from 12:30 PM to 4:00 PM at WorkSource Spokane. This self-paced lab provides a welcoming environment for individuals of all ages and skill levels to enhance their computer and internet skills. Attendees can expect to learn essential topics, ranging from basic computer operations to more advanced skills like email usage and word processing. Friendly staff will be on hand to offer personalized assistance and guidance as you navigate through various learning modules. Whether you're a beginner or looking to refresh your skills, this event is an excellent opportunity to gain confidence in using technology for daily life, work, or education. Don't miss out on this chance to improve your digital literacy in a supportive setting!

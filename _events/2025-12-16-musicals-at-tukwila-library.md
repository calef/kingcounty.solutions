  their friendship 25 years later at the band’s reunion concert.</p> <p>This and many
  other films can be streamed from the <strong>Kanopy</strong> database, free with

Join us at the Tukwila Library on Dec. 16 from 5:30 to 7:30 p.m. for a free screening of the uplifting musical *Greatest Days*. This 2023 film features the beloved songs of Take That and tells the story of five friends who reunite 25 years later at a concert, reigniting their bond. Enjoy a cozy evening filled with music and nostalgia in a welcoming environment. This event is suitable for adults aged 18 and older. Reasonable accommodations for individuals with disabilities are available upon request. Don't miss this opportunity to enjoy a delightful film with fellow community members at 14380 Tukwila International Blvd., Tukwila, WA.

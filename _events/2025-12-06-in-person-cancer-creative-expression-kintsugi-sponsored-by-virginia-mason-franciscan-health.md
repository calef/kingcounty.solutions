Join us on Dec. 6 from 1 to 3 p.m. at Cancer Lifeline, located at 6522 Fremont Ave. N, Seattle, for "Cancer & Creative Expression: Kintsugi," sponsored by Virginia Mason Franciscan Health. This unique workshop invites participants to explore healing through the Japanese art of Kintsugi, which highlights beauty in imperfection by repairing broken pottery with gold. Attendees will engage in a creative process, reflecting on their experiences with cancer and how these experiences can be honored rather than hidden. No prior artistic or writing experience is necessary—just bring an open heart and a willingness to share your story. This in-person event encourages resilience and connection, making it a meaningful opportunity for those impacted by cancer.

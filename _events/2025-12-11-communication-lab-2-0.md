Join us for Communication Lab 2.0 on Dec. 11, 2025, from 1 to 2:30 p.m. at The Center for Active Living, located at 4217 SW Oregon St., Seattle, WA. This engaging workshop, led by Sura Hart from the Center for Nonviolent Communication, invites attendees to explore and enhance their communication skills in a supportive environment. Participants will observe their communication habits, experiment with new practices, and engage in fun activities, including role plays and the No-Fault Zone® game. This event is perfect for anyone looking to foster care, respect, and honesty in their interactions. Come share your experiences and learn from others in this collaborative space!

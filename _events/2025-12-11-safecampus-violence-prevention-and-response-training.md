Join us for the SafeCampus Violence Prevention and Response Training on December 11, 2025, starting at 2:30 PM. This essential training session will equip attendees with the knowledge and skills necessary to recognize, prevent, and respond to potential violence in campus environments. Participants can expect interactive discussions, practical strategies, and resources to foster a safer community. The event will take place at a designated location on campus, creating an opportunity for students, faculty, and staff to engage in meaningful dialogue and learn from experts in the field. Don’t miss this chance to contribute to a safer campus for everyone.

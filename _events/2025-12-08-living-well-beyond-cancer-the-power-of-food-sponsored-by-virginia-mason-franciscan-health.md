Join us online on December 8 from 12:00 PM to 1:00 PM PST for "Living Well Beyond Cancer: The Power of Food," sponsored by Virginia Mason Franciscan Health. This informative session features registered dietitian Kate Ueland, who will explore the relationship between nutrition and colorectal cancer. Attendees will learn how to reduce their risk and maintain health during survivorship through dietary choices. The discussion will also address common myths about cancer and nutrition, including various diets like ketogenic, vegan, and intermittent fasting, as well as the role of sugar in cancer. Don't miss this opportunity to gain valuable insights into how food can empower your health journey.

Join the Green Tukwila Event on Dec. 10, 2025, from 9:30 a.m. to 1 p.m., held virtually. This engaging online volunteer opportunity invites you to help restore Tukwila’s natural spaces. Participants will work alongside Green Tukwila staff and Forest Stewards to remove invasive plants, plant young trees, and nurture native shrubs, contributing to the health of urban forests and watersheds. No prior experience is necessary, as all tools, gloves, and training will be provided. Just bring a water bottle, sturdy shoes, and your enthusiasm. Come together to make a meaningful impact and grow the forest of tomorrow!

Join us for a Community Potluck on December 5, 2025, from 5:30 PM to 7:00 PM. This event is a wonderful opportunity to gather with neighbors, share delicious homemade dishes, and enjoy great company. Bring your favorite dish to share and connect with fellow community members. Whether you’re a seasoned cook or bringing a store-bought treat, everyone is welcome to participate in this festive gathering. The potluck will take place at our local community center, providing a cozy atmosphere for all. Don’t miss out on this chance to celebrate community spirit and make new friends!

Join us for an energetic Zumba class on Dec. 10 from 6 to 7 p.m. at the South Park Neighborhood Center, located at 8201 10th Ave. S, Seattle, WA. This fun fitness program blends cardio with Latin-inspired dance, making it a great way to stay active while enjoying music and movement. Led by a certified instructor, the class is designed to be safe and enjoyable for seniors, ensuring a welcoming environment for all participants. Come ready to dance, exercise, and have a great time!

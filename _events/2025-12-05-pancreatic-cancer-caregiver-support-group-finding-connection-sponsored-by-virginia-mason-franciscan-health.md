Join the Pancreatic Cancer Caregiver Support Group: "Finding Connection," sponsored by Virginia Mason Franciscan Health, on December 5 from 2:00 PM to 3:00 PM PST. This online event is designed for caregivers of individuals living with pancreatic cancer, providing a supportive environment to share experiences and connect with others facing similar challenges. Facilitated by Kari Hilwig, LICSW, the group meets every other Friday and includes educational presentations to empower caregivers. Participants can expect to engage in meaningful discussions and find community support. To RSVP, please contact Kari at 206-356-8547.

The event "CANCELLED - Your Next Employment Opportunity - Job Club - Virtual" was scheduled for December 5, 2025, from 2:00 PM to 4:00 PM. This virtual job club aimed to provide attendees with a supportive environment to network with fellow job seekers, share experiences, and discuss challenges in their job search. Participants would engage in facilitated group discussions on effective strategies for securing employment, including insights into developing a "Success Mindset." Unfortunately, this event has been cancelled.

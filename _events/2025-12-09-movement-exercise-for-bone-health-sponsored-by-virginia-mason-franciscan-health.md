Join us online for "Movement & Exercise for Bone Health," a supportive exercise class sponsored by Virginia Mason Franciscan Health on Dec. 9 from 10 to 11 a.m. PST. This session is specifically designed to enhance bone health and reduce the risk of complications related to bone density, especially for those undergoing or recovering from cancer treatment. Participants can expect a series of safe and effective exercises tailored to improve physical function and overall quality of life. Don't miss this opportunity to focus on your health and well-being in a nurturing environment.

  meet with others in their community and learn about fostering healthy child development

  environments.</p> <p>¿Qué es Kaleidoscope Play &amp; Learn? Los grupos Kaleidoscope

Join us for the Kaleidoscope Play and Learn event on Dec. 10 from 10:30 a.m. to 12:30 p.m. at El Centro de la Raza, located at 2524 16th Ave. S, Seattle, WA. This free gathering is designed for family, friend, and neighbor caregivers, parents, and young children to connect and engage with one another in a supportive environment. Attendees can expect to learn about fostering healthy child development and school readiness through play. The groups are led by community members in culturally responsive settings, making it a welcoming space for everyone. Don't miss this opportunity to bond with others while enhancing your child's early learning experience!

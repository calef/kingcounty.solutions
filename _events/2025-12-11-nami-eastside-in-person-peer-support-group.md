Join NAMI Eastside for an in-person peer support group on Dec. 11 from 6:30 to 8 p.m. at the Together Conference Center, located at 16305 NE 87th St., Redmond, Washington. This supportive gathering is designed for individuals living with any form of mental illness, providing a safe space to share experiences and connect with others on a similar journey. The group meets every second and fourth Thursday evening, fostering a sense of community and understanding. Attendees can expect meaningful discussions and the opportunity to build supportive relationships. Access the center through the conference center doors on the east side of the building. We look forward to welcoming you!

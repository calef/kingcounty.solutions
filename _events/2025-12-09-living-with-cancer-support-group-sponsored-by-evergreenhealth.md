Join the Living with Cancer Support Group sponsored by EvergreenHealth on December 9 from 6:30 PM to 8:30 PM PST, held online. This supportive environment is designed for individuals who are newly diagnosed, undergoing treatment, or are post-treatment. Attendees can expect educational presentations and open discussions that provide valuable insights into navigating the cancer journey. Family and friends are encouraged to participate, fostering a sense of community and shared experience. Whether you seek information, support, or simply a listening ear, this group aims to help you feel less alone in your journey.

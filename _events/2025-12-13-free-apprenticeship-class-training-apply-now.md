  <h3 class="c-blue-dark">About the Manufacturing Academy</h3> <div class="text text-bigger">
  <p>AJAC’s Seattle Manufacturing Academy – a recognized apprenticeship preparation
  program – delves into essential technical skill development while students earn
  industry-recognized certifications—enriching their professional portfolios.</p>
  <p>AJAC’s extensive network of manufacturing employers in King County will play
  a pivotal role in bridging the gap between classroom learning and practical application.</p>
  <p>Upon successful completion of the Manufacturing Academy program, students will
  be connected with career opportunities within the local advanced manufacturing industries.</p>

Join the free apprenticeship class training on Dec. 13, 2025, from 8 a.m. to 11:30 p.m., held virtually across the United States. This event, hosted by AJAC’s Manufacturing Academy, offers participants an opportunity to gain essential skills and certifications for careers in aerospace and manufacturing. Attendees will learn about the program, which emphasizes technical skill development and connects students with local employers in King County. Enrollment is free for eligible Washington state residents. This session is part of a series, with additional classes available on Dec. 6, 20, and 27. Don't miss this chance to enhance your professional portfolio and prepare for exciting career opportunities in advanced manufacturing.

Join us for an exciting Improv event on December 8, 2025, from 1:30 PM to 2:30 PM at The Center for Active Living, located at 4217 SW Oregon St., Seattle, WA. This engaging session invites participants to unleash their creativity by performing instant stories through the art of improvisation. Led by theatre professor Tammi Doyle, attendees will learn to create characters and narratives using concentration, focus, and awareness. Expect to laugh, connect, and explore the joy of storytelling in a supportive environment. Whether you're a seasoned performer or a complete beginner, this event promises to be both fun and inspiring. Don't miss out on the chance to express yourself and enjoy a unique experience!

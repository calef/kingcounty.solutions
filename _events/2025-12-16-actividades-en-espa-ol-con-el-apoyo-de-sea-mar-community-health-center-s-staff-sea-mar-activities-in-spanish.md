Join the Sea Mar Community Health Center staff for a vibrant day of activities in Spanish on Dec. 16, 2025, from 9 a.m. to 2 p.m. at The Center for Active Living, located at 4217 SW Oregon St., Seattle, WA. This event welcomes all members of the Latinx community to enjoy fun activities, socialization, lunch, and access to valuable information and resources in Spanish. It's a fantastic opportunity to connect with others and engage in enriching experiences. For more details, feel free to reach out to Yanin Diaz at yanindiaz@sea.m.archc.org or call 206-227-6025. Don’t miss out on this community gathering!

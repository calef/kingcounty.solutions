  tribe-events-content\"> <p data-olk-copy-source=\"MessageBody\">We are excited to
  invite you to this year’s <strong>Las Posadas &amp; Christmas Market</strong>—a
  joyful community celebration filled with music, culture, and holiday cheer!</p>
  <p>\U0001F4C5 <strong>Friday, December 12</strong> \U0001F552 <strong>2:00 PM –
  8:00 PM</strong> \U0001F3AD <strong>Entertainment begins at 5:00 PM</strong> \U0001F4CD <strong>Centilia
  Cultural Center, 1660 S. Roberto Maestas Festival St., Seattle, WA 98144</strong></p>
  <p>Our evening entertainment will begin at <strong>5:00 PM</strong> with a special
  performance by <strong>UW Drama</strong>, presenting a unique piece inspired by
  the life and artistry of <strong>Frida Kahlo</strong>. The celebration continues
  at 5:55 PM with the <strong>Calaca Clown Show </strong> and then a musical performance
  by the <strong>José Martí All-Star Singers at 6:30 PM</strong>.</p> <p>To close
  the night, we will carry on the beloved tradition of <strong>Las Posadas</strong>,
  complete with <strong>three stations of singing</strong> led by the children and
  teachers of the José Martí Child Development Center.</p> <p>\U0001F31F <strong>Breaking
  News!</strong> We are thrilled to share that <strong>Chef Eva</strong> will be preparing <strong>her
  delicious pozole</strong> for the event! Be sure to come hungry and enjoy this warm,
  traditional dish—perfect for a winter celebration.</p> <p>This family-friendly event
  also includes <strong>hot chocolate, pan dulce, live music, a nativity scene, holiday
  market vendors, and a visit from Santa</strong>—all free for the community.</p>

Join us for Las Posadas & Christmas Market on December 12 from 5:00 PM to 8:00 PM at the Centilia Cultural Center in Seattle. This vibrant community celebration promises an evening filled with music, culture, and holiday spirit. Enjoy a special performance inspired by Frida Kahlo by UW Drama, followed by the Calaca Clown Show and a musical act from the José Martí All-Star Singers. Experience the cherished tradition of Las Posadas with singing led by children and teachers from the José Martí Child Development Center. Indulge in delicious pozole prepared by Chef Eva, along with hot chocolate, pan dulce, and festive market vendors. This family-friendly event also features a nativity scene and a visit from Santa, all free for the community. Come celebrate the season with us!

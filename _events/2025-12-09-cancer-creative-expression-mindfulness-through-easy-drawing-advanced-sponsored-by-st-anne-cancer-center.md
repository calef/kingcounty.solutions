Join us for "Cancer & Creative Expression: Mindfulness through Easy Drawing (Advanced)" on December 9 from 4:00 PM to 5:30 PM PST, hosted online by St. Anne Cancer Center. This engaging class is perfect for those who enjoy drawing structured patterns and want to expand their skills using the Zentangle© method. Building on previous classes, participants will explore new colors, materials, and more intricate patterns, or work on a festive seasonal project. The instructor will guide you step-by-step throughout the session. Be sure to have your drawing supplies ready, including paper, black Micron pens, and any coloring tools you wish to use. Embrace creativity and mindfulness in a supportive environment.

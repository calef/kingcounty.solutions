Join us on Saturday, December 13th, at the Rainier Beach Community Center for "Volunteer - Spread Joy this Holiday Season!" from 10:00 AM to 2:00 PM. This event, hosted by Community Passageways and the United Way of King County, invites you to help distribute winter supplies while enjoying a day filled with food, games, and holiday spirit. Volunteers will assist with event setup, ticketing, running activities, and cleanup. Enjoy coffee and pastries upon arrival, and help create a joyful atmosphere for the community. Additionally, we are collecting household essentials to support families in need. Everyone is welcome to participate and make a difference this holiday season!

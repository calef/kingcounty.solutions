Join the SSUG Ukulele Group on December 5 from 2:00 PM to 4:00 PM in the Chamberlain Room for an afternoon filled with music and fun! Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned player, this welcoming group is the perfect place to enhance your ukulele skills, meet fellow enthusiasts, and enjoy a relaxed atmosphere. Facilitated by Fabiola Rodriguez, the session encourages creativity and community spirit. Donations are appreciated, making it an accessible way to dive into the joy of music. Don’t miss out on this opportunity to strum along and kick off your weekend with good vibes!

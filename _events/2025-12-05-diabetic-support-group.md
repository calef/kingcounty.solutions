Join the Diabetic Support Group on December 5, 2025, from 1:00 PM to 2:00 PM at The Center for Active Living, located at 4217 SW Oregon St., Seattle, WA. This monthly gathering provides a welcoming space for individuals living with diabetes to discuss important topics such as nutrition, foot care, and vision care. Attendees can expect to connect with others, share experiences, and gain valuable insights to better manage their health. Please note that registration is required; contact social worker Toni Ameslav at 206-932-4044, ext. 9, or via email to secure your spot. Remember, only pre-registered participants will be admitted.

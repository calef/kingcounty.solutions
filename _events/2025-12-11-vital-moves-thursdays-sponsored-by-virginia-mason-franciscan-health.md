Join us online for Vital Moves Thursdays, sponsored by Virginia Mason Franciscan Health, starting Dec. 11 at 5:30 p.m. PST. This engaging event series focuses on gentle, upbeat movements designed to enhance energy, strength, clarity, and balance. It is especially beneficial for patients, survivors, and caregivers, particularly those affected by lymphedema. Participants can expect a supportive environment where they can learn and practice movements to improve their well-being. The session runs for one hour, providing an excellent opportunity to connect with others while promoting health and vitality. Don't miss this chance to invigorate your body and mind!

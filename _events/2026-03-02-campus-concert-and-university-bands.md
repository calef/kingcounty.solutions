Join us for an exciting evening of music at the Campus, Concert, and University Bands event on March 2, 2026, starting at 7:30 p.m. Held at the renowned Meany Hall, this concert will feature a diverse lineup of talented student musicians and ensembles from the university. Attendees can expect a vibrant atmosphere filled with engaging performances that showcase the creativity and skill of our campus bands. Don't miss this opportunity to support local talent and enjoy a night of captivating music in a beautiful setting.

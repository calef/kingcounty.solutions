Join us for the Russian-Speaking Senior Day Program on December 12 from 9:30 AM to 2:00 PM at the North Bellevue Community Center, located at 4063 148th Ave NE, Bellevue. This engaging program offers a welcoming environment where older adults can access resources and participate in culturally relevant activities. Designed to promote health, social connections, and overall well-being, attendees can look forward to a variety of activities tailored to enhance their quality of life. The program runs on Tuesdays and Fridays and is funded by the City of Bellevue and Amazon. Enjoy a day filled with connection and support in your preferred language, whether it be Russian or Ukrainian.

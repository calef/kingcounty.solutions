Join us for the NorthWest Share Free Weekly Food Truck on Dec. 11 from 6:30 to 8 p.m. at the Together Center, located at 16305 NE 87th St., Redmond, Washington. This community event aims to ensure that no one goes hungry, offering free vegan and vegetarian meals to anyone in need. Attendees can enjoy a variety of delicious options, including rice, lentil soup, vegetable or tofu curry, Indian flatbread, hot herbal tea, and seasonal fruits, all while supplies last. Whether you’re looking for a meal or want to support the cause, come by and experience the warmth of community and compassion.

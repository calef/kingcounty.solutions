Join the "Navigating Cancer & Grief" series on December 11 from 6:00 PM to 7:30 PM PST, hosted online by Cancer Lifeline. This virtual workshop offers a supportive environment for individuals affected by cancer, including patients, survivors, caregivers, and loved ones, to explore their feelings of grief and loss. Attendees can expect compassionate guidance and valuable resources to help them navigate their journey. This event aims to foster connection and understanding among participants facing similar challenges. Don't miss this opportunity for healing and support in a caring community setting.

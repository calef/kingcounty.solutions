Join us for the virtual transitioning job fair on Feb. 10, 2026. This online event offers job seekers the opportunity to connect with a variety of employers actively looking to hire. Attendees can explore different career options, attend virtual booths, and engage in informative discussions about job openings and industry trends. Whether you are transitioning careers or seeking new opportunities, this fair is designed to help you navigate the job market effectively. Don’t miss this chance to network with potential employers from the comfort of your home. Mark your calendars for Feb. 10, 2026, and prepare to take the next step in your career journey.

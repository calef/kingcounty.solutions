Join Antioch University for an Application Essay Workshop on Dec. 8, 2025, from 5 to 6 p.m. PST. This virtual event, led by writing composition instructor Robert Fox, MFA, aims to help prospective students navigate the often daunting process of writing personal statements and essays for college admissions. Attendees can expect guidance on brainstorming, outlining, drafting, and formatting their essays, as well as tips to overcome self-doubt. Additionally, admissions staff will be available to answer general questions about the application process. Whether you're applying for undergraduate or graduate programs, this workshop will equip you with the tools to craft a compelling personal essay. Don’t miss this opportunity to enhance your writing skills and boost your application!

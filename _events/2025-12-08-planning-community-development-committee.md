Join the Planning and Community Development Committee meeting on Dec. 8 from 5:30 to 6:45 p.m. This hybrid event will take place both onsite in the Hazelnut Conference Room at City Hall and virtually via MS Teams. Attendees can expect discussions on key legislative matters, including housing policies, land use, economic development, and sustainability. The meeting is open to the public, providing an opportunity to engage with committee members and contribute to community planning initiatives. Information on how to access the meeting will be available on the agenda. Don't miss your chance to be part of the conversation that shapes our community's future!

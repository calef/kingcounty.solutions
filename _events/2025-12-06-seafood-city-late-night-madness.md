Join us for Seafood City Late Night Madness on December 6 from 8:00 PM to 11:00 PM at Seafood City Supermarket in Tukwila. Experience the ultimate Filipino street food party as the store and food hall stay open late. Enjoy a vibrant atmosphere filled with music and a variety of delicious street food favorites, including Scrambol, Lumpia Overload, Sizzling Balut, and classic street food balls. This event celebrates Filipino flavors, community, and culture, making it a perfect night out for food lovers and families alike. Don’t miss the chance to indulge in these tasty treats and enjoy a fun-filled evening!

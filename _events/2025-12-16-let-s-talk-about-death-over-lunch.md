Join us for "Let's Talk About Death Over Lunch" on December 16, 2025, from 12:00 PM to 1:30 PM at The Center for Active Living, located at 4217 SW Oregon St., Seattle, WA. This engaging discussion group invites you to explore one of life's most significant topics—death. Inspired by Michael Hebb’s book, participants will enjoy a casual atmosphere where new talking points are introduced at each session, making for lively and thought-provoking conversations. Whether you bring your own lunch or just come as you are, this event aims to foster comfort and openness around the subject of death. Don't miss this unique opportunity to connect with others while discussing a topic that affects us all.

Join us for the Social Services Community Connection on Dec. 9, 2025, at the South Park Neighborhood Center, located at 8201 10th Ave. S, Seattle, WA. This event offers an opportunity to meet with Janette and learn about various social services and resources available in the community. Whether you have specific questions or just want to explore options, attendees can drop by anytime between 8:30 a.m. and 4:30 p.m. Janette is also available for appointments if you prefer a more personalized consultation. Spanish speakers are welcome, as ¡Se habla español! Don’t miss this chance to connect with valuable community resources.

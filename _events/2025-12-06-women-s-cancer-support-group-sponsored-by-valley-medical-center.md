Join the Women's Cancer Support Group sponsored by Valley Medical Center on December 6, 2025, from 10:00 AM to 12:00 PM PST. This online event offers a safe and supportive environment for women facing any type of cancer diagnosis, whether newly diagnosed, in treatment, or finishing treatment. Attendees can expect to engage in open discussions, share experiences, and learn from educational speakers about managing the impacts of cancer on their lives. This group fosters connection and understanding among women navigating similar journeys, ensuring that participants feel supported and empowered. Don't miss this opportunity to connect and gain valuable insights from others who understand your experience.

  <div class="tribe-events-single-event-description tribe-events-content"> <p><span
  class="wdyuqq">Inviting all children 0-5 and their caregivers to join us for 1.5
  hours of learning through play! Every playgroup has stations with rotating toys

  a <a href="https://childcare.org/family-services/find-care-ffn.aspx">Kaleidoscope

  to participate!</p> <p>We’ll be hosting playgroups at our new Family Resource Center
  at Cedar Crossing in Roosevelt (<a href="https://goo.gl/maps/rVQ3XrMbKc2SbXyM8">1005
  NE 67th St, Seattle, WA 98103</a>).</p> <p>Drop-ins are welcome, and registration

Join us for the English Playgroup, part of the Kaleidoscope Play & Learn Group, on Dec. 18 from 10 to 11:30 a.m. at the Family Resource Center, located at 1005 NE 67th St., Seattle, WA. This engaging event invites children aged 0-5 and their caregivers to enjoy 1.5 hours of learning through play. Attendees can expect a variety of stations filled with rotating toys, crafts, songs, and storytime, along with a snack. While the playgroup is conducted in English, participation is welcome for everyone, regardless of language proficiency. Drop-ins are encouraged, and no prior registration is necessary. For any questions, feel free to reach out to Dee Thomas-Butler.

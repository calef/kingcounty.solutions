Join us for "Jazz Innovations, Part I," on Feb. 18, 2026, at 7:30 p.m. in the Music Building (MUS). This exciting event will showcase a fusion of traditional jazz elements with contemporary influences, featuring talented musicians who will take you on a captivating musical journey. Attendees can expect an engaging evening filled with live performances, improvisation, and the exploration of new jazz styles. Whether you're a jazz enthusiast or just curious about the genre, this event promises to be a memorable experience for all. Don't miss the chance to immerse yourself in the vibrant world of jazz!

Join the Oral, Head & Neck Cancer Support Group sponsored by EvergreenHealth on Dec. 10, 2025, from 6:30 to 8:30 p.m. PST. This online event offers a supportive space for individuals living with oral, head, or neck cancer to connect with others facing similar challenges. Attendees can expect educational speakers and open discussions in a safe, nonjudgmental environment. Family and friends are welcome to participate as well. This is a great opportunity to share experiences, gain insights, and find community support. Don’t miss this chance to connect and learn together.

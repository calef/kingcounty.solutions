Join us at The Center for Active Living on Dec. 10, 2025, from 1 to 2:30 p.m. for an informative session on Social Security benefits. This group Q&A will cover essential topics such as the filing process for retirement, spousal, and survivor benefits, along with an overview of Medicare enrollment. Attendees will have the opportunity to ask questions and gain insights from David Carvalho, a retired Social Security claims specialist with two decades of experience. Whether you're approaching retirement or looking to understand your options better, this event will provide valuable guidance. An RSVP is required to attend.

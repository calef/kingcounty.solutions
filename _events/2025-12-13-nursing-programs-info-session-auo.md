  continuing a legacy of quality and practical expertise. Benefit from our flexible,

Join us for a virtual Nursing Programs Info Session hosted by Antioch University's Graduate School of Nursing and Health Professions on December 13, 2025, from 1:00 PM to 2:00 PM EST. This one-hour session will provide an overview of various nursing programs, including the Doctor of Nursing Practice (DNP) and Master of Science in Nursing (MSN). Attendees can engage directly with faculty and Admissions representatives to discuss program specifics, curriculum, and the admissions process. Whether you're looking to advance your nursing career or transition into the field, this session will offer valuable insights into Antioch's commitment to social justice and transformative healthcare education. Don't miss this opportunity to learn how Antioch can help you achieve your professional goals in nursing.

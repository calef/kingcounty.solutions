Join the Living with Metastatic Cancer Support Group, sponsored by EvergreenHealth, on Dec. 13 from 10 a.m. to 12 p.m. PST. This online support group offers a safe space for individuals living with advanced stage cancer to share their experiences and emotions. Attendees can connect with others facing similar challenges, fostering a sense of community and understanding. Caregivers are also welcome to participate. The group meets monthly via Zoom, providing an opportunity for meaningful discussions and support. For those needing assistance with registration or joining the meeting, EvergreenHealth offers support through their Lifeline at 206-297-2500. Don't miss this chance to connect with others who understand the journey of living with metastatic cancer.

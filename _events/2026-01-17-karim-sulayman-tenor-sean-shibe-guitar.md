  field--type-text-with-summary field--label-hidden"> <p>Karim Sulayman — lauded for
  his “velvety tenor and pop-star charisma” (<em>BBC Music Magazine</em>) — joins
  guitarist Sean Shibe, whose “music-making is masterful, beautiful and convincing
  in every way” (<em>The Times, UK</em>), for an intimate recital of music ranging
  from the Middle Ages to the present. This compelling musical journey examines the
  close cultural and musical ties between East and West, reflecting the artists’ personal
  experiences with roots in Lebanon and Japan. </p> <p> </p> <p><strong>PROGRAM</strong></p>
  <p>HENRY PURCELL: <em>Music for a While</em></p> <p>JOHN DOWLAND: <em>Praeludium;
  Fantasia; Time Stands Still</em></p> <p>GIULIO CACCINI: <em>Dalla porta d’oriente</em></p>
  <p>CLAUDIO MONTEVERDI: <em>Si dolce è’l tormento; La mia turca</em></p> <p>TRADITIONAL:
  <em>La prima vez (Sephardic) </em></p> <p>TRADITIONAL: <em>Lamma bada yetathanna
  (Arab Andalusian)</em></p> <p>SAYED DARWISH: <em>El Helwa Di</em></p> <p>RODRIGO/FAIRUZ:
  <em>Li Beirut</em></p> <p>JONATHAN HARVEY: <em>Sufi Dance </em></p> <p>LAYALE CHAKER:
  <em>A Butterfly in New York</em></p> <p>TŌRU TAKEMITSU: <em>In the Woods: Wainscot
  Pond</em></p> <p>BENJAMIN BRITTEN: <em>Songs from the Chinese </em></p> <p> </p>
  <p><strong>Chamber Music Series generously underwritten by Cecilia Paul &amp; Harry
  Reinert and Eric &amp; Margaret Rothchild​</strong></p> </div> </div> </div> </div>

Join us for an enchanting evening of music featuring tenor Karim Sulayman and guitarist Sean Shibe on Jan. 17, 2026, at Meany Hall. Attendees can expect a captivating performance that blends classical and contemporary styles, showcasing the unique synergy between voice and guitar. This event promises to be a memorable experience for music lovers, offering a chance to immerse yourself in the artistry of two exceptional performers. Don't miss this opportunity to enjoy a night of beautiful melodies and heartfelt interpretations in a stunning venue.

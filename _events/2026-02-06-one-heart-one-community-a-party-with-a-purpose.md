Join us for the Bellevue LifeSpring One Circle's annual "One Heart/One Community - A Party With a Purpose" on February 6, 2026, from 6:00 PM to 11:00 PM at the Overlake Golf and Country Club in Medina, Washington. This festive evening will feature cocktails, hors d'oeuvres, a delicious four-course dinner, dancing, and exciting raffle prizes. Attendees will have the chance to purchase unique experiences while enjoying a vibrant atmosphere. Most importantly, your participation will help provide essential nutrition for Bellevue children facing food insecurity. Registration opens at 5:30 PM, so come ready to make a difference while having a great time!

Join the Colorectal Support Group sponsored by Virginia Mason Franciscan Health on Dec. 8 from noon to 1:30 p.m. PST. This online gathering welcomes all patients and support persons impacted by various gastrointestinal cancers, including those of the colon, esophagus, stomach, and more. Attendees can expect informative presentations from speakers on relevant topics, as well as an open forum to discuss the physical and emotional challenges associated with a GI cancer diagnosis. This supportive environment aims to foster connection and understanding among participants. Feel free to join at any time during the session.

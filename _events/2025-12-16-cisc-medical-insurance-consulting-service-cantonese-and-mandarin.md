Join us on Dec. 16 from 9 a.m. to 5 p.m. at CISC, located at 16305 NE 87th St., Suite 123, Redmond, Wash., for the Medical Insurance Consulting Service available in Cantonese and Mandarin. This free event is designed to empower community members by providing person-centered assistance with healthcare coverage. Attendees can expect to receive valuable information about benefits, including Medicare, Medicaid, and various assistance programs tailored for low-income seniors and individuals with disabilities. The Healthcare Access Team will offer workshops and personalized support to help clients make informed decisions regarding their healthcare options. Don't miss this opportunity to gain insights into accessible healthcare solutions in your language!

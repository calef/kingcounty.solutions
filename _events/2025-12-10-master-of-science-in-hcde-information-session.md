Join us on Dec. 10, 2025, from 12 to 1 p.m. PST for an informative Zoom session about the Master of Science in Human Centered Design and Engineering (HCDE). This event, led by HCDE academic advisors, will provide insights into the MS curriculum, student experience, and admissions requirements. Attendees will also have the chance to learn about application tips and connect with the HCDE community, including faculty, students, and alumni. Bring your questions, as there will be a dedicated Q&A session to address any inquiries you may have. Don't miss this opportunity to explore how a master's degree in HCDE can enhance your career!

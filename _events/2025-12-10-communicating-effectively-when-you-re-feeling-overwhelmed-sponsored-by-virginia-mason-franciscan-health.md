Join us online on December 10 from 10:00 AM to 11:00 AM PST for "Communicating Effectively When You're Feeling Overwhelmed," sponsored by Virginia Mason Franciscan Health. This supportive class provides a safe space to explore how to express your thoughts and feelings with honesty and compassion, even during challenging times. Participants will learn gentle communication strategies that foster connection and understanding with others. This event is perfect for anyone looking to improve their communication skills in a caring environment. Don't miss this opportunity to feel heard and supported!

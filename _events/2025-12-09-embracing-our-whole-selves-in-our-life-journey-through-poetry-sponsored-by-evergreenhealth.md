Join us for "Embracing Our Whole Selves In Our Life-Journey Through Poetry," a transformative online event sponsored by EvergreenHealth, on December 9 from 11:00 AM to 1:00 PM PST. This three-part series, led by John Fox and Susan O’Connell, invites participants to engage in deep listening, self-expression, and kindness through poetry. No prior poetry experience is required, making it accessible for everyone. Attendees will have the opportunity to explore their emotions and share their truths in a supportive environment. This event is perfect for those looking to connect with themselves and others creatively. Don’t miss this chance to embark on a poetic journey of self-discovery!

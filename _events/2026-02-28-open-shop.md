Join us for Open Shop on February 28, 2026, from 1:00 PM to 5:00 PM at the Bike Works Warehouse, located at 3715 S Hudson St, Seattle, WA. This self-directed event provides adults with access to tools and repair stands, allowing you to work on your bike with or without the guidance of a professional mechanic. It's a welcoming and non-judgmental space where you can learn bike mechanics, assist others, and foster community connections. Whether you need to fix a flat tire or upgrade your handlebars, this is the perfect opportunity to get hands-on experience and demystify your bike. Come empower yourself and enjoy the camaraderie of fellow bike enthusiasts!

Join Still Waters for their Snack Pack Winter Donation Drive on December 19 at 8 a.m. at PO Box 88984 in Tukwila. This event aims to combat food insecurity for local homeless students, helping them focus on their education and future. Attendees can contribute by donating food items or funds, which will directly support the weekly Snack Packs provided to families in need. Volunteers are also welcome to assist with the assembly and distribution of these packs. Your generosity can make a significant difference in the lives of Tukwila students. For more information or to get involved, contact Still Waters Services for Families in Transition via email.

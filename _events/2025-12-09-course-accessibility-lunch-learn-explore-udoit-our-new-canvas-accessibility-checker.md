Join us on December 9, 2025, at 12:00 PM for a Course Accessibility Lunch & Learn focused on UDOIT, our new Canvas Accessibility Checker. This engaging session will provide attendees with insights into how to effectively use UDOIT to enhance course accessibility. Participants will learn practical tips and strategies for ensuring their online courses meet accessibility standards, fostering an inclusive learning environment for all students. The event will take place via Zoom, and registration is required to attend. Don't miss this opportunity to improve your course design and support diverse learners!

Join the Civil Service Commission meeting on Dec. 18 from 5 to 6 p.m. at 6300 Southcenter Blvd, Tukwila, WA. This meeting will cover important civil service matters, including examinations, appointments, promotions, and appeals related to police and fire personnel. Attendees can participate in person in the Duwamish Meeting Room on the second floor or join virtually by phone. For in-person entry, please contact Joy Maldonado at 206-658-5585. This is an opportunity to engage with local governance and understand the processes that affect public safety personnel in your community.

Join the Mason Health Cancer Wellness Support Group, sponsored by Cancer Lifeline, on Dec. 10 from 4 to 6:30 p.m. PST. This online event offers a nurturing space for cancer patients, survivors, and their companions to connect and support one another. Attendees can expect engaging group discussions, journaling, and expressive arts, all facilitated by skilled professionals. This yearlong wellness program encourages participation at any time, providing valuable tools and resources to enhance personal well-being. Whether you are seeking support or wish to share your experiences, this community gathering is a wonderful opportunity for healing and growth.

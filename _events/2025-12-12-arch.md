Join us at ARCH on December 12 from 10:00 AM to 2:00 PM for an open office hour event. Community members are invited to drop by to learn about ARCH programs, gather information on affordable housing, and receive assistance in signing up for email alerts about new low-income housing opportunities. This is a great chance to ask questions and connect with resources that can help you navigate housing options in the area. ARCH is located at 16305 NE 87th St, Suite 119, Redmond, WA 98052. Don't miss this opportunity to get informed and engaged!

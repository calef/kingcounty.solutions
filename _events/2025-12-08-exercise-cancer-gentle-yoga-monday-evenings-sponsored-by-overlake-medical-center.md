Join us for "Exercise & Cancer: Gentle Yoga," a supportive online class sponsored by Overlake Medical Center, starting on December 8 at 6:30 PM PST. This weekly session, running until further notice, offers a nurturing environment where participants can engage in soothing stretches, therapeutic postures, guided meditation, and breathwork. These practices are designed to help release tension, promote relaxation, and enhance overall well-being, making it ideal for individuals affected by cancer. Whether you're a beginner or have some experience, this gentle yoga session is accessible to all. Don’t miss this opportunity to connect with others and prioritize your health in a calming space.

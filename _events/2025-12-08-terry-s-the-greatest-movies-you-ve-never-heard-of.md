Join us on Dec. 8, 2025, at The Center for Active Living for "Terry’s The Greatest Movies You’ve Never Heard Of," starting at 12:30 p.m. This engaging event features a screening of the classic film "The Naked City" (1948), along with newsreels, popcorn, previews, and cartoons. Enjoy a nostalgic afternoon as you discover lesser-known cinematic gems in a welcoming community atmosphere. The event will take place in the second-floor Nucor Room, making it the perfect opportunity to gather with fellow movie enthusiasts. Don't miss this chance to explore the rich history of film while enjoying some delightful treats!

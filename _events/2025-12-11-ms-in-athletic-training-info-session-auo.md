Join us for the MS in Athletic Training Info Session at Antioch University on Dec. 11, 2025, from 5:30 to 6:30 p.m. EST. This virtual event is perfect for recent or soon-to-be graduates with a bachelor's degree in exercise science or kinesiology, as well as current undergraduates interested in pursuing a master’s degree. Attendees will gain valuable insights into the Master of Science in Athletic Training program, which emphasizes hands-on experience in various settings, including colleges, high schools, and clinical environments. Discover how this program prepares you to become a leader in athletic training and make a positive impact on the lives of active individuals. Don't miss this opportunity to start your journey in this rewarding field!

Join us for Men's Support Group #2 on December 15, 2025, from 11:45 AM to 12:45 PM at The Center for Active Living, located at 4217 SW Oregon St., Seattle, WA. This group provides a welcoming space for men to engage in meaningful discussions about the challenges of aging and share ideas, experiences, and resources. Participants can expect a supportive environment where they can connect with others facing similar situations. Whether you seek advice, camaraderie, or simply a listening ear, this group is here for you. For registration, please contact social worker Josh Maduell at 206-932-4044, ext. 2, or via email at joshm@wscenter.org.

Join us for a Volunteer Repair Party on Jan. 22, 2026, from 6 to 9 p.m. at the Bike Works Warehouse, located at 3715 S. Hudson St. #111, Seattle, Washington. This event is perfect for adults aged 18 and older who are interested in bike repair, regardless of their skill level. Volunteers will have the opportunity to learn and assist in repairing bikes, making it a great way to get involved with the community and support sustainable transportation. Please note that participants must complete an application and orientation process before attending. Come contribute your time and energy to help others while gaining valuable hands-on experience!

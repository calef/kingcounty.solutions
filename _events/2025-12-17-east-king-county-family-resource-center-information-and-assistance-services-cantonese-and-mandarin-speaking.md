The East King County Family Resource Center will host an Information and Assistance Services event for Cantonese and Mandarin speakers on Dec. 17 from 9 a.m. to noon at CISC, located at 16305 NE 87th St, Suite 123 in Redmond. Attendees can expect to receive support and resources tailored to their needs, including assistance with navigating government programs related to housing, healthcare, and financial stability. The bilingual and bicultural staff will provide advocacy, community building, and educational workshops. This free event aims to empower families and strengthen connections within the community.

Join us at the UW Surplus Public Store on December 9, 2025, starting at 10:00 AM, located in the Plant Services Building (PSB). This event offers a unique opportunity to browse and purchase surplus items from the University of Washington, including furniture, equipment, and other goods at affordable prices. Attendees can find great deals while supporting sustainability efforts by giving these items a second life. Whether you're looking for office supplies, home goods, or something special, there's something for everyone. Don't miss out on this chance to shop sustainably and discover hidden treasures!

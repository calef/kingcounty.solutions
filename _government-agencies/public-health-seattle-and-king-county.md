**Public Health – Seattle & King County** protects and improves the health and well-being of all residents.  
Its programs span multiple social service domains:

- **Health Care:** clinics, immunizations, reproductive health, and community health services  
- **Mental Health & Counseling:** crisis response, suicide prevention, and behavioral health integration  
- **Food & Nutrition:** WIC, nutrition education, and food safety initiatives  
- **Community & Social Services:** emergency preparedness, public health outreach, and health equity programs  

Public Health serves as the county’s primary health authority and a critical community resource.

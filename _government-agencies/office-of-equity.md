The **Office of Equity** leads statewide efforts to promote equitable access to government services and reduce disparities.  
Relevant to social services through:

- **Community & Social Services:** equitable access initiatives for marginalized communities  
- **Employment & Job Support:** inclusive workforce development strategies  
- **Education & Learning:** improving access to public education and training opportunities  

The Office of Equity helps ensure all Washington residents have fair opportunities to thrive.

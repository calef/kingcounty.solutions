The **Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services (CMS)** administers major federal health insurance programs.  
Its responsibilities include:

- **Health Care:** Medicare for seniors and Medicaid for low-income individuals  
- **Financial Assistance:** coverage subsidies and insurance regulation  
- **Senior Services:** long-term and home care supports  

CMS expands access to healthcare and financial protection for vulnerable Americans.

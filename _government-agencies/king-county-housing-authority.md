The **King County Housing Authority (KCHA)** provides affordable housing and rental assistance to low- and moderate-income residents.  
It focuses on:

- **Shelter & Housing:** public housing, Section 8, and rental vouchers  
- **Financial Assistance:** rent subsidy and housing stability programs  
- **Senior Services:** affordable senior housing communities  
- **Community & Social Services:** partnerships with nonprofits for supportive housing initiatives  

KCHA works to ensure that everyone in King County has access to safe, affordable homes.

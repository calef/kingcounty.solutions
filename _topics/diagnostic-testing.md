Diagnostic testing serves residents by identifying or confirming health conditions, infections, and diseases. These tests, including laboratory analyses and imaging, support early detection and effective treatment. By tracking outbreaks and monitoring chronic conditions, diagnostic testing plays a crucial role in maintaining public health and ensuring timely medical interventions for individuals.

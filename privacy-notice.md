## Information collected

Content is served without advertising or third-party trackers. If we add optional analytics or feedback tools in the future, we will disclose them here and provide choices where possible.

## External sites
When you follow links to other websites, their privacy practices apply. Review their policies before providing personal information.

## Data security
We take reasonable measures to protect this site, but no method of transmission or storage is completely secure. Avoid submitting sensitive information through or about this site.

## Updates and contact
We may update this notice as our practices evolve. For questions or to report concerns, contact the site maintainers before sharing confidential details.

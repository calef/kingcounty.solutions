The **United States of America (USA)** is a federal republic composed of 50 states, a federal district (Washington, D.C.), and several territories.
It is located primarily in North America and is bordered by Canada to the north, Mexico to the south, the Atlantic Ocean to the east, and the Pacific Ocean to the west.

The U.S. federal government is headquartered in **Washington, D.C.**, and operates through three branches: the Executive, Legislative, and Judicial branches.

Skykomish is a small mountain town in eastern King County located along U.S. Highway 2 in the Cascade Mountains near Stevens Pass. Once a vital Great Northern Railway stop, the town now serves as a gateway for outdoor recreation including hiking, skiing, and river rafting. Despite its size, Skykomish maintains a strong civic identity and historic downtown district.

The **Washington State Housing Finance Commission (WSHFC)** promotes affordable housing and community investment.  
Its key focus areas include:

- **Shelter & Housing:** financing for low-income housing and first-time homebuyers  
- **Financial Assistance:** down-payment support and energy-efficiency incentives  
- **Senior Services:** affordable housing for older adults  

The commission empowers individuals and families to achieve stable housing and financial independence.

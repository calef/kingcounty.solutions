The **Legal Services Corporation (LSC)** funds legal aid organizations that assist low-income Americans.  
It contributes to:

- **Legal Aid:** civil legal support for housing, benefits, and consumer issues  
- **Domestic Violence:** representation and advocacy for survivors  
- **Financial Assistance:** preventing unlawful evictions and debt collection  

LSC promotes equal access to justice and fair treatment under the law.

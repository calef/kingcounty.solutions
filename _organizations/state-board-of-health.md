The **State Board of Health (SBOH)** develops public health policies that shape statewide wellness and prevention initiatives.  It contributes to:

- **Health Care:** population health standards and policy development
- **Mental Health & Counseling:** behavioral health regulation and community input
- **Education & Learning:** health literacy and school health policy recommendations

The board connects public health policy with community well-being.

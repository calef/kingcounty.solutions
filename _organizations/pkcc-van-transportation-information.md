# PKCC Van & Transportation Information

The PKCC Van provides transportation for residents of Kirkland to local grocery stores, medical appointments, and the Peter Kirk Community Center. Operating Monday through Friday, rides require a reservation 72 hours in advance, with a suggested donation of $2 per trip.

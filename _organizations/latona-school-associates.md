Latona School Associates (LSA) is a nonprofit organization providing before and after school childcare at John Stanford International School. LSA focuses on social and emotional learning, experiential learning, and creating a safe, engaging environment for children. The program operates from 2:25 PM to 6:00 PM on weekdays and offers additional services during school breaks.

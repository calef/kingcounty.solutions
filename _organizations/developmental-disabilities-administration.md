The **Developmental Disabilities Administration (DDA)** supports individuals with intellectual and developmental disabilities and their families. It focuses on providing support in the areas of disabilities, employment and job support, and community and social services. DDA promotes inclusion, independence, and choice for people with developmental disabilities.

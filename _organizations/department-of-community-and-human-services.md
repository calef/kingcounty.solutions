The **Department of Community and Human Services (DCHS)** connects people, communities, and organizations to resources that support health, housing, and human potential.  
Key service areas include:

- **Shelter & Housing:** homeless prevention, affordable housing, and rental assistance  
- **Health Care & Mental Health:** behavioral health treatment, recovery, and supportive care programs  
- **Employment & Job Support:** workforce development and vocational training  
- **Youth and Family Services:** early learning, childcare, and family stability initiatives

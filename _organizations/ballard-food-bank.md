Ballard Food Bank is a “Hub for Hope,” providing choice-based groceries, no-cook bags, home grocery delivery, and weekend food for kids. The onsite Community Resource Hub offers rent and utility assistance, mail service, ID vouchers, and referrals for healthcare and emergency financial help. Anyone in Seattle can shop or visit the Kindness Café. The food bank also connects neighbors to transit routes and partners like United Way. Contact the team for service schedule details or to get involved.

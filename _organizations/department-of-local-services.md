The **Department of Local Services (DLS)** serves residents and businesses in unincorporated King County. Its social service contributions include:

- **Personal & Household Needs:** local assistance and neighborhood engagement programs
- **Transportation & Mobility:** road and transit access improvements for rural areas
- **Public Safety Support:** coordination with human services and emergency response agencies

DLS ensures equitable delivery of essential county services outside incorporated cities.

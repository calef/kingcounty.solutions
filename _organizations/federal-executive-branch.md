The **Federal Executive Branch** enforces and administers U.S. laws through the leadership of the **President**, **Vice President**, and **executive agencies**. It includes the **Cabinet departments**, **independent agencies**, and **regulatory commissions** that carry out federal policies and programs. The President serves as **head of state, chief executive, and commander in chief**, overseeing national defense, foreign affairs, and government operations.

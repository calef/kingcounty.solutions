Riverview School District provides a vibrant educational experience in the Snoqualmie Valley, serving over 3,000 students. The district emphasizes inclusion, innovation, and community engagement, offering a range of programs including Special Education and Career & Technical Education. With a commitment to academic excellence, Riverview fosters a supportive environment where every student is celebrated.

Riverview School District provides a vibrant educational experience in the Snoqualmie Valley, serving more than 3,000 students. The district emphasizes inclusion, innovation, and community engagement, offering a range of programs, including special education and career and technical education. With a commitment to academic excellence, Riverview fosters a supportive environment where every student is celebrated.

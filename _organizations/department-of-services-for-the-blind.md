The **Department of Services for the Blind (DSB)** supports individuals with vision loss through employment and independent living programs.  It contributes to several social service domains:

- **Disabilities:** rehabilitation, adaptive technology, training, and peer and family support resources
- **Employment & Job Support:** vocational training and job placement for blind and low-vision individuals

DSB empowers Washingtonians with visual disabilities to live and work independently.

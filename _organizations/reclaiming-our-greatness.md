Reclaiming Our Greatness (ROG) empowers families and individuals in King County, WA, through culturally appropriate support, focusing on housing stability, food security, and navigating complex systems. Our trauma-informed care aims to uplift BIPOC communities, providing essential resources and advocacy for lasting change.

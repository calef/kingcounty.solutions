Somali Community Services of Seattle (SCSS) empowers refugee, immigrant, and low-income families through housing assistance, education programs, youth mentorship, and senior support. Since 1995, SCSS has provided culturally relevant services to help families achieve self-sufficiency and build stable lives in Seattle.

The **Department of Natural Resources and Parks (DNRP)** manages King County’s environmental sustainability, parks, and open spaces.  Its programs contribute to social and community well-being through:

- **Recreation & Community Programs:** access to parks, trails, and natural spaces  
- **Community & Social Services:** environmental education and volunteer stewardship  
- **Transportation & Mobility:** regional trail systems improving accessibility and connection  

DNRP helps make King County’s communities healthier and more resilient.

Amy Summers PHD offers psychological and educational testing services for individuals from birth through adulthood. Specializing in learning disorders and attention difficulties, the practice provides evaluations for school admissions and accommodations. With extensive experience in both private practice and public schools, Amy aims to create a comfortable environment for children during testing.

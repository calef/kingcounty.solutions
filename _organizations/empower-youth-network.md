Empower Youth Network offers mentoring, job-readiness support, and life-skills coaching for youth in the Eastside area. The organization connects young individuals to education and employment pathways, helping to reduce service gaps for younger populations. Their efforts complement the goals of serving all ages and enhance the network's capacity to respond to youth-focused human services needs.

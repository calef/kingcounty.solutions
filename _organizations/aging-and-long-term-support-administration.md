The **Aging and Long-Term Support Administration (ALTSA)** provides services that help seniors and people with disabilities live independently. Its programs align with:

- **Senior Services:** in-home and community-based care, caregiver support
- **Disabilities:** support for adults with functional limitations
- **Health Care:** long-term medical and personal care coordination

ALTSA promotes dignity, safety, and independence for aging residents of Washington.

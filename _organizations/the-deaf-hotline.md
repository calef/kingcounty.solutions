The Deaf Hotline is a 24/7 ASL-accessible hotline for deaf survivors of abuse, offering safety planning, crisis intervention, and emotional support. Formed by a partnership between ADWAS and the National Domestic Violence Hotline, it provides culturally-adept advocacy and resources to empower survivors.

TRAC Associates provides comprehensive vocational assistance services to job seekers in Washington state. It focuses on helping disadvantaged individuals, including those with disabilities, veterans, and low-income job seekers, achieve self-sufficiency through training, rehabilitation, assessment, and counseling.

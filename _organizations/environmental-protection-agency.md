The **Environmental Protection Agency (EPA)** protects human health and the environment.  It contributes to social services by promoting:

- **Health Care:** environmental health and pollution prevention
- **Community & Social Services:** community cleanup and safety programs
- **Education & Learning:** public awareness of environmental risks

EPA safeguards clean air, water, and healthy communities for all Americans.

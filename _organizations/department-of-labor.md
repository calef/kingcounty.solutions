The **Department of Labor (DOL)** fosters job creation, worker rights, and economic opportunity.  Its programs align with:

- **Employment & Job Support:** training, apprenticeships, and job search programs
- **Financial Assistance:** unemployment insurance and workforce grants
- **Community & Social Services:** labor standards enforcement and worker protection

DOL supports a fair and safe workplace for every American worker.

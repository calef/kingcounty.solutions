Sound Generations is a multiservice nonprofit dedicated to supporting older adults in Seattle and King County. We provide essential services including Meals on Wheels, community dining, transportation, health and wellness programs, and caregiver support, ensuring seniors have access to the resources they need to live fulfilling lives.

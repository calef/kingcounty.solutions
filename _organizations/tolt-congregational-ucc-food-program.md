The Tolt Congregational UCC Food Program offers several free options in Carnation. Community meals are served to-go every Wednesday from 4:00–5:30 p.m. The onsite food pantry keeps non-perishable items available for pickup or donation at any time. A weekly food bank runs on Wednesdays 4:00–5:30 p.m., featuring rescued groceries from local stores and produce from nearby farmers—open to anyone in need. The church office can be reached at 425-333-4254 for details or to volunteer.

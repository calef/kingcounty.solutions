The SouthEast Seattle Senior Center (SESSC) provides a vibrant community hub for seniors aged 50+, offering meals, social activities, health and wellness programs, and support services. Our mission is to promote self-sufficient and fulfilling lives for aging adults through diverse recreational and educational opportunities.

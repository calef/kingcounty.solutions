The Phinney Neighborhood Association (PNA) is a community anchor for the Phinney Ridge, Greenwood, and broader northwest Seattle neighborhoods. From its historic campus and Greenwood Senior Center, the PNA runs senior services, PNA Village aging-in-place supports, memory loss programming, kids co-ops, and Whittier Kids before-and-after school care so that families at every stage can tap into trusted neighborhood resources.

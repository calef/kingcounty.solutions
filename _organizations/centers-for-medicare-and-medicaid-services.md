The **Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services (CMS)** administers major federal health insurance programs. Its responsibilities include:

- **Health care:** Medicare for seniors and Medicaid for low-income individuals
- **Financial assistance:** coverage subsidies and insurance regulation
- **Senior services:** long-term and home care supports

CMS expands access to health care and financial protection for vulnerable Americans.

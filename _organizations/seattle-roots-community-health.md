Seattle Roots Community Health provides high-quality, culturally appropriate primary health care to all individuals, regardless of their ability to pay or insurance status. Founded in the 1970s, the organization focuses on patient-centered care, offering services including routine care, behavioral health, and support for navigating housing and food needs.

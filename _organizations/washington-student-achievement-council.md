The **Washington Student Achievement Council (WSAC)** expands access to higher education and lifelong learning. Its work contributes to social services through:

- **Education & Learning:** financial aid, scholarships, and college readiness programs
- **Financial Assistance:** the Washington College Grant for low- and middle-income students
- **Employment & Job Support:** promoting equitable access to career pathways

WSAC supports student success through financial, academic, and outreach initiatives statewide.

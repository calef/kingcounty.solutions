The **Administration for Community Living (ACL)** ensures older adults and people with disabilities can live independently.  
It focuses on:

- **Senior Services:** in-home care, caregiver support, and aging networks  
- **Disabilities:** independent living and assistive technology programs  
- **Community & Social Services:** local partnerships promoting inclusion  

ACL supports independence, dignity, and community participation.

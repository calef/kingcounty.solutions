The **Substance Abuse and Mental Health Services Administration (SAMHSA)** advances behavioral health nationwide.  It supports:

- **Mental Health & Counseling:** crisis support, suicide prevention, and recovery programs
- **Community & Social Services:** integration of behavioral health into local systems
- **Education & Learning:** mental health awareness and prevention initiatives

SAMHSA improves lives through mental health and substance use recovery programs.

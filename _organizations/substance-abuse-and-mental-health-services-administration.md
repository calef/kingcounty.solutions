The **Substance Abuse and Mental Health Services Administration (SAMHSA)** advances behavioral health nationwide. It supports:

- **Mental health and counseling:** crisis support, suicide prevention, recovery programs, and integration of behavioral health into local systems
- **Education and learning:** mental health awareness and prevention initiatives

SAMHSA improves lives through mental health and substance use recovery programs.

HopeCentral provides pediatric primary care and integrated behavioral health services in South Seattle. Focused on building trust, they offer longer appointment times and 24/7 access to pediatricians. The clinic supports families with diverse needs, including those with special needs and low incomes, ensuring care for all regardless of ability to pay.

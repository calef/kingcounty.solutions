The **Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC)** protects public health through research, education, and disease prevention.  Its programs relate to:

- **Health Care:** infectious disease control, prevention initiatives, and emergency preparedness support for health departments
- **Education & Learning:** health literacy and public safety campaigns

CDC works globally and locally to improve population health outcomes.

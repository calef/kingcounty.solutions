The **Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC)** protects public health through research, education, and disease prevention.  
Its programs relate to:

- **Health Care:** infectious disease control and prevention initiatives  
- **Education & Learning:** health literacy and public safety campaigns  
- **Community & Social Services:** emergency preparedness and community health promotion  

CDC works globally and locally to improve population health outcomes.

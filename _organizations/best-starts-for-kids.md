**Best Starts for Kids (BSK)** is a King County initiative that invests in early childhood and youth development.  
It supports community-based programs that promote:

- **Education & Learning:** early learning and school readiness  
- **Community & Social Services:** youth mentorship and after-school programs  
- **Mental Health & Counseling:** youth resilience and prevention services  
- **Family Support:** parenting resources and family stability initiatives  

Best Starts for Kids advances equitable opportunities for every child in King County.

**Best Starts for Kids** is a King County initiative that invests in early childhood and youth development. It supports community-based programs that promote:

- **Education and Learning:** early learning and school readiness
- **Childcare:** youth mentorship and after-school programs
- **Mental Health and Counseling:** youth resilience and prevention services
- **Family Support:** parenting resources and family stability initiatives

Best Starts for Kids advances equitable opportunities for every child in King County.

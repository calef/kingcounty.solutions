The **Department of Transportation (DOT)** ensures safe, accessible, and efficient mobility.  
In the context of social services, it supports:

- **Transportation & Mobility:** paratransit, accessibility programs, and rural transit grants  
- **Community & Social Services:** connecting underserved populations to essential services  

DOT promotes equitable access to transportation nationwide.

NAMI Eastside supports individuals and families affected by mental illness by offering peer-led groups, education, advocacy, and resource connections. Their focus on behavioral health and family support strengthens their mission of serving across service domains, promoting organizational excellence, and adding a strong voice for Eastside human services.

The Washington State Department of Commerce strengthens communities by supporting economic development, affordable housing, infrastructure, and local partnerships. It administers programs that help businesses grow, expand clean energy and broadband access, and improve community resilience. Commerce also manages initiatives for housing stability, homelessness prevention, and energy efficiency, working closely with local governments, tribes, and nonprofits. Its mission is to foster sustainable, inclusive growth and ensure every community in Washington has the opportunity to thrive.

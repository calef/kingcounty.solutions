The **Department of the Interior (DOI)** manages natural resources and supports tribal communities. Its social service functions include:

- **Shelter & Housing:** Bureau of Indian Affairs housing, infrastructure, and health programs that stabilize tribal communities
- **Education & Learning:** schools for Native American students
- **Recreation & Community Programs:** park access and youth conservation initiatives

DOI protects cultural heritage and supports indigenous and rural communities.

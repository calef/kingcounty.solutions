The **Department of the Interior (DOI)** manages natural resources and supports tribal communities. Its social service functions include:

- **Shelter and Housing:** Bureau of Indian Affairs housing, infrastructure, and health programs that stabilize tribal communities
- **Education and Learning:** schools for Native American students
- **Recreation and Community Programs:** park access and youth conservation initiatives

DOI protects cultural heritage and supports Indigenous and rural communities.

Encompass NW partners with families to build healthy foundations for children through early learning, pediatric therapy, and family enrichment programs. Their comprehensive services for children promote developmental health, strengthen families, and support inclusive service frameworks across ages and capacities. Encompass NW is dedicated to ensuring that all children have access to the resources they need to thrive.

Holy Innocents Food Pantry (Holy Innocents Catholic Church) provides weekly groceries and household items to neighbors. Walk-in, choice-based distribution runs Tuesdays 3:00–6:30pm and Wednesdays 10:00am–12:00pm; seniors are served 10:00–11:30am on Thursdays with all clients 11:30am–12:45pm, plus pre-packed pickup Thursdays 1:30–2:30pm. Donations are accepted Mondays 9:00am–12:00pm and other times by arrangement. One visit per household per week; photo ID requested at each visit.

Holy Innocents Food Pantry (Holy Innocents Catholic Church) provides weekly groceries and household items to neighbors. Walk-in, choice-based distribution runs Tuesdays from 3 to 6:30 p.m. and Wednesdays from 10 a.m. to noon; seniors are served from 10 to 11:30 a.m. on Thursdays, with all clients from 11:30 a.m. to 12:45 p.m., plus pre-packed pickup on Thursdays from 1:30 to 2:30 p.m. Donations are accepted Mondays from 9 a.m. to noon and other times by arrangement. One visit per household per week; a photo ID is requested at each visit.

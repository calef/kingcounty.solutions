The City of Seattle is seeking applicants for the Seattle Social Housing Developer Board, a new entity aimed at addressing housing affordability and preventing displacement. This board will oversee the development of social housing projects and ensure they meet community needs. Applicants are encouraged from diverse backgrounds, particularly those with experience in housing development, community organizing, or related fields. The board will play a crucial role in shaping affordable housing strategies and policies, working closely with the city to create inclusive housing solutions. Interested individuals can apply through the city’s official website, with the application process designed to be accessible and straightforward. The formation of this board reflects Seattle's commitment to expanding affordable housing options and fostering community involvement in housing development initiatives.

King Conservation District is requesting qualifications from qualified consultants to prepare an Environmental Critical Area Tree Removal and Vegetation Restoration Application for a forest restoration project at Wolf Creek Ravine in Seattle, WA. This includes a geotechnical report and necessary SEPA checklist and associated reports. Proposals are due by December 10, 2025, with the project work period scheduled from January 1 to March 28, 2026. Interested consultants should submit proposals electronically to Ellen Arnstein, Project Manager.

The Snoqualmie Tribe has announced the funeral services for Tribal Elder Arlene Ventura. The viewing and memorial tribute will be held on November 5th from 4pm to 7pm at Flintoft’s Funeral Home in Issaquah, WA. On November 6th at 10am, the funeral service will take place at the Snoqualmie Casino & Hotel Ballroom, followed by a graveside service at 12:30pm in Fall City Cemetery. The community meal and remembrance program will take place from 2pm to 6pm at the Snoqualmie Casino & Hotel Ballroom.

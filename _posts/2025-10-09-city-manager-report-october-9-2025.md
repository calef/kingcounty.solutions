**Normandy Park City Manager Reports - Oct/Nov 2025**

City Manager Amy Arrington issued a series of reports in October and November 2025. A fall checklist provides guidance on how to prevent flooding and protect local waterways, highlighting the importance of clearing leaves and debris, cleaning gutters and downspouts, and properly disposing of pet waste. A public hearing on November 12 will consider the proposed 2026 property tax levy which will not exceed $1.60 per $1,000 of assessed value. Updates on the City Hall replacement project and plans to improve cell service by modernizing the city's telecommunications regulations were also discussed.

Join the Meals on Wheels team as a Caller in SeaTac! This volunteer position involves making calls to participants and taking their meal orders every Monday morning from 9 am to 12 pm. The role is designed for individuals who are organized, reliable, and looking to make a positive impact in the lives of homebound seniors. Volunteers help maintain the health and independence of these seniors by providing nutritious food and personal contact. This is a long-term commitment, ideal for those who can consistently dedicate their Monday mornings to this rewarding service. If you are interested in this opportunity, please reach out for an application to get started.

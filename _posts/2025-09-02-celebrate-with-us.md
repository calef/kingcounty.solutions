Join Lighthouse Northwest in celebrating their achievements and milestones at their upcoming event. This gathering will honor the hard work of staff, volunteers, and the community while showcasing the impact made over the past year. Attendees can look forward to inspiring stories, updates on ongoing projects, and an opportunity to connect with others who share a passion for the organization's mission. The event will also feature a special keynote speaker who will address important themes relevant to the community. Participants will have the chance to engage in meaningful discussions, enjoy refreshments, and participate in activities that highlight the organization's contributions. Whether you're a long-time supporter or new to Lighthouse Northwest, this celebration promises to foster community spirit and inspire continued collaboration. Save the date and join in recognizing the collective efforts that drive positive change in the community.

The Puget Sound Fire department had a vacancy on the Civil Service Commissioner Board (Position #3). Interested individuals were invited to submit their applications to the Civil Service Secretary. The position was to be filled by RFA Governance Board appointment, with the term lasting 6 years. The notice does not provide a link to requirements, duties, and the application form. As of October 24th, 2023, the position has been filled. Contact information for Puget Sound Fire is provided.

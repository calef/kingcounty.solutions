The Auburn Parks Department oversees nearly 1,000 acres of parkland and more than 20 linear miles of trails. The Auburn Farmers Market operates seasonally from June to September at Les Gove Park, offering fresh produce and local goods. Auburn Parks, Arts & Recreation provides various activities for children to encourage outdoor play. The Auburn Community & Event Center hosts a comprehensive community fitness program, featuring numerous fitness classes, open gym sessions, and access to equipment and weight rooms. Winter recreation activities are available for sign-up, providing residents with opportunities to engage in healthy and fun pursuits during the colder months.

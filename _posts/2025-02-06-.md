The University District Food Bank is open today, February 6, and emphasizes the importance of safe travel for its visitors. The organization is committed to keeping the community informed about any changes to its operational status due to weather conditions. The food bank primarily serves residents from specific Seattle zip codes, but welcomes everyone who needs assistance. Their distribution schedule is as follows: Monday from 9 am to 3 pm, Tuesday from 11 am to 7 pm, Thursday from 11 am to 7 pm, and Friday from 11 am to 4 pm. The food bank is located at 5017 Roosevelt Way NE in Seattle, and they encourage community involvement and support through various programs and volunteer opportunities.

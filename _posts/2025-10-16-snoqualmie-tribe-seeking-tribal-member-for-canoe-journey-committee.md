The Snoqualmie Tribe is seeking a tribal member to join the Canoe Journey Committee. This committee assists in the planning and execution of the annual Inter-Tribal Canoe Journey, an event that takes place at different hosting tribe locations every summer and requires year-round preparation. The chosen committee member will hold the position for either two or three years. Interested applicants must apply and, if shortlisted, interview for the role.

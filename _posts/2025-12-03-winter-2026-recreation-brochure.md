The Winter 2026 Recreation Brochure for Seattle Parks and Recreation is now available, featuring a variety of programs and activities for the upcoming winter season. The brochure includes information on classes, events, and recreational opportunities for all ages, such as sports leagues, fitness programs, and art classes. Participants can explore winter-themed activities like ice skating and holiday events. Registration for programs opens on December 12, 2025, and residents are encouraged to sign up early to secure their spots. The brochure also highlights new offerings, as well as returning favorites, ensuring that there is something for everyone. For those interested in outdoor activities, the brochure provides details on winter hikes and nature programs. Additionally, it emphasizes the importance of community engagement and encourages residents to connect with their local parks. The Winter 2026 Recreation Brochure is designed to promote health, wellness, and community involvement through diverse recreational opportunities during the winter months.

CAMFED has been named a beneficiary of The New York Times Communities Fund for the third consecutive year. Recognized for its grassroots-led approach, CAMFED is the sole international nonprofit among this year's selected organizations. The initiative focuses on empowering girls in rural African communities, helping them access education, achieve success, and become future leaders who can guide the next generation. The recognition highlights the impact of CAMFED's efforts in breaking the cycle of poverty through education. Additionally, recent donations from supporters have further fueled the organization's mission, emphasizing the importance of community involvement in fostering change.

  Good news for Fresh Bucks customers – there’s now another vibrant option to shop just south of Seattle. The Skyway Farmers Market, held in the heart of the Skyway community, offers an exciting opportunity to access fresh, local produce while supporting BIPOC farmers and grassroots food justice efforts.

  Born out of the Urban Food Systems Pact at the height of the COVID-19 pandemic in 2020, the Skyway Farmers Market was created to address food access needs in this diverse, multiracial community. What started as a community-driven response has grown into a monthly event that continues to nourish both local residents and community spirit.&nbsp;

   ![Skyway Farmers Market & Bazaar](https://www.seattlefreshbucks.org/wp-content/uploads/2025/04/Skyway-Market-Logo.png "Skyway-Market-Logo")

  Beginning May 25<sup>th</sup> and running through October, the market will operate on the 2nd and last Sunday of each month, from 11 a.m. to 2 p.m., at the Skyway Resource Center parking lot (12610 76th Avenue South). Here, shoppers can find a wide selection of fresh fruits and vegetables, along with edible plant starts to grow their own food at home.&nbsp;

  Fresh Bucks are accepted, along with SNAP/EBT, making the market an accessible and affordable option for many families. With Fresh Bucks and the SNAP Market Match program, customers can stretch their food budgets even further, maximizing the value of their benefits while supporting local farmers.&nbsp;

  Whether you’re looking to fill your fridge with fresh produce, start a garden, or connect with your community, the Skyway Farmers Market is the place to be this season.&nbsp;

  Mark your calendars for the 2025 market dates and come experience the flavor and community that Skyway has to offer:&nbsp;

  - May 25
  - June 8 and 29
  - July 13 and 27
  - August 10 and 31
  - September 14 and 28
  - October 12 and 26

Good news for Fresh Bucks customers in South Seattle: The Skyway Farmers Market is now accepting Fresh Bucks, providing a new option for shopping. Located in the Skyway community, this market supports BIPOC farmers and grassroots food justice initiatives. Established in response to food access challenges during the COVID-19 pandemic, the market has evolved into a monthly event that fosters community spirit.

Starting May 25 and continuing through October, the market will be open on the second and last Sunday of each month, from 11 a.m. to 2 p.m. at the Skyway Resource Center parking lot. Shoppers will find a variety of fresh fruits, vegetables, and edible plant starts for home gardening. The acceptance of Fresh Bucks, along with SNAP/EBT, makes it an accessible and affordable shopping option for families.

The Fresh Bucks and SNAP Market Match programs allow customers to maximize their food benefits while supporting local agriculture. The market dates for 2025 include May 25, June 8 and 29, July 13 and 27, August 10 and 31, September 14 and 28, and October 12 and 26.

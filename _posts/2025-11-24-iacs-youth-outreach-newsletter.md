On December 14, from 1 to 3 PM, a planning and kickoff project for the YO Drive will take place at the Together Center in Redmond. This event aims to mobilize volunteers and engage the community in efforts supporting youth outreach initiatives. Further details about participation and activities will be available for those interested in contributing to the project.

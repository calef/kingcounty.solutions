An interactive workshop will be held to cover accessibility features on Apple and Android devices, focusing on settings that assist users with hearing, vision, speech, and other impairments. Participants will receive guidance on customizing their mobile devices for easier use. The workshop is free of charge and is scheduled for Wednesday, December 31, from 1:00 pm to 2:00 pm in the Exercise Room at the Shoreline-LFP Senior Activity Center, located at 18560 1st Ave NE, Bldg G, Shoreline, WA. To register, attendees are encouraged to call the Reception Desk. The center's hours are Monday to Thursday from 8:30 am to 4:00 pm and Friday from 8:30 am to 1:00 pm.

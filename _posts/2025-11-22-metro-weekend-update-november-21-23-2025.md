Routes 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 13, 14, 21, 24, 27, 28, 33, 40, 62, 124, 131, 132, and the RapidRide C, D, E, and H Lines will experience service impacts due to the closure of 3rd Avenue & Virginia Street from 7 p.m. on Friday, November 21 to 5 a.m. on Monday, November 24 for construction. Additionally, Route 14 will have specific stops closed on S Jackson Street from 7 a.m. on Saturday, November 22 to 8 p.m. on Sunday, November 23. Route 49 will be rerouted in the Eastlake area during the same weekend, while Route ST 522 will be rerouted off Beardslee Boulevard from 1 a.m. on Saturday to 4 a.m. on Monday. Route ST 545 will also be rerouted off Olive Way from 6 a.m. on Saturday to 6 p.m. on Sunday. Riders are encouraged to use Metro and Link light rail to access local sporting events. The Water Taxi has transitioned to its winter schedule, and the Seattle Center Monorail will operate varying hours throughout the weekend. Text for Departures service is available for real-time updates on bus departures. Alerts for King County Metro service can be customized for specific routes.

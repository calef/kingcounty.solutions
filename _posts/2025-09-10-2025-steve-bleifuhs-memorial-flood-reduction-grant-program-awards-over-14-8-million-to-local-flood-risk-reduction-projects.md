The King County Flood Control District has announced the recipients of the 2025 Steve Bleifuhs Memorial Flood Reduction Grant Program, awarding $14.8 million to 35 projects across the county. These projects, aimed at reducing flood risks and improving community resilience, include culvert replacements, coastal erosion solutions, and green stormwater infrastructure. The grant program, named in honor of floodplain management leader Steve Bleifuhs, supports flood risk reduction, habitat restoration, and infrastructure protection. In 2025, the program received 37 grant requests totaling over $30 million, funding seven projects fully and 28 projects partially.

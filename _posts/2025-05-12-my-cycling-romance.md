Antonio Zebedeo-Abad shares his lifelong relationship with cycling, highlighting the practical and personal benefits of biking. He recalls his journey from riding a toddler tricycle to owning various bikes, ultimately settling on a Bike Friday Pocket Llama, which he customized for touring. His cycling lifestyle is a family affair, with his children also engaging in long-distance cycling adventures. 

Zebedeo-Abad emphasizes the growth of cycling during the COVID-19 pandemic, as people sought healthier, eco-friendly transportation options. He recounts lessons learned from his travels, including an eye-opening experience in Spain where he discovered the convenience of biking over driving, especially in navigating small cities. His extensive planning for a pilgrimage through Spain showcases his dedication to cycling, as he prepared his bike with necessary enhancements and selected routes that honored his cultural heritage.

As he reflects on his experiences, he expresses a commitment to maintaining an active lifestyle through cycling, blending travel, culture, and fitness. Zebedeo-Abad looks forward to future adventures, considering renting bikes for travel in South America, while cherishing the moments shared with his family on their cycling journeys.

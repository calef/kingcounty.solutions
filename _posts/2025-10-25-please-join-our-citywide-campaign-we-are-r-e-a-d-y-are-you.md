The Auburn Parks Department oversees nearly 1,000 acres of parkland and more than 20 miles of trails, providing residents with various outdoor recreational opportunities. The Auburn Farmers Market operates seasonally from June to September at Les Gove Park, offering locally sourced goods. The Auburn Community & Event Center hosts a comprehensive Community Fitness Program featuring numerous classes, open gym access, and facilities for weight training. In addition, the city is launching a campaign titled "WE ARE R.E.A.D.Y. – ARE YOU?" to promote community engagement and readiness.

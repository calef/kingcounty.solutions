An armed felon was arrested after ramming a stolen car into a police vehicle in Seattle, injuring two officers. The incident occurred when officers attempted to stop the suspect, who had a history of violent crime and was wanted on multiple warrants. After the suspect refused to stop, he accelerated towards the police vehicle, resulting in a collision. The injured officers were treated for non-life-threatening injuries. Following the crash, the suspect attempted to flee on foot but was apprehended after a brief chase. He faces several charges, including assault and possession of a stolen vehicle. The Seattle Police Department emphasized the danger posed by individuals with a history of violent offenses and the importance of swift action in such situations to ensure community safety.

We appreciate our members who contribute to making The Center for Active Living a vibrant community. Your participation creates an inviting atmosphere filled with community spirit. Many members also serve as volunteers and donors, providing essential time and financial support that enables various programs and services at The Center. Your engagement ensures our offerings remain dynamic and meaningful. Together, we have fostered an environment where individuals can explore new activities, maintain an active lifestyle, and build connections with one another. The staff at The Center extends heartfelt thanks and wishes everyone happy holidays.

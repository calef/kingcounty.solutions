This week, we published 15 posts highlighting significant community initiatives and updates across King County. From mental health advocacy to infrastructure improvements, these stories reflect ongoing efforts to enhance the well-being and engagement of our residents.

### Spotlight stories
- **[Honoring Our 2025 Mental Health Champions: Senator Manka Dhingra and Congressman Adam Smith](https://nami-eastside.org/honoring-our-2025-mental-health-champions-senator-manka-dhingra-and-congressman-adam-smith/)**: Recognized by NAMI Eastside, Senator Dhingra and Congressman Smith have made substantial contributions to mental health care in Washington. Their advocacy for patient-centered care and insurance parity underscores a commitment to accessible mental health services.
- **[How would you spend $100,000 to improve Tukwila?](https://www.tukwilawa.gov/how-would-you-spend-100000-to-improve-tukwila-submit-your-ideas-for-the-peoples-project/)**: Tukwila residents are invited to submit ideas for small improvements in the city as part of the People’s Project. This participatory budgeting initiative empowers the community to influence local development from October 1 to November 15.
- **[October is Breast Cancer Awareness Month](https://snoqualmievalleyhealth.org/october-is-breast-cancer-awareness-month/)**: Snoqualmie Valley Health emphasizes the importance of early detection during Breast Cancer Awareness Month, promoting advanced screening technologies like 3D mammography to aid in accurate diagnoses.

### Mental Health Advocacy and Recognition
This week, the focus on mental health was highlighted by the recognition of **[Senator Manka Dhingra and Congressman Adam Smith](https://nami-eastside.org/honoring-our-2025-mental-health-champions-senator-manka-dhingra-and-congressman-adam-smith/)** as champions in the field. Their advocacy for improved behavioral health systems and patient-centered care is crucial for enhancing access to mental health services. Additionally, the **[University of Washington Master of Social Work student](https://www.vrfa.org/2025/10/university-of-washington-master-of-social-work-student-joins-vrfa-cares-team/)** joining the VRFA CARES Team reflects a commitment to providing support for low-income individuals seeking behavioral health services.

### Community Engagement and Improvement Initiatives
Tukwila's **[People’s Project](https://www.tukwilawa.gov/how-would-you-spend-100000-to-improve-tukwila-submit-your-ideas-for-the-peoples-project/)** invites residents to propose ideas for utilizing $100,000 for community enhancements, fostering local engagement in decision-making. Meanwhile, the **[Yarrow Point Town Council](https://yarrowpointwa.gov/2025/10/notice-town-council-regular-meeting-october-14-2025-400-pm-town-hall/)** will hold a regular meeting on October 14 to discuss critical topics, including the 2026 Property Tax Levy and budget considerations.

### Health Awareness and Services
In recognition of **[Breast Cancer Awareness Month](https://snoqualmievalleyhealth.org/october-is-breast-cancer-awareness-month/)**, Snoqualmie Valley Health is promoting the importance of early detection through advanced screening technologies. Additionally, they offer personalized treatment options for headaches, addressing a common health concern among residents as detailed in their post about **[headache treatments](https://snoqualmievalleyhealth.org/got-headaches-discover-personalized-treatment-options-at-snoqualmie-valley-health/)**.

### Environmental and Infrastructure Updates
The completion of the **[Momb Revetment 2020 Repair Project](https://kingcountyfloodcontrol.org/protecting-homes-and-roads-momb-revetment-2020-repair-project-completed/?utm_source=rss&utm_medium=rss&utm_campaign=protecting-homes-and-roads-momb-revetment-2020-repair-project-completed)** enhances flood protection for the Cedar Grove community. The **[City Manager Report](https://normandyparkwa.gov/city-news/city-manager/city-manager-report-october-9-2025/)** from Normandy Park also outlines efforts to prevent flooding and improve local infrastructure.

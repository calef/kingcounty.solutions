The City Council of Tukwila, Washington, is seeking applications for the Pro Tem Position 6. Eligibility requires applicants to be registered voters in Tukwila and residents for at least one year prior to appointment. The appointed individual will serve until the incumbent returns, resigns, or until the certification of the November 2027 General Election. Applicants cannot hold the Pro Tem position and any other public office or employment within the City of Tukwila government simultaneously. Applications must be completed, signed, and received no later than 5:00 p.m. on Friday, August 15, 2025.

Kiara Porter, an artist at SEEDArts Studios, blends mixed media visuals with 3D printing to push the limits of contemporary aesthetics. Her work fosters a dialogue between the humanities, agriculture, and science, creating a narrative that connects cultural memory with scientific innovation. Through her artistic practice, Porter aims to reimagine sustainable futures, using aesthetics as a means to bridge these diverse fields.

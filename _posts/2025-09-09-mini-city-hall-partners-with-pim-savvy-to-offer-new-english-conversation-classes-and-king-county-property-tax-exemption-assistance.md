  <p>By Janelle Wetzstein<br>Community Development Public Information Officer</p>



  <p>To provide residents with additional tools and support, Mini City Hall is excited to announce new free services supporting King County property tax exemption applications and conversational English language classes.</p>



  <p>Property tax exemption support is especially valuable for qualifying homeowners particularly seniors and people with disabilities on fixed incomes. During dedicated office hours, residents can receive step-by-step support to help them understand the process, fill out the correct paperwork and submit applications. This one-on-one guidance is designed to make the process less overwhelming and ease financial burdens for those who qualify.</p>



  <p>The new English language conversational services provide a welcoming space for community members to build confidence and skills in everyday communication. Free classes are offered twice a month virtually and in-person, making it easy for participants to engage in a way that works best for them. Sessions are relaxed and friendly, with opportunities to practice real-life conversations, ask questions and connect with others who are also new to the area. Facilitators help participants to feel comfortable and encouraged. Classes will be from 10:15-11 a.m., on the second and fourth Wednesday of each month. As space is limited, early registration is encouraged.</p>



  <p>Located inside Crossroads Shopping Center, Bellevue’s Mini City Hall provides convenient access to city services, information and community programs in multiple languages, ensuring all residents feel supported and connected. Through its partnership with PIM Savvy, Mini City Hall has been able to meet people where they are, offering resources in a familiar, welcoming and accessible setting.</p>

Mini City Hall has partnered with PIM Savvy to introduce new free services, including assistance with King County property tax exemption applications and conversational English classes. The property tax exemption support is particularly beneficial for qualifying homeowners, such as seniors and individuals with disabilities on fixed incomes, providing step-by-step guidance to simplify the application process. The English language classes aim to help community members enhance their everyday communication skills in a welcoming environment. These classes will be held virtually and in-person twice a month, allowing participants to engage in a manner that suits them best, with sessions designed to foster a friendly atmosphere for practicing conversations. Classes are scheduled for the second and fourth Wednesday of each month from 10:15 to 11 a.m., with early registration recommended due to limited space. Located in Crossroads Shopping Center, Mini City Hall offers convenient access to city services and community programs in multiple languages, ensuring all residents feel supported. This partnership enables Mini City Hall to better serve the community by providing resources in an accessible and familiar setting. For more information, residents can contact Mini City Hall directly.

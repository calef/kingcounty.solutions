This article lists various free Thanksgiving meal events in the Seattle area, providing details on time, location, and offerings. 

1. **Bread of Life Mission** - Serving turkey, ham, and sides on November 27th from 11:30 a.m. to 1:30 p.m.
2. **The Alajawan Brown Foundation** - Free dinner with games at Talley High School on November 27th from 11 a.m. to 2 p.m.
3. **The Hall at Fauntleroy** - 28th Annual Free Thanksgiving Dinner on November 27th from 12 p.m. to 3 p.m., no RSVP needed.
4. **Lil Red’s Catering and Takeout** - Community meal on November 27th from 11 a.m. to 3 p.m.
5. **Rainier Community Center** - Free community dinner on November 26th from 6 p.m. to 9 p.m.
6. **St. James Cathedral Kitchen** - Thanksgiving dinner on November 27th from 3 p.m. to 4 p.m., first come, first served.
7. **Stand Tall Outreach** - Offering meals and warm clothing on November 27th from 10 a.m. to 2 p.m. at Bailey Gatzert Elementary School.
8. **The Salvation Army – Greenwood** - Free meal on November 27th from 11 a.m. to 1 p.m.
9. **Phinney Neighborhood Association** - Thanksgiving potluck on November 27th from 4 p.m. to 6 p.m., RSVP recommended. 

Transportation assistance is available for those in need.

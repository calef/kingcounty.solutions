  <p><strong>Internship and Work-Study Programs </strong></p>



  <p>Our Internship and Work-Study programs welcomed outstanding students from the University of Washington and Seattle University this season. These emerging leaders brought fresh energy, creativity, and a strong sense of purpose to El Centro de la Raza—supporting critical projects that advanced our mission across multiple departments.</p>



  <p>Thanks to their contributions, we were able to expand our reach, strengthen our community connections, and enhance the effectiveness of our programming. Our Communications and Digital Media Interns played a key role in developing the marketing strategy for the El Centro Skate Rink, elevating our customer service efforts, and managing dynamic social media content that increased public engagement.</p>



  <p>Our Procurement Intern contributed directly to the success of our signature event, the <em>Building the Beloved Community Gala</em>, by supporting the auction process—helping secure in-kind donations, organizing items, and ensuring a seamless experience for guests and donors alike.</p>



  <p>Their work has had a lasting impact, not only on the programs they supported but also on the lives of the individuals and families we serve every day.</p>

El Centro de la Raza recently welcomed exceptional students from the University of Washington and Seattle University into their Internship and Work-Study programs. These students contributed significantly to various projects, enhancing El Centro's mission across multiple departments. Their engagement allowed the organization to expand its outreach, strengthen community ties, and improve programming effectiveness. 

Notably, the Communications and Digital Media Interns were instrumental in creating a marketing strategy for the El Centro Skate Rink, which improved customer service and boosted social media engagement. Additionally, the Procurement Intern played a vital role in the success of the Building the Beloved Community Gala by managing the auction process, securing in-kind donations, and ensuring a smooth experience for all guests and donors. 

The interns' efforts have had a lasting influence on both the programs they supported and the individuals and families served by El Centro de la Raza, reflecting their commitment to community betterment. The organization expresses heartfelt gratitude for the support received, underscoring the importance of these contributions to their mission.

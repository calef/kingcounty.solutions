  During her 19 years at Boyer, Dottie touched the lives of hundred of families. Thank you, Dottie, for all you’ve done to make our world a better, brighter and more compassionate place for all.

We celebrate the life and legacy of Dottie Pence, a beloved former Teachers Aide at Boyer Children's Clinic, who served from 1980 to 1999. Dottie was a cherished partner to families and staff, known for her kindness and ability to connect with others. Former colleague Patti Price recalls how Dottie's friendship made a significant impact on her when she joined Boyer, highlighting Dottie's remarkable gift for comforting babies and children. Price expresses a desire to honor Dottie's memory by embodying her compassionate spirit through acts of kindness and advocacy. Throughout her 19 years at Boyer, Dottie positively influenced the lives of countless families, leaving a lasting impression of care and compassion. Her contributions are deeply appreciated and continue to inspire others to make the world a better place.

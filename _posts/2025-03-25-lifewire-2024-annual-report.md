Nearly 7,000 survivors of domestic violence in nine counties of Western Washington received vital support in 2024, thanks to the generosity of donors, corporate sponsors, and volunteers. This assistance has provided survivors with safety, stability, and healing. The LifeWire 2024 Annual Report highlights the significant impact of this support through stories of resilience, notable milestones, and families working toward brighter futures. The gratitude expressed by the LifeWire team emphasizes the importance of community partnerships in facilitating these life-changing outcomes for survivors.

The September-October 2025 newsletter from Ballard Senior Center highlights various activities and events for seniors. Upcoming programs include fitness classes, arts and crafts sessions, and social gatherings aimed at fostering community engagement. Special events this month feature a fall festival with seasonal activities and a wellness fair offering health screenings and resources. The center encourages participation in regular exercise programs, emphasizing their importance for physical and mental well-being.

Additionally, the newsletter introduces new staff members who will support the center's mission to provide quality programs for seniors. There are updates on ongoing volunteer opportunities, inviting community members to contribute their time and skills. The center also shares success stories from participants who have benefited from its programs, showcasing the positive impact on their lives.

The newsletter provides information on transportation services available to seniors, ensuring accessibility to the center and its events. Lastly, it reminds readers to stay updated by following the center on social media and subscribing to future newsletters for the latest news and offerings.

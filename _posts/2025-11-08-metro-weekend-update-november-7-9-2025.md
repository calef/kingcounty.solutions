Route updates for King County Metro services are scheduled for the weekend of November 7-9, 2025. Route 181 will be rerouted off E Main Street in Downtown Auburn on Saturday, November 8, from 5 a.m. to 4 p.m. for the Veterans Day Parade. Additionally, Route 22 and RapidRide C Line will detour off 44th Avenue SW from Saturday, November 8, at 7 a.m. to Sunday, November 9, at 5 p.m. due to construction. ST 522 to Woodinville will be rerouted off I 405 and SR 522 from Friday, November 7, to Monday, November 10, nightly from 11 p.m. to 4 a.m., and ST 554 to Issaquah will be rerouted off the I-90 floating bridge from Friday, November 7, at 10 p.m. to Saturday, November 8, at 6 a.m.

Transit options are available for attending sporting events throughout Seattle, including the Seahawks game at Lumen Field on Sunday at 1:05 p.m. The Water Taxi is on a winter schedule, and the Seattle Center Monorail will operate during limited hours over the weekend. Riders can also utilize the Text for Departures service for real-time updates on departures.

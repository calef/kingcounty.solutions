The Tukwila City Council has appointed Joe Torres Camacho as Councilmember pro tem for Position 6. Joe was officially sworn in on September 2, 2025. The council had announced its search for a temporary council member for Position 6, with the term of office to last until the incumbent returns, resigns, or the November 2027 General Election is certified. Applications were accepted until August 15, 2025. The pool of applicants verified as eligible by King County Elections included Ziggy Samra, Steven Miller, Tim Collins, Krysteena Mann, Feven Gebrekristos, Gina Bernhardt Nielsen, Peggy McCarthy, Sarah Gollus, Kate Kruller, and Apneet Sidhu.

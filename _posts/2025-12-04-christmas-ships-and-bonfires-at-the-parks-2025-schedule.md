The 2025 Christmas Ships and Bonfires event in Seattle will feature a festive schedule of activities across various parks. The Christmas Ships, a beloved local tradition, will showcase a parade of decorated boats on multiple evenings, with specific dates and locations announced for public gatherings. Families and friends can enjoy bonfires at designated parks, providing a cozy atmosphere to celebrate the holiday season. Each bonfire event will include music and entertainment, enhancing the festive experience. Participants are encouraged to bring their own seating and refreshments to enjoy by the fire. The schedule is designed to allow community members to come together, share in the holiday spirit, and create lasting memories. Detailed event times and locations will be made available as the date approaches, ensuring everyone can partake in the seasonal fun.

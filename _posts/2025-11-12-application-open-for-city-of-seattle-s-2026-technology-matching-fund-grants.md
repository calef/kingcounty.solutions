The City of Seattle has opened applications for the 2026 Technology Matching Fund (TMF) grants, offering up to $45,000 for non-profits and community groups focused on closing the digital divide and promoting digital literacy. A total of $455,000 is available this year, with a community match requirement of 25% of the requested funding. Eligible projects include digital navigator services, digital literacy training, and internet connectivity initiatives. Applications are due by January 13, 2026, with information sessions scheduled for November 20 and December 8 to assist applicants.

The City of Seattle has opened applications for the 2026 Technology Matching Fund (TMF), offering matching grants up to $45,000 for nonprofit organizations and community groups focused on closing the digital divide and enhancing digital literacy. A total of $455,000 is available for eligible projects, which must include a community match of 25% of the requested funding. Application submissions are due by January 13, 2026, with winners notified by the end of March 2026. Information sessions will be held to assist applicants in understanding the process.

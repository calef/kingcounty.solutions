Walmart is partnering with Operation Homefront for the third consecutive year through its Spark Good program to support military families. From November 3 to November 30, customers can round up their purchases at checkout on Walmart.com and the Walmart app to contribute to Operation Homefrontâ€™s mission of creating strong, stable military and veteran families. Walmart will match these customer donations at a rate of 2:1, up to $2 million. This initiative reflects Walmart's commitment to honoring veterans, a principle rooted in the company's history dating back to founder Sam Walton, a World War II veteran. The Walmart Foundation focuses on improving employment, education, and well-being for military families. To date, the Spark Good Round Up program has raised over $5 million for Operation Homefront, which provides critical services to prevent short-term needs from becoming long-term struggles for military families. Operation Homefront emphasizes its gratitude for Walmart's ongoing support, highlighting the significant impact this partnership has on families who have served the nation.

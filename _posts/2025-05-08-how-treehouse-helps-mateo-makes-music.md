In honor of National Foster Care Month, Treehouse highlights the story of Mateo, a senior who has committed to pursuing a degree in music education at a Washington university. Initially uncertain about his future during his junior year, Mateo found common ground with a Graduation Success Education Specialist, who shared his passion for music. This connection helped shift Mateo's focus towards collegiate music programs. With support from Just-in-Time Funding, he enrolled in Running Start at a local community college and participated in his first drum corps summer tour.

The 2025 RBC Race for the Kids took place on June 8, 2025, at Magnuson Park, drawing over 450 participants and volunteers. The event achieved a record-breaking fundraising total of $67,053, which will directly benefit children and families served by Boyer Children's Clinic. The community's enthusiastic engagement was highlighted as a key aspect of the event's success. The organizers expressed gratitude to RBC, the title sponsor, along with other sponsors, for their generous support that made the race possible. Race times were mentioned to be available for participants to check.

The Department of Education and Early Learning (DEEL) hosted the "Thrive & Vibe: A Youth Health and Wellness Celebration" on October 25 at El Centro de la Raza’s Centilia Cultural Center. The event aimed to provide Seattle youth with access to free mental wellness resources and featured various activities such as performances, a resource fair, and wellness workshops. Seahawks legend Ray Roberts delivered an inspirational message about the importance of seeking help for mental health. Attendees engaged in fitness activities, mindfulness sessions, and creative workshops, emphasizing personal empowerment and community building. Partner organizations, including Celebrating Roots and The Good Foot Arts Collective, showcased their programs supporting youth development through arts and mentorship. Participants received wellness swag giveaways, including special items from the Seattle Seahawks. The event highlighted the significance of mental health support for youth aged 13 to 24, encouraging them to reach out for help when needed. For additional resources on mental health care options, attendees were directed to visit the city’s youth mental health website.

The Seattle Department of Education and Early Learning (DEEL) hosted a Wellness Fair aimed at supporting youth in the community. This event provided a variety of resources and activities designed to help young people thrive. Attendees had access to mental health services, physical health resources, educational support, and community engagement opportunities. The fair featured interactive workshops, wellness activities, and information booths from local organizations. DEEL emphasizes the importance of holistic well-being for students, recognizing that mental and physical health significantly impacts academic success. The event also fostered connections among families, educators, and community partners, promoting a collaborative approach to youth development. By offering a platform for discussion and resource sharing, the Wellness Fair aimed to empower Seattle's youth to build resilience and improve their overall quality of life.

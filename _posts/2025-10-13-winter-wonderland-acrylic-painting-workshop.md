Local artist Laura Brewer will conduct a winter wonderland acrylic painting workshop at the Shoreline-LFP Senior Activity Center. Participants will receive step-by-step guidance to create their own winter scene. The workshop is scheduled for Monday, December 15, from 12:00 pm to 3:00 pm in the Craft Room. The cost to attend is $40 for members and $45 for non-members. Interested individuals should call the Reception Desk to register. The center is located at 18560 1st Ave NE, Bldg G, Shoreline, WA 98155, and operates Monday through Thursday from 8:30 am to 4:00 pm, and Friday from 8:30 am to 1:00 pm.

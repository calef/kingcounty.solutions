The Town of Yarrow Point is looking for a resident to fill a vacancy on the Planning Commission. This volunteer position will complete an unexpired term ending December 31, 2028. The Planning Commission serves as an advisory body to the Town Council, providing guidance on the community's future growth. Responsibilities include attending monthly meetings at 6:00 p.m. on the third Tuesday, reviewing and making recommendations on various topics such as planning, land use, building development, zoning, middle housing, short-term rentals, and affordable housing. The appointed commissioner will also represent the interests of Yarrow Point residents. Interested individuals or referrals can contact Mayor Katy Harris at mayor@yarrowpointwa.gov or 425-941-5289.

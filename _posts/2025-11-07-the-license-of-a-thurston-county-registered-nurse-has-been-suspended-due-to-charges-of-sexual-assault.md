The Washington State Department of Health has suspended the license of Thurston County registered nurse Bradley Alan Ermey due to allegations of repeatedly sexually assaulting a vulnerable adult. This suspension prevents Ermey from practicing as a nurse in Washington, prioritizing patient safety and public health. He has 20 days to contest the charges through a hearing request. Legal documents regarding the case are available online, and individuals can report any concerns about health care providers to the Department of Health. The agency regulates and ensures the quality and safety of health care in Washington.

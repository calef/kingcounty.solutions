Notice is given for a special meeting of the Town Council of Yarrow Point on November 20, 2025, at 4:00 PM at Town Hall. The agenda includes a discussion on Flock LPR Operations. The Town has transitioned to hybrid meetings, allowing attendance both in-person and virtually. Individuals wishing to speak during the meeting must register with the Deputy Clerk by 2:00 PM on the day of the meeting, either by calling 425-454-6994 or via email. Public comments can also be submitted by email or regular mail to the town's address. The meeting will be accessible online through Zoom, with details provided for joining. Attendance is encouraged.

Praisealujah offers essential support services, including food, housing, and recovery programs, to individuals in need. The organization aims to expand its outreach to assist more people by encouraging donations. Community engagement is fostered through various events, such as the monthly "Church Beyond the 4 Walls" which features a free drive-thru food giveaway and praise and worship sessions. These events take place on the first Sunday of every month at the Les Schwab Tire Center in Burien. Additional Sunday services are held at the New Beginnings Christian Fellowship Church, including worship and an "Hour of Power." Weekday morning classes offer praise and worship followed by inspirational sessions led by peer counselors and program graduates. Furthermore, Praisealujah provides hot meal services to the public every weekday afternoon, aiming to deliver nutritious meals to the community. The organization encourages individuals to stay connected through various platforms and participate in its diverse programs and events.

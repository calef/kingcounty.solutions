  <p><em><b>Grand opening celebration marks Vibrant’s first Staten Island presence, expanding access to services for families and youth.</b></em></p>
  <p><b>NEW YORK, NY – September 26, 2025 – </b><a href="http://www.vibrant.org"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Vibrant Emotional Health</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;">, one of the nation’s leading behavioral health organizations, announced the grand opening of its new Family and Youth Peer Support Services (FYPS) Community Center in Staten Island, New York. This milestone marks Vibrant’s first program in the borough and represents a major step forward in creating a community-rooted, resilience-building system of care for local families. </span></p>
  <p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Located at </span><b>120 Stuyvesant Place</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">, the Staten Island center builds on Vibrant’s growing network of community-based programs,</span></p>

  <p><span style="font-weight: 400;">creating new opportunities to meet rising demand for youth and family peer-led support and empowerment services across New York City. </span></p>
  <p><span style="font-weight: 400;">The grand opening celebration drew proclamations from New York State Senator Jessica Scarcella-Spanton and New York City Councilmember Kamilah Hanks, along with community partners, families, and local leaders. Guests attended an open house that highlighted the wide range of services designed to strengthen resilience among Staten Island youth and families. </span></p>
  <p><span style="font-weight: 400;">“The Staten </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Island FYPS office is the physical embodiment of what’s possible when we put people first. When we lead with care. When we trust those who’ve walked the road themselves to help guide others forward,” </span><b>said Brenda Tong, Chief Program Officer of Vibrant Emotional Health. “</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">Vibrant holds peer-led work as a core component of what real community support looks like. It’s my firm belief that lived experience is a kind of expertise that can’t be taught; it’s earned. And when that expertise is honored, invested in, and brought into the center of our systems, we see transformation. We see youth who feel empowered. We see families who feel connected. We see communities that begin to heal from within.”</span></p>
  <p><span style="font-weight: 400;">“I’m so honored to support and recognize the outstanding service to our great city and state, and for the great contributions that FYPS is bringing to our community,” </span><b>said New York State Senator Jessica Scarcella-Spanton.</b><span style="font-weight: 400;"> “A great state is only as great as those individuals who perform exemplary service on behalf of their community and contribute positively to that environment.” </span></p>
  <p><b>Councilmember Kamilah Hanks added</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">, “Thank you to Vibrant and the FYPS program. A city’s greatness is defined by the people who dedicate themselves to serving their communities. Whether through unique, personal achievement in their professional or other endeavors or simply through a lifetime of philanthropy and community service, we have seen that achievement today.”  </span></p>
  <p><b>Meeting a Critical Need in Staten Island</b></p>
  <p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Vibrant’s FYPS program pairs peer advocates with lived experience alongside families and youth navigating emotional and behavioral challenges. Services include:</span></p>
  <ul>
  <li style="font-weight: 400;" aria-level="1"><span style="font-weight: 400;">One-on-one peer support for young people and caregivers </span></li>
  <li style="font-weight: 400;" aria-level="1"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Parenting workshops and youth leadership groups</span></li>
  <li style="font-weight: 400;" aria-level="1"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Referrals to essential services, including counseling, educational support, and housing assistance</span></li>
  </ul>
  <p><span style="font-weight: 400;">With early intervention, FYPS helps families build the skills and confidence to better support their children.</span></p>
  <p><span style="font-weight: 400;">The Staten Island launch expands Vibrant’s footprint across New York City, joining existing FYPS centers in the Bronx and Queens. By investing in borough-based services, Vibrant is strengthening its community mental health care, bridging crisis response with long-term culturally informed support. </span></p>
  <p><span style="font-weight: 400;">To learn more about the FYPS program, visit </span><a href="https://www.vibrant.org/what-we-do/community-programs/parents-caregivers/family-youth-peer-support-services/"><span style="font-weight: 400;">vibrant.org/what-we-do/community-programs/parents-caregivers/family-youth-peer-support-services</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;">.</span></p>

Vibrant Emotional Health has opened a new community center on Staten Island, expanding its reach in mental health services. This center aims to provide accessible support to the local community, addressing the growing need for mental health resources. The facility will offer a variety of programs, including counseling, workshops, and support groups tailored to the unique needs of Staten Islanders. Vibrant's mission is to enhance emotional well-being and ensure that individuals have access to the necessary tools for mental health care. The opening of the center is part of a broader initiative to improve mental health services across New York City, highlighting the importance of community-based support systems. Local leaders and community members expressed enthusiasm for the new center, which is expected to make a significant impact on the mental health landscape in the area. Vibrant Emotional Health continues to advocate for mental health awareness and accessibility, striving to create a supportive environment for all.

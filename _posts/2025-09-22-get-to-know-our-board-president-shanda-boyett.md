Shanda Boyett serves as the board president of FamilyWorks Seattle, bringing a wealth of experience in leadership and community engagement. With a background in social work, she is passionate about supporting families and advocating for mental health resources. Boyett is dedicated to fostering a collaborative environment within the organization, emphasizing the importance of teamwork and connection among board members and staff. Her vision includes expanding FamilyWorks' reach and impact within the community, ensuring that families have access to essential services and support. Outside of her role at FamilyWorks, Boyett enjoys spending time with her family and engaging in outdoor activities, reflecting her belief in the importance of work-life balance. She encourages others to get involved with FamilyWorks, highlighting the positive difference that community support can make in the lives of families facing challenges. Boyett’s leadership style is characterized by empathy and a commitment to inclusivity, striving to create a welcoming space for all individuals seeking assistance. Her ongoing efforts aim to strengthen FamilyWorks’ mission and enhance the well-being of families throughout Seattle.

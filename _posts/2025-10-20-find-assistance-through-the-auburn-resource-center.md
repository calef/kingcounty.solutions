Auburn Parks, Arts & Recreation manages nearly 1,000 acres of parkland and over 20 linear miles of trails. The Auburn Farmers Market operates seasonally from June to September each year at Les Gove Park. The Auburn Community & Event Center offers a comprehensive Community Fitness Program featuring numerous classes, open gym access, and a well-equipped weight room. Additionally, the Auburn Resource Center, located at 2814 Auburn Way N, provides assistance to community members facing various challenges. The Resource Center is dedicated to helping individuals in need by offering a range of support services.

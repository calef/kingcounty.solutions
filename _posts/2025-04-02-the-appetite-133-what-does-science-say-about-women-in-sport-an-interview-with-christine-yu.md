Christine Yu's book, "Up to Speed: The Groundbreaking Science of Women Athletes," challenges long-held beliefs about women's athletic capabilities, emphasizing that despite a male-centric model, women have achieved remarkable feats in sports. In a recent episode of The Appetite, Kara Bazzi, Opal’s Director of Exercise+Sport, interviews Yu, who shares insights on the intersection of gender and sport. Their discussion highlights the need for better information and understanding of gender differences in athletics, aiming to support all athletes regardless of their experiences. Yu's work focuses on disseminating essential knowledge and acknowledging the ongoing efforts required to improve the landscape for female athletes. The conversation underscores the importance of access to information in empowering women in sports.

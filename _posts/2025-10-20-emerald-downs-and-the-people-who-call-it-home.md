The Auburn Parks Department oversees nearly 1,000 acres of parkland and more than 20 miles of trails, providing ample recreational opportunities for residents. The Auburn Farmers Market operates seasonally from June to September at Les Gove Park, offering local produce and goods. The Auburn Community & Event Center hosts a variety of fitness programs, including numerous classes, open gym sessions, and access to equipment and weight rooms, encouraging community engagement and healthy lifestyles.

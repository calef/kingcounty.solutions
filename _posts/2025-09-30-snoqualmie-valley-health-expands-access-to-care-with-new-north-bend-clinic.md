  **SNOQUALMIE, WA** – Snoqualmie Valley Health is proud to announce the opening of its new North Bend Clinic, expanding access to care for residents across the Valley. The clinic will welcome its first patients on Monday, October 20, 2025., with a Grand Opening Celebration to be announced.&nbsp;

  Located in the former Chase Bank Building at 712 SW Mt Si Blvd, North Bend, WA 98045, the clinic features six exam rooms designed to serve the growing needs of the community.

  “The North Bend Clinic is an important step forward in our mission to inspire wellness for life’s great adventures,” said Renee K. Jensen, CEO of Snoqualmie Valley Health. “By bringing more care closer to home, we’re making it easier for patients to stay healthy and keep doing the things they love.”

  The clinic will launch with primary care services led by Dr. Shantal Postiglione, DNP, ARNP a trusted nurse practitioner dedicated to whole-person care and long-term patient relationships. In the months ahead, the clinic will also host rotating specialty services, bringing regional expertise to the rural community and further increasing access to healthcare close to home.

  “I am honored to be part of this exciting new chapter for Snoqualmie Valley Health,” said Dr. Shantal Postiglione, DNP, ARNP. “My passion is building strong, long-term relationships with patients, and I look forward to serving families across the lifespan in North Bend with compassionate, accessible care.”

  A patient recently shared about their care experience, “Dr. Postiglione takes my concerns seriously. She shows compassion for my health and my decisions about my health. She is knowledgeable and flexible in meeting my needs.”

  The new clinic reflects Snoqualmie Valley Health’s ongoing commitment to expand healthcare options, reduce travel barriers, and ensure families can access high-quality, compassionate care right here in the Valley.

  For more information about Snoqualmie Valley Health and its services, please visit [SnoqualmieValleyHealth.org](https://snoqualmievalleyhealth.org/).

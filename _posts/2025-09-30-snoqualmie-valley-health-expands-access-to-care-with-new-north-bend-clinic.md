Snoqualmie Valley Health has opened a new clinic in North Bend, Washington, located at the former Chase Bank Building. The clinic features six exam rooms and aims to expand access to care for residents across the Valley. It launched with primary care services led by Dr. Shantal Postiglione, a nurse practitioner. In the future, the clinic plans to host rotating specialty services to increase access to healthcare in the rural community.

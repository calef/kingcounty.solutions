  **NORTH BEND, WA** – Snoqualmie Valley Health announces that the North Bend Clinic is now open and accepting new patients, expanding convenient access to trusted, high-quality care for residents of the upper Snoqualmie Valley.

  The new clinic brings comprehensive primary care to North Bend, along with a growing lineup of rotating specialty services. Pediatric care is available every Tuesday, Neurology every Monday, and additional specialties will be added in the coming months to meet the growing needs of the community.

  “Our goal is to bring care closer to where people live and make it easier for families to get the services they need,” said Renée K Jensen, CEO. “The North Bend Clinic is an important step in expanding access and delivering exceptional care throughout the Valley.”

  To celebrate the opening, Snoqualmie Valley Health hosted a grand opening event with community members, partners, and staff. Guests enjoyed refreshments, toured the new clinic, met providers, learned more about available services, and joined a ribbon-cutting ceremony to mark the start of patient care at the new location.

  The North Bend Clinic is accepting new patients for primary care and specialty appointments. Patients can schedule [online](https://snoqualmievalleyhealth.org/appointments/) or by calling 425-831-2313.

  For more information about the North Bend Clinic and SVH services, please visit SnoqualmieValleyHealth.org.

Snoqualmie Valley Health has opened a new clinic in North Bend, which is now accepting new patients. This facility aims to enhance healthcare access for the local community and provide comprehensive services. The clinic will offer a range of medical services, including primary care, behavioral health, and preventive care. With a focus on patient-centered care, the clinic employs a team of experienced healthcare professionals dedicated to meeting the diverse health needs of the community. Patients can expect personalized care tailored to their individual health goals. The new clinic is part of Snoqualmie Valley Health's ongoing commitment to improving health outcomes and expanding healthcare options in the Snoqualmie Valley area. Community members are encouraged to schedule appointments and learn more about the services available.

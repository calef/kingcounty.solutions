You're invited to join Lighthouse Northwest at their annual gala, set for Saturday, March 11, 2023, at 6:00 PM. The event will take place at the historic 1912 Building in Tacoma, featuring a night of celebration, community, and support for Lighthouse's mission. Guests can look forward to a gourmet dinner, inspiring stories, and live entertainment. The evening aims to raise funds to support Lighthouse's programs that empower individuals facing mental health challenges. Attendees will have the chance to participate in a silent auction, offering unique items and experiences. Tickets are available online, and various sponsorship opportunities exist for businesses and individuals looking to contribute further. Your participation will help Lighthouse continue its important work in the community, providing resources and support to those in need. Join in to make a difference and enjoy a memorable evening with like-minded individuals dedicated to fostering mental health awareness and support.

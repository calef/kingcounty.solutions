The November-December 2025 newsletter from Ballard Senior Center highlights upcoming events and activities designed for seniors in the community. Key events include the annual Thanksgiving dinner, which will be held at the center, inviting members to gather and celebrate the holiday together. Additionally, the newsletter features various classes and workshops, such as art, fitness, and technology, aimed at enhancing skills and promoting social interaction among participants.

There is also a focus on health and wellness, with information on upcoming health screenings and flu shots available to seniors. Community resources and services are highlighted, encouraging seniors to take advantage of local support systems. The newsletter emphasizes the importance of staying engaged and active during the colder months, providing details on regular social events, game nights, and seasonal celebrations.

Members are encouraged to participate in the various offerings and to contribute their ideas for future programs. The center aims to foster a welcoming environment where seniors can connect, learn, and thrive. Overall, the newsletter serves as a comprehensive guide to the center's offerings for the months of November and December.

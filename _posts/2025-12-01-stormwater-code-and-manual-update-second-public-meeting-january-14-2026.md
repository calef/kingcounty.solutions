The City of Seattle is hosting the second public meeting to discuss updates to the Stormwater Code and Manual on Jan. 14, 2026. This meeting aims to gather community feedback on proposed changes and improvements to stormwater management practices. Participants will have the opportunity to review draft updates and provide their input. The updates are intended to enhance the city's approach to stormwater management, ensuring better environmental protection and compliance with regulatory requirements. Information about the updates and the meeting will be shared through various channels, allowing residents to stay informed and engaged. The city encourages public participation to ensure that the new code reflects community needs and priorities.

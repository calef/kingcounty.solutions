  **This week on the Eastside September 9, 2024**

  September is National Suicide Prevention Month and this week is suicide prevention week. Congratulations to everyone that attended Saturday’s [safeTALK Suicide Prevention training](https://www.facebook.com/share/2T9Gg9kkBhY9YzoP/) at Together Center and thank you to the cities of Bothell and Kenmore for recognizing the importance of this week at their city council meetings. If you’re looking for additional knowledge and experience when it comes to suicide prevention, mark your calendar for our upcoming Mental Health First Aid class November 9-10 from 10 a.m. to 3 p.m. on both days. Overlake & their partners also have free resources available this month for suicide prevention education and youth mental health. Find links to QPR Suicide Prevention training on the 18th, Youth Mental Health First Aid on the 23rd and more at [overlakehospital.org/classes/](https://www.overlakehospital.org/classes/search?prod_events_algolia%5BrefinementList%5D%5Bevent_type%5D%5B0%5D=Mental%20%26%20Behavioral%20Health)

September is National Suicide Prevention Month, and various events were held in the week of September 9-24, 2024, to raise awareness and provide resources for suicide prevention. These included safeTALK Suicide Prevention training and the recognition of the importance of the week by the cities of Bothell and Kenmore. The NAMI Eastside offered several support group meetings, Mental Health First Aid classes, and other resources like free suicide prevention education and youth mental health resources provided by Overlake and their partners. A series of events were also scheduled for the rest of September and October, focusing on mental health and well-being.

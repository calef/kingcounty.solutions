  FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE&nbsp;

  **Essentials First Remains Committed Amid Federal Funding Uncertainty** &nbsp;

  Essentials First is deeply concerned about the [recent federal aid freeze](https://www.nbcnews.com/politics/donald-trump/white-house-orders-freeze-federal-aid-focus-trumps-conservative-agenda-rcna189552) announced through a memo from the White House Office of Management and Budget. If enacted, this decision will pause the disbursement of federal financial assistance across various programs, which raises significant uncertainty about its impact on vulnerable communities. While the memo notes exceptions for Social Security, Medicare, and direct payments to individuals, there remain many unknowns regarding how this freeze will affect other essential programs, including those like SNAP and EBT, which millions of families rely on for basic needs.&nbsp;

  At Essentials First, our mission remains unchanged: to provide equitable access to hygiene products and other necessities for those who need them most. Regardless of the federal government’s decisions, our doors are open, and we are committed to supporting individuals and families during these uncertain times.&nbsp;

  We will continue to monitor developments closely and advocate for policies that protect the dignity and well-being of all people. Essentials First stands ready to step in where gaps may emerge to ensure that everyone has access to the essential items they need to live with dignity.&nbsp;

  Please feel free to visit our website for assistance as we await further details on the implications of this policy.&nbsp;

  In solidarity,&nbsp;

  Essentials First&nbsp;

Essentials First expresses deep concern over a recently announced federal aid freeze by the White House Office of Management and Budget. This freeze will pause disbursement of federal financial assistance across various programs, creating uncertainty for vulnerable communities. While Social Security, Medicare, and direct payments to individuals are exempt, the effects on essential programs like SNAP and EBT, which support millions of families, remain unclear. Despite these challenges, Essentials First remains committed to its mission of providing equitable access to hygiene products and other necessities. The organization emphasizes that it will continue to support individuals and families during this uncertain period, monitoring developments closely and advocating for policies that protect the well-being and dignity of all. Essentials First stands ready to address any emerging gaps in assistance to ensure that everyone has access to essential items needed for a dignified life.

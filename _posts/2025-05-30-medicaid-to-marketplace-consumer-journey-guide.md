The Medicaid to Marketplace Consumer Journey Guide provides a comprehensive overview of the transition from Medicaid to Marketplace coverage. This resource is designed as a tip sheet to assist consumers navigating changes in their health insurance. It highlights key steps and considerations for individuals moving from Medicaid to Marketplace plans. The guide emphasizes the importance of understanding coverage options, eligibility requirements, and enrollment processes. It aims to facilitate a smooth transition for consumers, ensuring they are informed about their choices and the implications of switching coverage. This resource is particularly relevant for those who may experience changes in their income or household circumstances that affect their Medicaid eligibility. The guide is part of ongoing efforts to support consumers in accessing affordable health care options.

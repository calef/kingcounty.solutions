  of sheltering women in crisis – she built a community of care, compassion, and giving.

  not only shelter but also dignity, support, and hope. Because of Helen’s dedication,

  that serves hundreds of women every day, guiding them toward stability and independent
  living.\n\nHer legacy lives on through an incredible network of women – staff, volunteers,
  case managers, donors, and community members – who devote their time, energy, and
  resources to uplifting others. The [Support Network](https://sophiaway.org/strong-roots/),
  a group of women who have met every month since 2010, embodies this spirit of generosity
  and commitment. They provide meals, collect essential items, and inspire their congregations
  to give selflessly. Their support extends beyond donations; it is a testament to
  the power of community and the belief that when women come together, we can change
  lives.\n\nThe National Women’s History Project has declared the theme of Women’s
  History Month 2025 as “Moving Forward Together! Women Educating and Inspiring Generations”
  – a theme that perfectly reflects the essence of The Sophia Way. Every day, we witness
  the power of mentorship, leadership, and education. The[women we serve](https://sophiaway.org/women-we-serve/)
  come from diverse backgrounds and face immense challenges, yet they show remarkable
  resilience. Despite their struggles, they support one another, offering comfort,
  understanding, and friendship to those who share their journey. Many women who once
  sought shelter return years later, staying connected to the community that supported
  them. The relationships they build – with one another and with case managers – endure
  long after they transition to stable housing.\n\nThe impact of The Sophia Way extends
  beyond the women we directly serve. Our donors and supporters, many of whom have
  been with us for years, continue to invest in the well-being of women in need. Their
  generosity fuels our mission, ensuring more women can find [shelter, safety, and
  stability](https://sophiaway.org/about/).\n\nWe celebrate every woman who has shaped

The Sophia Way, a non-profit organization that supports and empowers homeless women, celebrates Women's History Month by honoring extraordinary women who have been part of its journey. Founded by Helen Leuzzi, the organization has grown from a small non-profit into a vital resource that serves hundreds of women daily, guiding them towards stability and independent living. The Sophia Way's work extends beyond providing shelter, it builds a community of care, compassion, and support. The organization's impact goes beyond the women it directly serves, thanks to donors and supporters who fuel its mission.

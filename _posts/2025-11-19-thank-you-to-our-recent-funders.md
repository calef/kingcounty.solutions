Juma Ventures expresses gratitude for the generous support received in the recent quarter, highlighting how this funding enables youth to achieve personal and social change. Through these contributions, young individuals can earn an income, acquire career skills, and enhance their financial capabilities. Key funders mentioned include the T-Mobile Foundation, Liberty Mutual Foundation, BlackRock, and Bank of America Foundation, along with several individuals such as Kyle Bernhard, Adriane Armstrong, and Jim Hansen. This support plays a crucial role in empowering youth and fostering their development within the community.

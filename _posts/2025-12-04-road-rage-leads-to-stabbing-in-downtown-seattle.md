A road rage incident in downtown Seattle escalated into a stabbing on December 3, 2025. The confrontation began when two drivers engaged in a heated argument after a traffic-related dispute. The altercation intensified, leading one driver to exit their vehicle and confront the other. Witnesses reported that during the exchange, one individual was stabbed. Emergency services arrived at the scene and transported the victim to a local hospital with serious injuries. Law enforcement officials are currently investigating the incident and seeking any additional witnesses to gather more information. Authorities urge anyone with details about the confrontation to come forward as they work to determine the circumstances surrounding the stabbing.

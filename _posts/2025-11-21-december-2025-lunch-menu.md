The December 2025 lunch menu features a variety of meals designed for community members. Key events during the month include the Jazz Nutcracker Outing on December 6, and a presentation titled "Travels with Anita - Road Trip USA" scheduled for December 30. Additionally, a Purple Ambassadors Bingo event will take place on November 25, and a Holiday Lights Outing is planned for December 23. These activities are organized to engage the community and provide enjoyable experiences for seniors. The menu and events aim to foster social interaction and participation among attendees throughout the holiday season.

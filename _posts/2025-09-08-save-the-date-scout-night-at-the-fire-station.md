The Valley Regional Fire Authority (VRFA) is hosting a Scout Night at the Fire Station on October 29th, from 5:30 -8:30 PM at Station 33 in Lakeland Hills, Auburn, WA. The event is open to all scouting/adventure groups and offers activities to assist in meeting badge requirements including first aid, CPR/AED awareness, knot tying, disaster preparedness, home fire escape planning, and forensics. Activities range from 10-15 minutes, with a few up to 20 minutes. Registration can be done by emailing group details to public.info@vrfa.org. Another similar event is being held by Puget Sound Fire Authority on September 29th at their Station 35 in Kent.

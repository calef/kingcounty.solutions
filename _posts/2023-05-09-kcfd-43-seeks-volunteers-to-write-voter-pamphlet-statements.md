King County Fire District #43 is seeking volunteers to write voter pamphlet statements for a proposition to be submitted to voters on August 1, 2023. The proposition involves restoring the District's property tax levy rate to $1.50 per $1,000 of true and assessed valuation and establishing a limit factor of up to 6% for the next five years. Interested residents are invited to join the pro and con committees for the voter pamphlet statements. They should contact District Secretary Jessica Steward by 4:00 pm on Friday, May 12, 2023. Committee appointments must be submitted to the King County Elections Department by the same date.

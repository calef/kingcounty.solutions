In this edition, you’ll meet Erica, whose journey to safety and stability was powered by your generosity, relive the joy of our 1980s-themed World of Hope Gala and LifeWire&#8217;s Holiday Shop, and learn about exciting new partnerships helping survivors and their pets.</p>

  <p>Just as Erica was about to begin school and build a new life for herself and her two daughters, her former partner &#8211; whom they had escaped &#8211; found them. The safety risks were so severe that she knew her only option was to move across the country, staying with her brother while they searched for a new community to put down roots. Thanks to the generosity of our partners and supporters, LifeWire was able to step up and provide crucial assistance.</p>



  <p>We navigated the complex process of transferring her Section 8 housing voucher to a new city, secured a new birth certificate, and assisted her in getting approved for a new home. LifeWire also provided support during their long journey. Today, Erica and her children are safely housed and thriving in their new community, embracing their fresh start.</p>

  <p>On October 26th, LifeWire&#8217;s <strong><mark style="background-color:rgba(0, 0, 0, 0);color:#793581" class="has-inline-color">World of Hope Gala &amp; Auction</mark></strong> transported more than 300 supporters, advocates, and change-makers back to the 1980s. It was a Prom Night extravaganza celebrating our work offering hope, stability, and healing. Big hair, neon outfits, and incredible generosity filled the room as attendees and sponsors raised more than <strong><mark style="background-color:rgba(0, 0, 0, 0);color:#7a3783" class="has-inline-color">$312,000</mark></strong> to support survivors of domestic violence and their families. Every dollar will make a meaningful difference.</p>



  <p>Guests had a blast while making a difference for survivors and their families, enjoying new experiences like <strong><mark style="background-color:rgba(0, 0, 0, 0);color:#783680" class="has-inline-color">Amazon</mark></strong> Blast from the Past Packs, the <strong><mark style="background-color:rgba(0, 0, 0, 0);color:#783780" class="has-inline-color">Bank of America</mark></strong> Time Machine, and LifeWire Radio by <strong><mark style="background-color:rgba(0, 0, 0, 0);color:#793781" class="has-inline-color">Intracorp</mark></strong>,<strong> </strong>as well as bidding on incredible auction packages and gourmet desserts. Special thanks to our sponsors, dedicated volunteers, Event Committee members, and everyone who made this event unforgettable.</p>



  <p>Don&#8217;t miss next year&#8217;s <strong><mark style="background-color:rgba(0, 0, 0, 0);color:#793581" class="has-inline-color">World of Hope Gala &amp; Auction</mark></strong> on <strong><mark style="background-color:rgba(0, 0, 0, 0);color:#7a3683" class="has-inline-color">September 13th, 2025</mark></strong> at Meydenbauer Center—we can’t wait to see you there! For more information on sponsorship, auction donations, or volunteering, contact Patrick Martin, Director of Community Relations, at <a href="mailto:patrickm@lifewire.org">patrickm@lifewire.org</a>.</p>

  <p>The holiday season shines brightest when it’s filled with joy—and at LifeWire, joy includes gifts! This year, our Holiday Shop brought smiles and warmth to <strong><mark style="background-color:rgba(0, 0, 0, 0);color:#773580" class="has-inline-color">111 parents</mark> </strong>and their <strong><mark style="background-color:rgba(0, 0, 0, 0);color:#793682" class="has-inline-color">245 children</mark></strong>.   <p>As one participant shared with their LifeWire advocate, <mark style="background-color:rgba(0, 0, 0, 0);color:#76357e" class="has-inline-color">&#8220;Last year we had a very difficult holiday.   <p>LifeWire is thrilled to announce our <strong><mark style="background-color:rgba(0, 0, 0, 0);color:#793682" class="has-inline-color">RedRover</mark></strong> Safe Housing grant, supporting survivors of domestic violence and their pets. This funding tackles a critical issue: ensuring survivors never have to choose between their safety and leaving beloved animals behind.</p>



  <p>Our new Hope Starts Here apartment complex is LifeWire&#8217;s, and one of the region&#8217;s, first pet-friendly domestic violence shelters, allowing survivors to stay with their pets as they rebuild their lives.</p>



  <p>The grant enables us to provide essential pet care, including:</p>



  <ul class="wp-block-list">
  <li>Discounted routine veterinary care (vaccinations, spaying/neutering).</li>



  <li>Pet deposits at pet-friendly hotels.</li>



  <li>Emergency vet services.</li>



  <li>Supplies like food, crates, and leashes.</li>
  </ul>



  <p>Our partnership with <strong><mark style="background-color:rgba(0, 0, 0, 0);color:#793581" class="has-inline-color">Seattle Humane</mark></strong> strengthens this work by offering affordable veterinary services, boarding services, and staff training to meet the unique needs of survivors and their pets. </p>



  <p>For many, pets are more than companions—they&#8217;re lifelines. Thanks to <strong><mark style="background-color:rgba(0, 0, 0, 0);color:#783680" class="has-inline-color">RedRover</mark></strong> and <strong><mark style="background-color:rgba(0, 0, 0, 0);color:#783680" class="has-inline-color">Seattle Humane</mark></strong>, LifeWire can ensure safety, stability, and healing for survivors and their furry family members.</p>

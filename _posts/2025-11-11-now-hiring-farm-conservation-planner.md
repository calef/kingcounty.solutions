King Conservation District is hiring a Farm Conservation Planner. The role involves providing technical assistance to farmers and land managers to promote best management practices (BMPs) and protect natural resources. Responsibilities include on-site farm visits, writing Farm Conservation Plans with a focus on regenerative agriculture, promoting BMPs through a cost-sharing program, managing projects, preparing educational materials, hosting farm tours and webinars, and giving presentations. The Farm Conservation Planner will work directly with farmers and land managers. Interested candidates are asked to email a resume, cover letter, and personal equity statement to HR@kingcd.org.

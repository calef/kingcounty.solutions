The King County Fire Marshals have implemented a Stage 2 Burn Ban effective August 15, 2023. All types of outdoor fires are prohibited under this ban, including recreational campfires, pellet fireplaces, cooking fires, and ceremonial fires. For more information, refer to the Outdoor Burning page of Puget Sound Fire.

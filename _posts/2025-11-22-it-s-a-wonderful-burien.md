It’s a Wonderful Burien returns for the holiday season, celebrating community, local businesses, and the arts. The event features the 2025 Winter Light Display in Town Square Park, running from November 12 to December 31, illuminating the park every night. The Burien Winter Market operates Thursdays from November 6 to December 18, offering unique holiday gifts and locally made foods. Key events include "The Past, A Present Yet to Come" at Burien Actors Theatre from November 28 to December 21, with tickets ranging from $5 to $25. On November 29, enjoy the Mimosa Walk and the "Santa Claus is Coming to B-Town" event, where donations benefit the Highline Area Food Bank. The Nutcracker ballet will be performed on December 4-6, and various seasonal celebrations like “Tis the Season” and “ChristmasSounds” showcase local dance and music talent. Additional events include the Cookie Crawl on December 6, La Posada on December 13, and festive bazaars. Burien invites everyone to shop local and partake in the joyful holiday spirit throughout the season.

CCIIO and the FTC have issued a partnership letter regarding compliance guidance. This resource, dated January 1, 2025, includes a dynamic list of information related to the partnership. The letter emphasizes the cooperation between the Centers for Consumer Information and Insurance Oversight (CCIIO) and the Federal Trade Commission (FTC) to enhance regulatory compliance and consumer protection. The document serves as guidance for agents and brokers involved in health insurance marketplaces. Additionally, downloadable resources are available, including the signed final version of the partnership letter and related follow-up correspondence. This initiative highlights the commitment to maintaining standards and ensuring that consumers are well-informed and protected in their interactions with health insurance providers.

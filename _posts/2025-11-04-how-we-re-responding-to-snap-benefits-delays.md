FamilyWorks Seattle is addressing delays in SNAP benefits, which have affected many families relying on this critical assistance. The organization is actively working to support clients who are experiencing these disruptions. They are providing information and guidance on how to navigate the challenges associated with delayed benefits. In addition, FamilyWorks is offering food resources and assistance to ensure that families do not go hungry during this period of uncertainty. They encourage affected individuals to reach out for help and provide a clear path for accessing available resources. The organization emphasizes the importance of community support and remains committed to helping families through these difficulties while advocating for timely SNAP benefit distribution.

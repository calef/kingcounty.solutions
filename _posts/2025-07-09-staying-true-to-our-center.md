Six months into 2025, MSC reaffirms its commitment to community-centered service amid policy challenges. With over 50 years of experience, MSC pledges to continue supporting the health and well-being of the community, standing in solidarity with partner organizations and maintaining its core values. The agency provides vital services, including a market-based food bank that serves over 1,500 households weekly through various delivery methods. Additionally, MSC assists with employment, shelter, housing, utility and heat assistance, as well as services for immigrants, refugees, seniors, and individuals with disabilities in long-term care. The collective effort to uplift neighbors enhances the community for all. Despite uncertainties in the world, MSC remains dedicated to helping those in need, upholding human dignity and compassion. The organization expresses gratitude to its partners and supporters who contribute to this essential work.

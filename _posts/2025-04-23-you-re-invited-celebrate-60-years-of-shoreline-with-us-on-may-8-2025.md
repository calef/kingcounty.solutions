Join Shoreline Community College on May 8, 2025, from 3:00–7:00 p.m. for a 60th Anniversary Open House Celebration. This free, family-friendly event will honor students, alumni, staff, faculty, and community members who have contributed to the college's legacy since its founding in 1964. The celebration will feature live performances and films by students, interactive demonstrations, recreational activities like pickleball and basketball, and engaging mini lectures by faculty. Attendees can explore various college programs, apply, and register for classes. Food options will be available from local food trucks, along with light refreshments, raffle prizes, and giveaways. Shoreline Community College, which has served thousands of students from over 65 countries, is committed to inclusive learning and student success. The event aims to reflect on the past, engage with the present, and inspire a bright future. RSVPs are encouraged, so bring a friend and come celebrate!

Establishing trust is vital for effective dental care and enhancing patient experiences. A new course for dental professionals offers strategies to foster meaningful, trust-based relationships with patients through a patient-centered approach. Participants will learn the importance of empathy, active listening, clear communication, and respectful understanding in the patient-dental professional relationship. The course objectives include identifying practices that build trust and rapport, utilizing inquiry-based tools to engage patients in decision-making, and creating a dental environment that emphasizes respect, empathy, and individualized care. 

Dr. Marcqwon Daywalker, an integrated family medicine physician and lifestyle medicine specialist, presents the course. He serves as the Executive Vice President of Clinical Services and Chief Medical Officer at Avenue 360 Health & Wellness and has extensive experience in trauma-informed community medicine. His past roles include Chief Health Equity and Wellness Officer at AccessHealth Community Health Centers, where he implemented programs to mitigate burnout during the COVID-19 pandemic. Dr. Daywalker is active in various health committees and has presented at numerous conferences. The course offers 1.5 continuing dental education credits and is recognized by the ADA CERP.

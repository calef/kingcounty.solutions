We share with prayers and sadness the passing of beloved Tribal Elder Irene Daniels, who passed away on November 23, 2025, at the age of 85. Irene was born on February 4, 1940. In accordance with tribal policy, flags are flying at half-staff as a mark of respect. Additional information regarding her passing will be shared when it becomes available. The Snoqualmie Tribe is mourning this significant loss, reflecting on Irene's contributions and legacy within the community.

Detectives are investigating a homicide that occurred at a hookah lounge in Seattle. The incident took place late on November 30, 2025, when officers responded to reports of a disturbance. Upon arrival, they found a man with gunshot wounds inside the lounge. Despite life-saving efforts by emergency medical personnel, the victim was pronounced dead at the scene. Witnesses reported hearing multiple gunshots and observed individuals fleeing the area. Officers canvassed the neighborhood and collected evidence, including shell casings. The Seattle Police Department is urging anyone with information about the incident or the individuals involved to come forward. This marks the latest in a series of violent incidents in the area, prompting concerns about safety and crime in Seattle's nightlife.

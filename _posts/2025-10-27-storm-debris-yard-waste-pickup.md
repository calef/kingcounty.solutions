The City of Normandy Park has announced a storm debris yard waste pickup service for Single-Family Residence Customers who subscribe to Yard Waste service. On October 28 and November 11, 2025, these customers can include up to 96 additional gallons of storm debris with their regular yard waste at no extra charge. The storm debris, which includes branches less than 4 inches in diameter, limbs, and leaves, can be placed in extra garbage cans labeled "Yard Waste", kraft paper yard bags, or bundled using degradable string or twine. Bundles should be up to two feet in diameter by four feet in length and no more than 55 pounds. For more information, contact 855-672-7596 or visit the city's website.

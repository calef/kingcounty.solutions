Notice is given for a regular meeting of the Town Park Commission on Wednesday, November 19, 2025, at 7:00 PM at Town Hall. The agenda includes discussions on the Planting Plan and Budget for the corner of 92nd and Points Drive, updates on benches, and a Morningside update. The Town of Yarrow Point has transitioned to hybrid meetings, allowing participation in person at Town Hall or virtually online or by phone. Those wishing to speak live must register with the Deputy Clerk by 2:00 PM on the day of the meeting. Public comments can also be submitted via email or regular mail to the Town of Yarrow Point. For virtual attendance, participants can join on a computer or mobile app, or call in for audio-only participation. Meeting details, including the link and passcode for online access, are provided. Attendance is encouraged.

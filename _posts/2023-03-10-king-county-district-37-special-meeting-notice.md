King County Fire Protection District 37 will hold a Special Meeting on Wednesday, March 15, 2023, starting at 4:30 p.m. The meeting will take place at Fire Station 78, located at 17820 SE 256th St. Covington, WA 98042. Attendees also have the option to join the meeting virtually via Zoom. The meeting will address the posted agenda items. Contact Puget Sound Fire at 20811 84th Avenue S Kent, WA 98032, phone number (253) 856-4300, or email fire@pugetsoundfire.org for more information.

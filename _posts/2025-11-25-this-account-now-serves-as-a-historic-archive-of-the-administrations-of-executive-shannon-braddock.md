This account now operates as a historic archive for the administrations of Executive Shannon Braddock and Executive Dow Constantine. It is no longer active or monitored. In line with public records law, the content will remain accessible for transparency and historical reference. The public is encouraged to stay engaged with King County's ongoing initiatives by following other official accounts.

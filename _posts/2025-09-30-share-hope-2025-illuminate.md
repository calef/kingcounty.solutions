Join the community for Share Hope 2025, a special evening dedicated to supporting survivors of interpersonal violence on Vashon Island. This event, themed around healing and giving, invites attendees to enjoy a warm gathering as autumn sets in. Guests will have the opportunity to partake in food and drinks while celebrating DOVE's holistic approach to healing and hope. Cocktail attire is encouraged to enhance the evening's elegance. Tickets are available for purchase, with a 20% discount offered for those who buy before October 5th using the promo code EARLY. Don't miss this chance to be part of a meaningful journey with the community.

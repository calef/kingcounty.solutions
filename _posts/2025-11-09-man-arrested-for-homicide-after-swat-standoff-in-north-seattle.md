The Seattle police SWAT team arrested a 51-year-old man for homicide following a standoff in North Seattle. Patrol officers initially responded to reports of threats involving a knife at 11:45 a.m. on North 84th Street. After negotiations failed, SWAT officers took the man into custody without further incident.

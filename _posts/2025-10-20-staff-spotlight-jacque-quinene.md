Jacque Quinene serves as the Accounting Manager at Aging Wisdom, where she focuses on streamlining systems and improving cash flow. With a background in tech, her transition to Aging Wisdom was inspired by personal experiences caring for her mother, which ignited her passion for making a meaningful impact in the field of aging. Since joining, Jacque has successfully reduced the accounts receivable balance and enhanced team efficiency through better processes, including a significant payroll transition to BambooHR that lowered administrative time by 30%. Her role has evolved from handling daily tasks to designing more efficient workflows, aligning closely with her desire to support the team in delivering compassionate client care. Jacque values the collaborative spirit and shared purpose of her colleagues, which she believes enhances the organization's mission. She finds motivation in creating impactful changes that facilitate smoother operations and improve the experience for both clients and staff. Outside of work, Jacque enjoys outdoor activities like snowboarding and mountain biking, reflecting her enthusiastic, people-oriented approach to her professional role.

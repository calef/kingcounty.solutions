The Snoqualmie Tribe has announced the distribution of tickets for the PWHL and Seattle U basketball games at Climate Pledge Arena. Tickets will be sent electronically to the email addresses on file and require a Ticketmaster account for access. Tribal members who are unable to attend their games are encouraged to notify the Tribe via email.

The winners for the upcoming games include:

- Desiree Jai Weese: Torrent vs. New York Sirens on Dec 3, 2025
- Tylynn Burns and Thomas Zackuse: Torrent vs. Ottawa Charge on Dec 17, 2025
- Olivia Johnston: Torrent vs. Montreal Victoire on Dec 23, 2025
- Elisa Slowey: Torrent vs. Boston Fleet on Jan 18, 2026
- Suzanne Sailto and Megan Roy: Torrent vs. Toronto Sceptres on Feb 27, 2026
- WillaMae Madsen and Troy Wyatt: Torrent vs. Boston Fleet on Mar 11, 2026
- Summer Larson: Torrent vs. Vancouver Goldeneyes on Apr 18, 2026
- Johnathan Barker, Jon Jacobson, and Autumn Liner-Sanders: Torrent vs. Montreal Victoire on Apr 25, 2026
- Seattle U vs. UW on Dec 19, 2025
- Seattle U vs. WSU on Dec 30, 2025
- Seattle U vs. Gonzaga on Jan 17, 2026

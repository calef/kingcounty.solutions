The Town Council of Yarrow Point will hold a regular meeting at Town Hall on October 14, 2025, at 4:00 PM. The meeting will be conducted in a hybrid format, both in-person and virtual. Topics to be discussed include the Critical Areas Ordinance, the 2026 Property Tax Levy, the 2026 Preliminary Budget, and a Verizon lease agreement. A public hearing will address potential increases in the tax levy. Those wishing to speak should register their request with the Deputy Clerk before 2:00 PM on the day of the meeting. Attendees can join the meeting via Zoom or phone.

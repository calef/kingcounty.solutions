The Town Council of Yarrow Point will hold a regular meeting at Town Hall on Oct. 14, 2025, at 4 p.m. The meeting will be conducted in a hybrid format, both in-person and virtual. Topics to be discussed include the Critical Areas Ordinance, the 2026 property tax levy, the 2026 preliminary budget, and a Verizon lease agreement. A public hearing will address potential increases in the tax levy. Those wishing to speak should register their request with the deputy clerk before 2 p.m. on the day of the meeting. Attendees can join the meeting via Zoom or phone.

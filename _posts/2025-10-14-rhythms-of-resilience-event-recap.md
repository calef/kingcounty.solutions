On October 4, Casa Latina hosted the Rhythms of Resilience Gala, celebrating the strength and commitment of the Latino immigrant workforce. The event featured guest speakers Claudia Castro Luna, Councilmember Alexis Mercedes Rinck, and community leader Lizbeth, who shared inspiring stories and insights. The gala successfully raised $143,637, aimed at enhancing the well-being and empowerment of Latino immigrants. Casa Latina expressed gratitude to its partners, volunteers, and supporters, whose contributions are vital for fostering education, employment, and leadership within the community. The evening highlighted the importance of unity and ongoing support in building a resilient future for Latino immigrants. The Casa Latina Team looks forward to continuing its mission with the help of the community.

Rootstock Northwest is hosting its first fundraiser on Black Friday at Local Yokels, inviting the community to celebrate local food chains. Attendees will have the opportunity to meet the Rootstock Northwest team, enjoy cookies, and participate in ornament-making activities for children. This event aims to foster community engagement and support for local food initiatives.

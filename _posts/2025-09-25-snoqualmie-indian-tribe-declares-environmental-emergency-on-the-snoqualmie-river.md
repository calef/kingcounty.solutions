The Snoqualmie Indian Tribe has declared an environmental emergency on the Snoqualmie River. The Tribal Council determined that the river's conditions have worsened significantly, with record low flow levels, unmet minimum instream flows for many years, and water usage exceeding the natural supply. The ecosystem's health is deteriorating due to persistent drought and low snowpack. The tribe urges partners to support their efforts to protect and restore the river.

Forgetting a password can be stressful and time-consuming. To recover forgotten passwords, consider these tips:

Look in your web browser, as many browsers like Google Chrome, Microsoft Edge, Safari, and Firefox store passwords. Access saved passwords through the browser’s settings, usually found in the top right corner. Look for a “password” tab to find saved credentials for various sites.

Having a backup email address is also beneficial. This can be your own or a trusted friend or family member’s email. A secure backup email can receive password reset links if you forget your password.

For recovery instructions specific to Apple, Microsoft, and Gmail accounts, follow the step-by-step guidance provided through QR codes included in the article. To use a QR code, open your phone's camera, point it at the QR code, and click on the link that appears.

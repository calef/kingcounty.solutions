original_markdown_body: |-
  When Kelly arrived at&nbsp;Mary’s Place&nbsp;with her two young sons, Cole and Jayce, they had nothing but the clothes on their backs. Like many families fleeing&nbsp;domestic violence, Kelly had to leave everything behind—family photos, important documents, and her children’s favorite stuffed animals.

  After a few nights in a hotel, Kelly’s money was gone. With no place left to go, she began to prepare for the unthinkable—sleeping in her car with her boys. That’s when a kind stranger told her about&nbsp;Mary’s Place, where she could find&nbsp;emergency shelter and housing resources for families experiencing homelessness.

  From the moment Kelly and her children walked through our doors, they were welcomed with warm meals, cozy beds, and something even more powerful —&nbsp; **hope**.

  Within days, our team helped Kelly take the first steps toward&nbsp;healing and housing stability. Together, they created a plan to remove her name from the unsafe lease she had fled, connected her with&nbsp;employment workshops, and provided&nbsp;counseling resources&nbsp;to help her and her children heal from trauma.

  Meanwhile, Cole and Jayce found safety, comfort, and fun in&nbsp;Kids Club, where they could play, learn, and just be kids again!

  Today, Kelly has a new job as a receptionist for a dental office and is on her way to&nbsp;safe, stable housing&nbsp;— a fresh start for her family after months of fear and uncertainty.

  Stories like Kelly’s are a powerful reminder that&nbsp;no parent should have to choose between safety and shelter. Because of the generosity of our community, families like hers find warmth, stability, and hope at Mary’s Place every single day.

  Your support makes all the difference. When you give to&nbsp;Mary’s Place, you help families facing homelessness find safety, resources, and the chance to rebuild.

Kelly arrived at Mary’s Place with her two sons, Cole and Jayce, after fleeing domestic violence, leaving behind their belongings. After exhausting her funds for a hotel stay, she faced the prospect of sleeping in her car until a stranger informed her about Mary’s Place, which offers emergency shelter and resources for homeless families. Upon arrival, they received warm meals and a sense of hope. The staff quickly assisted Kelly in creating a plan to escape her unsafe situation, including removing her name from a dangerous lease, connecting her with job workshops, and providing counseling for trauma recovery. Meanwhile, Cole and Jayce enjoyed safety and playtime in the Kids Club. Today, Kelly has secured a job as a receptionist at a dental office and is on the path to stable housing, marking a fresh start for her family after a period of fear and instability. Kelly's story highlights the importance of community support in helping families like hers find safety, stability, and hope through Mary’s Place.

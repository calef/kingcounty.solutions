Bridgette, a dedicated Marine Corps spouse and employee of Operation Homefront, expresses her passion for supporting military families through various initiatives. She shares her personal experiences of military life, emphasizing the challenges and joys she encountered while her husband was deployed. After 17 years, her military career transitioned into life as a veteran spouse, and she takes pride in her role at an organization that values all stages of military life.

Bridgette encourages military families to engage with Operation Homefront, which offers support and resources as a gesture of gratitude for their service. She emphasizes the importance of community, suggesting that attending events helps military spouses find comfort and connections. She also highlights the significance of contributions from supporters, whether through volunteering or donations, which help maintain the resilience of military families. Bridgette notes that these acts of kindness can have a profound impact, such as providing essential resources during events like Star-Spangled Babies® Showers and Holiday Meals for Military®. Ultimately, she stresses the importance of appreciation in fostering a supportive environment for military families facing the challenges of deployment and transitions.

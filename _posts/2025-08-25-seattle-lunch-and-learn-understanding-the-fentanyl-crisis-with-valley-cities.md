Valley Cities Behavioral Health Care is hosting a Lunch & Learn event on September 24 at the World Trade Center Seattle, focusing on the fentanyl crisis affecting King County. The event aims to provide insights from clinical leaders about the realities of treatment and recovery. Presenters from Recovery Place Seattle and Recovery Place Kent will share their experiences, highlighting both the challenges and potential for community support in addressing this crisis. The session encourages open dialogue, with time allocated for audience questions, and is limited to 15 attendees. Tickets are free and available on a first-come, first-served basis.

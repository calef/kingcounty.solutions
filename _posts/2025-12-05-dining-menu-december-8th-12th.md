The dining menu at Ballard Senior Center for December 8th to December 12th features a variety of meals designed to cater to seniors. On December 8th, the menu includes chicken salad, coleslaw, and fruit. The following day, participants can enjoy a beef stew served with biscuits and a dessert of pumpkin pie. December 10th features a baked fish option accompanied by rice pilaf and broccoli. On December 11th, the menu offers a turkey sandwich with a side of potato salad and a slice of cake. Finally, on December 12th, a vegetarian chili is served with cornbread and a fruit cup. The meals are aimed at providing nutritious options while promoting social interaction among seniors at the center.

The City of Normandy Park is hiring a part-time assistant preschool teacher. The candidate should have a background in early childhood education and experience working with children aged 2 1/2 - 5 years in a preschool environment. The role involves creating lesson plans to foster social, emotional, and cognitive development. Requirements include a high school diploma or G.E.D, the ability to obtain a First Aid/CPR Card before employment, and a valid Washington State driver's license. The position requires 20 hours per week, with potential for extra hours. Applications are reviewed on a rolling basis and can be submitted to Erin Smith.

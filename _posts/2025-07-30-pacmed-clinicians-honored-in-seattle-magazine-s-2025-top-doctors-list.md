PacMed proudly announces that 44 of its clinicians have been recognized in Seattle Magazine's 2025 Top Doctors list. This prestigious acknowledgment highlights physicians across various specialties who exemplify exceptional care, compassion, and clinical expertise. The Top Doctors list is compiled annually in collaboration with Castle Connolly, a respected healthcare research organization. The selection process involves a rigorous peer-driven nomination, where doctors recommend their trusted colleagues based on merit and professional respect. This year's honorees represent a diverse range of specialties, including cardiology, dermatology, family medicine, and more. Notable PacMed clinicians include Dr. Keiko Aikawa and Dr. Philip Massey in cardiology, Dr. Susan Leu in dermatology, and Dr. Shilpa Desai in obstetrics and gynecology, among others. The recognition is a testament to the high standards of care and dedication demonstrated by the PacMed team, as affirmed by their peers in the medical community.

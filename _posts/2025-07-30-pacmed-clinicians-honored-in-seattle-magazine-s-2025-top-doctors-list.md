  <p>PacMed is proud to share that <strong>44 outstanding clinicians</strong> have been named to <em><strong>Seattle Magazine’s</strong></em><strong> 2025 Top Doctors list</strong>! These physicians represent a wide range of specialties and exemplify the highest standards of care, compassion, and clinical excellence.</p>
  <p><strong>About the Top Doctors List</strong></p>
  <p>Each year, <em>Seattle Magazine</em> partners with <strong>Castle Connolly</strong>, a nationally respected healthcare research company, to identify the region’s most trusted physicians. The selection process is rigorous and peer-driven:</p>
  <ul>
  <li><strong>Doctors nominate other doctors</strong> they would trust with the care of their own family and friends.</li>
  <li><strong>Castle Connolly’s physician-led research team</strong> reviews each nominee’s credentials, board certifications, hospital affiliations, and professional achievements.</li>
  <li><strong>No one can pay to be included</strong>—this recognition is earned through merit and peer respect.</li>
  </ul>
  <p>This year’s list reflects the voices of thousands of doctors across the region and highlights those who go above and beyond in their fields.</p>

PacMed proudly announces that 44 of its clinicians have been recognized in Seattle Magazine's 2025 Top Doctors list. This prestigious acknowledgment highlights physicians across various specialties who exemplify exceptional care, compassion, and clinical expertise. The Top Doctors list is compiled annually in collaboration with Castle Connolly, a respected healthcare research organization. The selection process involves a rigorous peer-driven nomination, where doctors recommend their trusted colleagues based on merit and professional respect. This year's honorees represent a diverse range of specialties, including cardiology, dermatology, family medicine, and more. Notable PacMed clinicians include Dr. Keiko Aikawa and Dr. Philip Massey in cardiology, Dr. Susan Leu in dermatology, and Dr. Shilpa Desai in obstetrics and gynecology, among others. The recognition is a testament to the high standards of care and dedication demonstrated by the PacMed team, as affirmed by their peers in the medical community.

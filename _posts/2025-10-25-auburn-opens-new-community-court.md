Auburn Parks and Recreation oversees nearly 1,000 acres of parkland and more than 20 miles of trails. The Auburn Farmers Market operates seasonally from June to September at Les Gove Park, providing fresh produce and local goods. The Auburn Community and Event Center offers a variety of activities for children, alongside a comprehensive Community Fitness Program that includes numerous classes, open gym access, and a well-equipped weight room. Community members interested in volunteering for the newly established Community Court in Auburn, set to open on May 27, 2021, can reach out to Callista Welbaum, the therapeutic courts manager for King County District Court.

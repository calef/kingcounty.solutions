Alieu Samateh joins Inatai as an IT Service Desk Associate, bringing a background in IT support and a passion for community engagement. He describes himself as authentic, approachable, and witty. Initially coming to Inatai as an IT contractor, Alieu built relationships and connected with the foundation’s mission, which motivated him to apply for a permanent role. He values the culture at Inatai that fosters a sense of belonging and community. Alieu is involved in the Gambian community in Seattle and the Lynnwood Muslim community, where he participates in cultural events and local activities. He finds hope in the support and togetherness within his community, which aligns with Inatai’s long-term vision. Alieu resonates most with the value of racial justice and appreciates Washington's diverse climates and landscapes, particularly Alki Beach for its scenic views and recreational opportunities. He enjoys staying active, socializing with friends, and exploring new foods outside of work. Recently, he read about AI's impact on IT tools, expressing enthusiasm for how technology can enhance efficiency and user experience.

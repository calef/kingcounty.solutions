The National Veterans Golden Age Games has launched an online store to help promote the event and support participants. The store offers a variety of merchandise, including T-shirts, hats, and other apparel, showcasing the Games' logo and branding. Proceeds from the sales will go towards enhancing the experience for Veteran participants, particularly in the areas of sports and wellness. The games, which are the largest multi-sport event for older Veterans, feature various competitions aimed at promoting health and fitness among participants aged 55 and older. The online store aims to foster community support and engagement, allowing individuals to contribute to the well-being of Veterans through their purchases. This initiative reflects the commitment to celebrating the accomplishments and resilience of older Veterans while encouraging active lifestyles.

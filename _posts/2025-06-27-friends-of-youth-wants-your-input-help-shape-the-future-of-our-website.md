Friends of Youth is seeking community input to enhance its website. The organization emphasizes the importance of user experience, inviting feedback from anyone who has visited the site, whether occasionally or regularly. Friends of Youth is interested in hearing about aspects that work well, desired features, or areas that could be improved for easier use. This feedback will guide updates to create a website that better meets the needs of the community and aligns with what is most significant to its users. Community members are encouraged to share their thoughts in the comments section, contributing to the journey of improving the website.

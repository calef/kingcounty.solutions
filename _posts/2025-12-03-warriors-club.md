  For more than a century, DAV has stood strong for America’s veterans and their families. T **hat strength comes from people like you—veterans and supporters who believe in improving the lives of those who served.** That legacy of service would not have been possible without the steadfast support of DAV members and their families. If it’s been said it once, it’s been said it a thousand times: As a member, YOU are the lifeblood of DAV.

  One of the exclusive benefits of being a fully paid DAV life member is eligibility to join our [DAV Warriors Club](https://help.dav.org/page/53020/donate/1?locale=en-US)—a way for fully paid life members to directly fuel the programs and services veterans depend on.

  With a monthly commitment of $21 or more ($252 annually), you can join the DAV Warriors Club, helping to strengthen the DAV Life Membership Fund and the legacy of the organization.

  Right now, thousands of DAV members are donating $21 or more each month, helping ensure the fund remains strong and capable of supporting local programs and services for veterans and their families well into the future. These funds support local initiatives and the national programs DAV is able to offer veterans, their families and survivors at no cost to them.

  As I’m sure many of you are wondering, yes, there are unique benefits for every fully paid DAV life member who joins the DAV Warriors Club.

  You will be paired with a dedicated DAV Warriors Club advisor who is ready to assist with any questions or concerns you may have. In addition, those who join the Warriors Club will be recognized on our Wall of Honor at DAV National Headquarters.

  You will receive ongoing updates on the impact of your support, a beautiful DAV calendar and newsletters featuring stories of the lives you help transform.

  On top of all that, you will receive special invitations to our biannual stakeholder calls where you can hear directly from DAV leadership and fellow veterans.

  The DAV Warriors Club is another way for you to express your support for our organization and to leave a lasting impact and legacy for DAV and your fellow veterans.

  The significance of DAV’s local programs and services cannot be overstated. This is just another chance for you to have a say in our future as we continue our second century of serving veterans and their families.

  Learn more by sending an email to [warriorsclub@dav.org](mailto:warriorsclub@dav.org)

The Warriors Club, initiated by the Disabled American Veterans (DAV), aims to provide veterans with a supportive community and opportunities for camaraderie. Members can engage in various activities, including social events, volunteer opportunities, and advocacy efforts that promote veteran issues. The club emphasizes the importance of connection, allowing veterans to share their experiences and challenges while fostering a sense of belonging. 

Additionally, the Warriors Club offers resources for mental health support and access to benefits that veterans may need. By focusing on inclusivity and empowerment, the club seeks to enhance the quality of life for veterans and their families, ensuring they receive the recognition and support they deserve. The initiative reflects DAV’s commitment to serving those who served, creating a platform for veterans to unite and thrive together.

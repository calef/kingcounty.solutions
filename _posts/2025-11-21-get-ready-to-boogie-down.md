Get ready to boogie down at the far-out 60’s Dance Party at The Center for Active Living! Join the fun on Thursday, December 1, from 7 to 9 p.m. and enjoy a groovy night filled with live DJ music and dancing. The event is open to the community for those aged 21 and over, making it a perfect opportunity to bring friends and relive the 60s vibe. Attendees can purchase beer, wine, non-alcoholic drinks, and snacks. Tickets are available for $10 at the door, so start searching for your favorite 60’s attire, including tie-dye outfits. Don’t miss this chance to have a blast and celebrate in style!

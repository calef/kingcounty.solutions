WDC is launching a new virtual workshop series designed for small business owners, aimed at providing essential resources for sustainable growth and resilience. These free workshops will cover various topics, equipping participants with valuable networks, tools, and strategies to empower their businesses. The initiative focuses on fostering a supportive environment where entrepreneurs can gain insights and practical skills necessary for navigating the challenges of running a small business.

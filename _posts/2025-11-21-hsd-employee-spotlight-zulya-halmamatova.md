Zulhumor (Zulya) Halmamatova is a Senior Grants & Contracts Specialist in the Homelessness division of the Seattle Human Services Department. Since joining in September 2024, she has focused on ensuring smooth funding for prevention agencies to help individuals maintain their housing. Zulya highlights the importance of teamwork and the growth of her team, noting the development of trust and effective communication. She values the support from her colleagues and the meaningful impact of her work, where each task contributes to keeping people housed and avoiding crises. Zulya emphasizes her commitment to racial equity, drawing from her immigrant experience to advocate for clearer processes and support for marginalized communities. She believes that equity is reflected in communication, fairness, and transparency. Her motivation stems from the collective goal of providing support and the dedication of her team, encouraging fellow employees to seek support and remain connected to their mission.

Metro service updates for November 14-16, 2025, report several construction-related impacts on bus routes. Stop #85541 at Federal Way Downtown Station will be closed from November 14 through 17. Route 4 will be rerouted off 5th Avenue N from November 15 at 7 a.m. to November 16 at 5 p.m. Other route adjustments include the closure of Route 7's stop at Rainier Avenue S & S Othello Street from 8 a.m. to 5 p.m. on November 15, and closures for Route 14 on S Jackson Street from November 15 at 7 a.m. to November 16 at 8 p.m. Additionally, Route 49 will be rerouted from November 14 at 11 p.m. to November 17 at 5 a.m., and Route 62 will adjust service between November 15 and 16.

Two significant events will also affect service: the Husky football game on November 15 from 3 p.m. to 8:30 p.m. and the Kirkland Turkey Trot on November 16 from 8:30 a.m. to 11:30 a.m. Riders are encouraged to use Metro and Link light rail for accessing local sports events and to sign up for alerts about service changes.

The Town Council of Yarrow Point will hold a special meeting and public hearing for the final 2026 budget on November 12, 2025, at 4:00 PM at Town Hall. The meeting will also cover a councilmember vacancy and a contract for accounting services. The council has moved to hybrid meetings, allowing for in-person and virtual attendance. Individuals wishing to speak need to register their request before 2:00 PM on the day of the meeting. The meeting can be joined via Zoom or by phone. Public attendance is encouraged.

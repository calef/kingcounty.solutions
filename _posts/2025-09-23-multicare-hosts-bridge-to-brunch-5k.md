MultiCare will host its eighth annual Bridge to Brunch 5K run/walk on Saturday, October 11, at 8:30 a.m. in Spokane, Washington. The event aims to raise funds for the MultiCare Academy for Students in Healthcare (M.A.S.H.) Camp, a free program for high school students to explore healthcare careers. Registration fees are $40 for adults and $10 for youth ages 5-17, while children under 4 can participate for free. Participants will enjoy a waffle breakfast, brunch beverages, a beanie hat, and prizes for race winners. Attendees are encouraged to share their experiences on social media using the hashtag #BridgeToBrunch25. MultiCare Health System is a not-for-profit organization that operates 13 hospitals and employs over 28,000 team members, providing a broad range of healthcare services throughout Washington state.

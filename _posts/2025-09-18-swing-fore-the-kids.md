Join the inaugural "Swing Fore the Kids" Golf Outing benefiting Boyer Children's Clinic from June 18-20, 2026, at the Pronghorn Golf Club in Bend, Oregon. This exclusive three-day event includes four rounds of championship golf on the Tom Fazio Championship Course and the Jack Nicklaus Signature Course, offering a premium golf experience alongside luxury accommodations. Participants can enjoy a weekend of camaraderie and community while supporting Boyer's mission to aid children and families in need.

Event highlights include a cocktail reception, breakfast on the range, and an awards lunch, with airport transportation provided. The package price is $2,950 for two golfers, which includes two nights at Juniper Lodge and a $200 resort credit for services. An additional room with a resort credit can be purchased for $750. Grand Marshals Kraig McCoy and John Stockamp will lead the festivities, making this event a unique opportunity to connect with fellow supporters while enjoying a beautiful setting and a meaningful cause. For inquiries, contact Boyer’s Events Manager, Kimberly McDonald.

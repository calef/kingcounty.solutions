The City of Tukwila has released a Video Voters' Guide for the upcoming general election on Nov. 4, 2025. The guide, aimed at helping Tukwila residents familiarize themselves with the candidates for the council positions, features a two-minute statement by each candidate. Residents can register to vote until eight days before the election online, by mail, or in person through Election Day at the King County Elections headquarters. The registration forms, available in multiple languages, must be received, not postmarked, by the eight-day deadline.

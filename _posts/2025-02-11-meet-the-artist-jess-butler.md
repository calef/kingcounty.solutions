Jess Butler is a multidisciplinary artist based in South Seattle, known for her oil painting and rug tufting, which she practices in her SEEDArts studio. In addition to her art, she runs a budding brand called “by jess,” focusing on high-end maternity and loungewear apparel, crafted in her home sewing studio. Butler earned her BFA in painting, drawing, and printmaking from Western Washington University, where she began creating large-scale seascapes. She also studied fashion design through the Apparel Design & Development program at Seattle Central College. Her artistic work interweaves various mediums, exploring themes of vulnerability and sensation in relation to transitory environments, emphasizing texture, color, and scale. Butler expresses a particular fondness for soft materials and sneakers, reflecting her personal style and creative influences.

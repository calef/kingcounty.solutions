Veterans seeking employment can explore various job opportunities highlighted for the week of Nov. 24, 2025. Key positions include roles in logistics, healthcare, and information technology across multiple organizations. Companies such as Amazon, CVS Health, and Northrop Grumman are actively hiring veterans, offering positions that leverage military experience and skills. Notable job listings feature logistics managers, registered nurses, and cybersecurity analysts, with competitive salaries and benefits. 

Additionally, resources are available for veterans to assist with job applications and interview preparation. Networking events and online job fairs are also scheduled, providing veterans with direct access to employers committed to hiring former service members. This initiative aims to bridge the gap between military service and civilian employment, ensuring veterans can successfully transition into the workforce.

  <p>We are thrilled to announce that 23 of our outstanding PacMed clinicians have been recognized in the <strong>Seattle Met 2025 Top Doctors</strong> list! This annual honor celebrates the region’s most trusted and respected medical professionals, as nominated by their peers and patients.</p>
  <p>The Seattle Met Top Doctors list is compiled through a rigorous peer-nomination process, making it a meaningful reflection of professional respect and trust. It is a moment to celebrate the clinicians who go above and beyond to provide “Simply the Right Care,” a core value of PacMed.</p>
  <p><strong>PacMed Honorees by Specialty</strong></p>
  <p>These exceptional providers represent a wide range of specialties—from primary care to oncology, gastroenterology, and beyond. Their commitment to excellence not only improves the lives of our patients but also strengthens the broader healthcare community in the Puget Sound region.</p>

Twenty-three clinicians from Pacific Medical Centers (PacMed) have been recognized in the Seattle Met 2025 Top Doctors list, honoring the region's most trusted medical professionals based on peer and patient nominations. This annual list reflects the professional respect and trust that these clinicians have earned and highlights their commitment to delivering "Simply the Right Care," a core value of PacMed. The honorees represent various specialties, including allergy and immunology, cardiology, dermatology, family medicine, gastroenterology, internal medicine, neurology, optometry, pain medicine, pediatrics, rheumatology, and urology. Their dedication not only enhances patient care but also contributes to the healthcare community in the Puget Sound region. Notable providers include Dr. John Yuen in Allergy & Immunology, Dr. Keiko Aikawa and Dr. Philip Massey in Cardiology, Dr. Susan Leu and Dr. Melinda Liu in Dermatology, and several others across different specialties, showcasing the diverse expertise available at PacMed.

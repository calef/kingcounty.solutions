The 13th Annual Fill the Bus School Supply Drive is scheduled for Thursday, August 14, 2025, from 3-5 PM at Kent Station. Community members are encouraged to donate school supplies during this time. For additional information, Angela Stave, the Development Director, can be contacted via email at angelas@cisskc.org or by phone at 253-867-5637. Everyone is invited to participate in supporting students as they prepare for the upcoming school year.

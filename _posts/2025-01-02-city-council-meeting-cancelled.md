Join the City of Black Diamond for the 2023 Harvest Festival on Saturday, October 14, from 10 a.m. to 3 p.m. at the Black Diamond Park. This family-friendly event features a variety of activities including a pumpkin patch, hayrides, and live music. Local vendors will offer crafts and food, while a petting zoo will entertain children. The festival aims to celebrate the fall season with community spirit and fun for all ages. Admission is free, and the event promises a day full of entertainment, making it a perfect outing for families and friends.

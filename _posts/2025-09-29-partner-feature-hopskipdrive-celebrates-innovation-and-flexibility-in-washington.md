Washington state has enacted SB 5009, a new law designed to modernize funding formulas for student transportation, effective July 27. This legislation aims to facilitate access to innovative, multimodal transportation solutions for schools across the state. By streamlining these funding mechanisms, the law ensures that every student has reliable access to education. As school districts in Washington begin to explore new transportation options, the emphasis will be on maintaining safety and reliability in student transport. This development is seen as a significant advancement in promoting equity and innovation within the realm of student transportation.

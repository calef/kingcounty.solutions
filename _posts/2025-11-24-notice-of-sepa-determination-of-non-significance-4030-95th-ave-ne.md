A SEPA Determination of Non-Significance has been issued for the property located at 4030 95th Ave NE. This determination indicates that the proposed project is not expected to have a significant adverse impact on the environment. In addition to this notice, the town has announced a series of meetings: a Town Council Special Meeting on November 20, 2025, at 4:00 PM at Town Hall, a Town Park Commission Special Meeting on November 19, 2025, at 7:00 PM at Town Hall, and the cancellation of the Town Planning Commission Regular Meeting originally scheduled for November 18, 2025.

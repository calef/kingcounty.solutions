A physician assistant at the VA Nebraska-Western Iowa Health Care System played a crucial role in saving the life of a veteran experiencing a cardiac event. The incident occurred while the veteran was in the waiting room for an appointment. The physician assistant, who was nearby, quickly recognized the signs of a heart attack and sprang into action. She began performing CPR and called for assistance, while a nurse joined her in managing the situation. Emergency services were contacted, and the veteran was stabilized before being transported to the hospital for further treatment. The swift response and teamwork of the medical staff highlighted the importance of being prepared for emergencies and the vital role healthcare professionals play in saving lives. The veteran is now recovering, thanks to the prompt actions of the physician assistant and her colleagues.

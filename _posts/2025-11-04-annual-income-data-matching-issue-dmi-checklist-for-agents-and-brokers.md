The Annual Income Data Matching Issue (DMI) Checklist for Agents and Brokers provides essential resources related to data matching issues. It includes dynamic list information and is applicable as of October 1, 2025. This checklist serves as a guide for agents and brokers to ensure accurate income data matching when assisting clients with health insurance applications. The resource is categorized under general resources and is available for download, allowing agents and brokers to access the material conveniently. The focus is on facilitating proper income verification processes to avoid discrepancies that may affect clients' eligibility for health coverage.

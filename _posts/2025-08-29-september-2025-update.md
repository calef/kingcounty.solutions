John will be out of the office on September 12, and there will be no tech help or Tech 101 classes that day. No programming will be held on Labor Day, September 1, and no meals will be served. On October 8, a Medicare/Medicaid expert will be available to answer questions. Roundtable Conversations, led by social worker Syl, will take place on September 10 and 24 at 1:00 PM, focusing on topics like favorite movies and personal accomplishments.

John will teach three Tech 101 classes this month: Digital Privacy Basics on September 5, Privacy Settings 101 on September 19, and AI & Privacy Basics on September 26, all at 10:00 AM. King County Metro will provide assistance on September 8 at 1:30 PM for Senior and Disabled Regional Reduced Fare Permits. A Fall Prevention Seminar by Humana will occur on September 15, and a Legal Planning Presentation by the Pro Bono Council will be on September 22 at 1:30 PM. Health screenings by Birds Eye Medical will be offered on September 26 from 10:30 AM to 2:00 PM, with no insurance required.

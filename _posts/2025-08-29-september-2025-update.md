John will be out of the office on Sept. 12, and there will be no tech help or Tech 101 classes that day. No programming will be held on Labor Day, Sept. 1, and no meals will be served. On Oct. 8, a Medicare/Medicaid expert will be available to answer questions. Roundtable Conversations, led by social worker Syl, will take place on Sept. 10 and 24 at 1 p.m., focusing on topics like favorite movies and personal accomplishments.

John will teach three Tech 101 classes this month: Digital Privacy Basics on Sept. 5, Privacy Settings 101 on Sept. 19, and AI & Privacy Basics on Sept. 26, all at 10 a.m. King County Metro will provide assistance on Sept. 8 at 1:30 p.m. for Senior and Disabled Regional Reduced Fare Permits. A Fall Prevention Seminar by Humana will occur on Sept. 15, and a Legal Planning Presentation by the Pro Bono Council will be on Sept. 22 at 1:30 p.m. Health screenings by Birds Eye Medical will be offered on Sept. 26 from 10:30 a.m. to 2 p.m., with no insurance required.

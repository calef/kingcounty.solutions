  <li><strong>Blood Tests:</strong> Blood tests are less common but provide a comprehensive analysis of drug use. A sample of blood is drawn and tested for specific substances, reflecting recent use. This method is expensive and typically reserved for legal circumstances or complex cases of suspected drug use.</li>

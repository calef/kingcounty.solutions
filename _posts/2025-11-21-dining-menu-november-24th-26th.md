The dining menu for Ballard Senior Center from November 24th to 26th features a variety of meals. On Monday, the menu includes a Grilled Roast Beef & Cheddar Sandwich, Veggie Barley Soup, Fruit, and a Cookie. Tuesday is dedicated to a Thanksgiving Lunch, featuring Roast Turkey, Mashed Potatoes with Gravy, Stuffing, Seasonal Veggies, Rolls, Cranberry Sauce, and Pumpkin Pie, with a vegetarian option of Veggie Cottage Pie. On Wednesday, the offerings are a Twice Baked Potato, Veggie Chili, and Fruit. The center will be closed on Thursday and Friday in observance of Thanksgiving, extending warm wishes for a joyful holiday.

The International Special Review District has announced the results of its annual election. The election results indicate the selection of new representatives who will serve on the district’s board. This year's election saw participation from community members who cast their votes to influence decisions affecting the district. The newly elected members are tasked with addressing key issues and initiatives within the district, ensuring that the community's interests are represented. The district aims to enhance engagement and collaboration among residents to foster an inclusive environment that reflects the diverse needs of the community. The election results signify a commitment to transparency and accountability in the district’s governance. The board will hold its first meeting with the new members in January, where they will outline their priorities for the upcoming year. Community members are encouraged to stay informed and involved in the district’s activities as the new board members begin their terms.

Praisealujah offers essential services including food, housing, and recovery programs for those in need. They host a free drivethru food giveaway on the first Sunday of every month from 11 AM to 1 PM at Les Schwab Tire Center in Burien, WA. This initiative, titled "Church Beyond the 4 Walls," aims to support the community through food distribution. In addition to the food giveaway, Praisealujah conducts weekly worship services and weekday morning classes at New Beginnings Christian Fellowship Church, located at 1320 SW 102nd St, Seattle, WA. The church also provides hot meal services to the public every weekday from 12 PM to 12:30 PM, offering nutritious meals prepared daily. Praisealujah encourages community involvement and donations to expand their outreach and assistance programs.

The City Council of Bellevue is inviting applications for a vacancy on the Communities of Color Coordinating Team (CCC). Interested individuals must apply by 5 p.m. on June 23. The CCC initiative focuses on fostering racial equity and community engagement through dialogue among Bellevue's diverse populations. The team aims to build trust, awareness, and provide opportunities for all communities in Bellevue to thrive by enhancing racial literacy and training. The CCC team, which meets monthly, is composed of diverse members dedicated to improving racial equity in the city. Applicants must live, work, or attend school in Bellevue. Applications can be submitted via hand delivery, mail, or email. Additional information about the role can be requested from the DEI community outreach and engagement administrator. The City of Bellevue encourages diversity and welcomes all interested candidates to apply.

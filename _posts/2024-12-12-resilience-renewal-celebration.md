The Sophia Way, an organization dedicated to helping women, recently celebrated its 15-year journey with a Resilience and Renewal event on December 7. The celebration honored the community, staff, and supporters who have contributed to the organization over the years. The Sophia Way is committed to creating lasting change for the women it serves, looking forward to continuing its critical work with hope and determination. The event included a timeline of the organization's journey, a video showcasing 15 years of hope and change, and a collection of favorite images from the event.

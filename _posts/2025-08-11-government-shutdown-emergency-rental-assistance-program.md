  ### To support residents being impacted by the federal government shutdown, Community Roots is temporarily expanding our Emergency Rental Assistance program during the months of November and December 2025.

  We recognize that the government shutdown is impacting many Community Roots residents’ access to critical programs like SNAP, WIC, and LIHEAP, and some may be experiencing major hikes to insurance premiums. This program is designed to help residents who have had a financial setback or unforeseen circumstance (including but not limited to impacts from the government shutdown) disrupt their ability to pay up to **TWO (2) MONTHS of rent.**&nbsp;

  **[Learn more about the expanded Emergency Rental Assistance program.](https://communityrootshousing.org/current-residents/resident-services/emergency-rental-assistance/)**

Community Roots is expanding its Emergency Rental Assistance program temporarily during November and December 2025 to support residents affected by the federal government shutdown. This initiative aims to assist those facing financial setbacks or unforeseen circumstances, which may include disruptions in access to critical programs like SNAP, WIC, and LIHEAP, as well as increases in insurance premiums. Eligible residents can receive help covering up to two months of rent. The program is designed to ensure that 25 Community Roots households can remain housed during these challenging times. Donations are being sought to help achieve this goal.

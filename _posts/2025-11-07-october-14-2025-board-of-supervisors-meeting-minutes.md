The King Conservation District held a Board of Supervisors meeting on October 14, 2025, the details of which are captured in the minutes. The meeting discussed various topics related to community agriculture, education, environmental events, forestry, grants, and regional food system programs. The Board also focused on the Landowner Incentive Program, Native Bareroot Plant Sale, and Riparian Restoration. Additionally, the Board deliberated the possibility of a Farm Conservation position and the commencement of the 2026 Native Plant Store preview. Other topics included volunteer opportunities, member jurisdiction, and upcoming educational events.

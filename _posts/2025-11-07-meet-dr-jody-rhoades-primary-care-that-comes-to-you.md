  ### **Wherever Life Takes You, Care Is Just a Click Away**

  ![](https://www.pacificmedicalcenters.org/wp-content/uploads/2025/11/RHOADES-4-300x300.jpg)

  Whether you are at home, at work, or on the go, [**Dr. Jody Rhoades**](https://www.providence.org/doctors/internal-medicine/wa/bothell/jody-rhoades-1104851039) is here to provide expert care virtually. As a trusted internal medicine provider at **PacMed** , Dr. Rhoades offers **Connected Video Visits** , making high-quality health care more accessible than ever for established patients.

  **If you are an established PacMed patient** and would like to schedule an appointment with Dr. Rhoades, call **1.888.472.2633** or [make an appointment via MyChart.](https://mychartwa.providence.org/mychart/)

  #### A Partnership for Better Health

  Dr. Rhoades believes that great care starts with collaboration. By educating and empowering patients, she helps them take charge of their health. Her approach is built on partnership—working together toward one shared goal: a happy, healthy lifestyle.

  #### Licensed and Ready to Care

  Dr. Rhoades is medically licensed in **Arizona, New Mexico, and Washington** , and provides virtual care for **established patients only.** Her services include:

  - **Acute urgent visits** (appropriate for video appointments)
  - **Chronic condition management**
  - **Hospital/ER follow-up** (transitional care management)
  - Most other conditions within the primary care scope

  #### What She Does Not Offer

  To ensure the best experience, please note that Dr. Rhoades is **unavailable** for:

  - New patient appointments
  - Annual physical exams
  - Controlled substance refills or prescriptions
  - Labor & Industries (L&I) claims
  - FMLA or disability paperwork
  - Motor Vehicle Accident (MVA) claims

Dr. Jody Rhoades offers virtual primary care services for established patients at PacMed, providing accessible health care through Connected Video Visits. She emphasizes a collaborative approach, empowering patients to take charge of their health and work towards a happy, healthy lifestyle. Licensed in Arizona, New Mexico, and Washington, Dr. Rhoades is available for various services, including acute urgent visits, chronic condition management, and hospital follow-ups, all appropriate for video appointments. However, she does not accept new patients, conduct annual physical exams, refill controlled substances, or handle specific claims like Labor & Industries (L&I) or motor vehicle accidents. Established PacMed patients can schedule appointments with Dr. Rhoades by calling 1.888.472.2633 or using MyChart.

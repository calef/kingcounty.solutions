Seattle police are investigating a vehicle collision that occurred on December 2, 2025, involving a pedestrian. The incident took place around 7:00 PM in the Capitol Hill neighborhood. A woman in her 30s was struck by a vehicle while crossing the street. Emergency services responded promptly, and the pedestrian was transported to a local hospital with serious injuries. The driver remained at the scene and is cooperating with the investigation. Detectives are working to determine the circumstances surrounding the collision, including factors such as speed and any possible impairment. Witnesses are being sought to provide additional information. Traffic on the road was diverted while investigators processed the scene. This incident highlights ongoing concerns about pedestrian safety in the area.

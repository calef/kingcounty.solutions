Seattle police are investigating a vehicle collision involving a pedestrian that occurred on December 2, 2025. The incident took place at approximately 7:00 PM near the intersection of 12th Avenue and Pine Street. Witnesses reported seeing a vehicle strike the pedestrian, who was crossing the street at the time. Emergency responders arrived quickly on the scene, providing medical assistance to the injured pedestrian before transporting them to a nearby hospital for treatment. The extent of the pedestrian's injuries has not been disclosed. The driver remained at the scene and is cooperating with the investigation. Authorities are urging anyone with additional information about the incident to come forward. The investigation into the circumstances surrounding the collision is ongoing, and Seattle police are looking into factors that may have contributed to the accident.

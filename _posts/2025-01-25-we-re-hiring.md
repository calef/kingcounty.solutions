Seattle Indian Health Board is hiring for several key positions: Medical Assistant, Dental Assistant, and Environmental Services (EVS) Assistant. 

Medical Assistants will play a crucial role in supporting patients throughout their healthcare journey by providing culturally respectful care, taking vital signs, and explaining procedures. Dental Assistants are needed to ensure patient comfort during dental visits, assist dentists and hygienists, and engage in community outreach and education. EVS Assistants are responsible for maintaining a safe and welcoming environment, performing light repairs, and addressing health and safety concerns.

The organization encourages applications from individuals with or without experience in these roles, highlighting opportunities for growth within the Seattle Indian Health Board. Interested candidates are invited to explore the full job board for additional possibilities and to apply to help shape the future of Native healthcare.

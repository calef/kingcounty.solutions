The KCSARC Youth Advisory Council (YAC) has introduced its 2025 cohort, comprising motivated teens from King County dedicated to preventing sexual violence. Each member shared their motivations for joining and a fun fact about themselves:

- **Anusha**, a senior, aims to educate others about sexual assault and plays guitar and piano.
- **Cara** wants to make an impact in mental health while supporting survivors.
- **Cass** seeks to amplify youth voices and enjoys sewing and learning languages.
- **Everett** aspires to be a professional musician and is involved in community prevention work.
- **Grace** is focused on educating about sexual violence in schools and enjoys traveling.
- **June** is committed to raising awareness and sharing resources for assault survivors.
- **Kenzie**, a freshman, participates in various sports and advocates for those affected by sexual violence.
- **Maya** emphasizes the need for open discussions about recovery resources.
- **Simone** aims to destigmatize sexual violence and empower her peers.

This diverse group is focused on making a difference in their communities through education and advocacy.

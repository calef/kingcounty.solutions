**Fire Commissioner Vacancy in District #37**

King County Fire District No. 37 has a vacancy for Commissioner Position #2. The position will be filled by appointment, with the term lasting until the November 2023 General Election certification. The position will be up for election in November 2025. The KCFD #37 Board of Commissioners, consisting of three members, act as the fire district's board of directors and voting members of the Puget Sound Regional Fire Authority Governance Board. Interested applicants, who must be registered voters residing within the fire district, can submit an application form to the district’s secretary by 5:00 pm on March 6, 2023.

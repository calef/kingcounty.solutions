The National Council on Disability (NCD) is seeking public input on disability training programs to enhance the effectiveness of services provided to individuals with disabilities. This initiative aims to gather insights from various stakeholders, including individuals with disabilities, families, service providers, and educators. The NCD is particularly interested in understanding the current gaps in training and the specific needs of communities to ensure that training is relevant and impactful. Feedback can be submitted through an online form or by attending upcoming public meetings. The NCD emphasizes the importance of comprehensive training that addresses the diverse and evolving needs of people with disabilities. The deadline for input is set for January 15, 2026, as the NCD aims to incorporate this feedback into its recommendations for improving disability training across the country.

On October 11, Day of the Girl emphasizes the power of education in unlocking leadership potential among girls. CAMFED is dedicated to supporting girls and young women, ensuring they not only stay in school but thrive academically and socially. Students like Francisca and Fatou from Ghana share their aspirations of becoming lawyers, highlighting the importance of education in shaping their futures and advocating for marginalized voices. Both actively participate in their school's debate team, enhancing their confidence and leadership skills. After graduation, they will join the CAMFED Association, a network of over 313,000 young women who have received CAMFED support, with many taking on leadership roles in their communities.

Resolution 25-004 has been adopted to fill the vacant Position #2 on the King Conservation District (KCD) Board of Supervisors. This resolution aims to ensure effective governance and support for the district's initiatives. KCD plays a crucial role in assisting local farmers, managing agricultural practices, and promoting environmental stewardship within the community. The Board of Supervisors is responsible for overseeing KCD's activities and making decisions that impact regional conservation efforts. The process to fill this position is open to candidates interested in contributing to local conservation and agricultural management. The resolution reflects KCD's commitment to engaging community members and enhancing its leadership structure to better serve the needs of the region.

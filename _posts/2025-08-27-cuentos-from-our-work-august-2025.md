The event took place in a local park, fostering community joy and togetherness. The organization also hosted a successful King County Adult Dental Program event, providing essential dental care to diverse community members and highlighting the importance of language access. KeyBank supported back-to-school efforts by collecting supplies for K-8 students, ensuring they are well-prepared for the school year.

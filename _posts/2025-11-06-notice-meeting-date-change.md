The Valley Regional Fire Authority (VRFA) has announced a change in the date of its November Board of Governance meeting. Originally set to take place on Veterans Day, Nov. 11, the meeting has been rescheduled to observe the holiday. The new date for the meeting is Wednesday, Nov. 12. The meeting will commence at 5 p.m. and will be held at VRFA Station 31.

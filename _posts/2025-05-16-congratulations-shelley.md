Congratulations to Shelley Puariea, the director of the Federal Way Senior Center, who has received the Community Citizen Award from East Hill Grange No. 786. This award recognizes her over 35 years of service with the Boys & Girls Clubs in Puget Sound and her significant contributions to the Federal Way Senior Center. Shelley manages the Nutritional Meal Program, Community Garden, and Food Bank, collaborating with organizations like Food Lifeline, Safeway, and Costco to benefit the community. She also organizes weekly lunches, bingo, and various senior programs. Her hard work and dedication make a meaningful impact in the lives of many. The award was presented to her on May 8, 2025. Community members have expressed their congratulations and gratitude, highlighting her commitment and the positive influence she has had on those in need.

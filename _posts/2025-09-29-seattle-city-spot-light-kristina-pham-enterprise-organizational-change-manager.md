Kristina Pham serves as the Enterprise Organizational Change Manager at Seattle City Light, where she has worked for four years. Her primary role involves leading a team focused on supporting employees during organizational change initiatives. They develop and implement strategies to ensure smooth transitions, particularly during technology implementations and process changes. Pham holds a degree in business management with a minor in information technology from George Mason University and hails from Fairfax, Virginia. 

She lives in Auburn with her husband, Andy, and their two children, Lawson and Kaylani. Pham expresses a deep passion for the mission of Seattle City Light, emphasizing the importance of their work in fostering positive community impact. She highlights the essential role of communication tools, such as her laptop and phone, in managing change effectively. 

Throughout her career, Pham has navigated challenges, including leading a large team in a complex project to select a new grant management system for the city. Her approach involved engaging technical experts and maintaining open communication to successfully guide her team in meeting project goals within a tight timeline.

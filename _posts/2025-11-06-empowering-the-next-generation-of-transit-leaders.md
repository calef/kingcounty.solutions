On October 25, Sound Transit hosted a Youth Conference for 90 young people aged 15–22, focusing on the impact of transit on the region and encouraging youth involvement in its future. Participants engaged in sessions about light rail planning, system safety, and career opportunities in transit. The event emphasized building connections among students, community partners, and transit staff, highlighting values of sustainability and equity. Sound Transit aims to empower the next generation to shape mobility in the Puget Sound region while fostering a talent pipeline for future careers in the transit industry.

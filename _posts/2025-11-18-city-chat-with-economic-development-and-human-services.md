Join the "City Chat" on Nov. 20 from 5:30 to 6:30 p.m. at the Burien Library Multipurpose Room. Meet the Economic Development and Human Services staff and learn about their community support work. Free light refreshments will be provided. Attendees are encouraged to bring nonperishable food and winter coat donations to help support local neighbors.

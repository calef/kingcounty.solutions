Cheri reflects on her 28 years with Angel Flight West, highlighting the evolution of the organization from a simple mission tracking system to a sophisticated operation that now manages nearly 5,000 missions annually. Despite the challenges posed by COVID-19, which temporarily halted growth, the team adapted by providing essential supplies and shifting to remote work. Cheri emphasizes that her experiences extend beyond numbers and technology, focusing instead on the relationships she has built with volunteers, passengers, and staff. She shares memorable moments, including the dedication of pilots who have gone above and beyond to support families in need and the profound impact of passengers she has met. Cheri recalls both joyful and heartbreaking memories, such as the stories of passengers who have faced significant challenges. Her reflections convey deep gratitude for the friendships and lessons learned throughout her tenure. Cheri expresses hope to continue her involvement with Angel Flight West, cherishing the connections that have enriched her life over nearly three decades.

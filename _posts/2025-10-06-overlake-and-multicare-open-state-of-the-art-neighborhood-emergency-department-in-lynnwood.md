  LYNNWOOD, Wash. — Overlake Medical Center & Clinics and MultiCare Health System are proud to announce the opening of their new, 11,000-square-foot neighborhood emergency department in Lynnwood. Located at 3011 196th St SW, this state-of-the-art facility brings high-quality, 24/7 emergency care closer to home for residents of the north Puget Sound region.

  [Overlake MultiCare Emergency – Lynnwood](https://multicareer.org/locations/lynnwood/) comes at a time of rapid regional growth, bringing much-needed access to emergency services in the region.

  The new standalone emergency department features 12 exam rooms, as well as on-site radiology services, a point-of-care testing lab, and pharmacy services – allowing for treatment for a wide range of emergencies.

  “Since its inception, Overlake has been a fixture of compassionate, leading-edge care on the Eastside, and our dedicated team of health care professionals is ready to bring that same level of service to communities in the North Sound,” says Jon Duarte, Overlake president & CEO and CEO of MultiCare’s North Sound Region. “To our new neighbors in Lynnwood – Overlake MultiCare Emergency’s doors will always be open for you when you need us most.”

  The Lynnwood location is an extension of Overlake’s trusted emergency care in Bellevue that has served the region for more than six decades.

  “MultiCare’s mission is partnering for healing and a healthy future,” says Bill Robertson, CEO of MultiCare. “It’s our privilege to work with Overlake to bring these much-needed emergency services to the Lynnwood community. This is one more example of how like-minded organizations are able to accomplish so much more together than we can apart.”

  Overlake MultiCare Emergency – Lynnwood is the seventh community emergency department in the MultiCare system, adding to a statewide network of emergency, urgent, primary and specialty care facilities.

  Overlake and MultiCare also partnered with [Emerus](https://www.emerus.com/), a national leader in building and operating small-format hospitals and off-campus emergency departments. Emerus is renowned for its commitment to transforming emergency services by designing care around the patient and family allowing them an exceptional patient experience in a cost-efficient manner.

Overlake Medical Center & Clinics and MultiCare Health System have opened a new 11,000-square-foot neighborhood emergency department in Lynnwood, Washington. Situated at 3011 196th St SW, this facility provides 24/7 emergency care to residents in the north Puget Sound region, addressing the growing demand for emergency services. The standalone department features 12 exam rooms, on-site radiology, a point-of-care testing lab, and pharmacy services, enabling comprehensive treatment for various emergencies. 

Jon Duarte, CEO of Overlake and MultiCare's North Sound Region, emphasized the commitment to compassionate care, stating the facility is an extension of Overlake’s longstanding service in Bellevue. Bill Robertson, CEO of MultiCare, noted the partnership's goal of enhancing community health services. This emergency department marks the seventh in MultiCare’s network, which includes various health facilities across Washington. Overlake and MultiCare collaborated with Emerus, known for its innovative approach to emergency care, focusing on patient and family-centered services. Both organizations have a strong commitment to quality care, with Overlake recognized for its excellence in health services and significant charitable contributions.

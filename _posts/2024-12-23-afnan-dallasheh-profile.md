Afnan Dallasheh has been an integral part of the MAPS community for the past five years, focusing on empowering youth, women, and advocating for sustainability. She has volunteered at various MAPS events and through the MAPS Girl Scouts. Afnan engages with multiple PTSA organizations and communicates with district leaders to address the needs of Muslim children in public schools. Professionally, she has experience as a Business Relationship Manager in an e-commerce company, where her organizational and project management skills are highly valued as MAPS plans for future growth. Afnan aims to enhance MAPS programs to ensure they are sustainable and equitable, while also expanding outreach and strengthening community ties. She holds a degree in Pharmacy from Jordan and has a diverse professional background, including roles as a Pharmacy Manager, Pharmaceutical Representative, and science teacher at both elementary and secondary levels.

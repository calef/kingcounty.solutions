Mark your calendar for Giving Tuesday on December 2, 2025, a significant day dedicated to supporting nonprofit organizations in the community. The Center for Active Living encourages participation through various ways to contribute, including making financial donations, volunteering, donating gently used items to the Thrift Store, upgrading to a Lifetime Membership, and purchasing gift cards or Center merchandise as holiday gifts. The Center expresses gratitude to all supporters and emphasizes the importance of community engagement on this global day of giving.
